    Blue Lock Chapter 244 Spoilers

    Yoichi Isagi and others ponder over the Blue Lock auction system. It seems like the greater their number of goals, the more value they’ll get to their name. Goals are estimated at a value between 30-50 million in this case, meaning any given game Isagi could be one goal from reaching the top!

    Shifting away from the Ness and Kaiser backstory, we instead saw some intense soccer being played between PXG and FC Barcha. The game began with earnest forward momentum, especially once Rin Itoshi was set up for the team’s first goal! However, this means the gulf between him and Isagi could widen.

    But then a team subbed out Rin for Ryusei Shido, whose infamous shot-taking was complemented by the same player who set Rin up for his goal. This phantom playmaker is revealed to be Charles Chevalier, and we’ll surely see more of him as PXG nets an impressive 2-0 lead already. We can’t wait for the next installment in Blue Lock Chapter 245!
