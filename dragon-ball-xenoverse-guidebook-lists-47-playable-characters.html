    28 Jun 2024, 00:00

    A guidebook for Dragon Ball Xenoverse has now been released in Japan, and the book shows 47 playable characters.

    This is according to a report from Saiyan Island. The website says there is a chance that there might be more characters added to the game. Not to mention the guidebook doesn’t show the characters that will be added in as DLC in the future.

    Saiyan Island also did a little more research and managed to find 57 characters for Dragon Ball Xenoverse so far. This includes the DLC characters plus others they’ve found via screenshots and trailers.

    The Dragon Ball Xenoverse guidebook also reveals there are 50 parallel quests, 200 different skills and over 400 different pieces of clothing you can use for your created characters.
