    The Brimstone Sands expansion has brought tons of new content to New World. On top of the new story expansion and new weapon type, there has been the addition of tons of new end-game content for players to get through. One such addition is the new Ancient Glyphs that are scattered around the new area of New World. These glyphs are hidden away in quite sneaky places and players are having a hard time finding them all. Let’s go over the location of all the Ancient Glyphs in New World.

    Every Ancient Glyph Location in New World

    Before you go off to try and find all of the Ancient Glyphs, you will want to complete the new series of main story quests as some of the glyphs are in areas that you can’t access until after specific main story quests. This also makes accessing the rewards for finding the Ancient Glyphs much easier and more worth it.

    Each Ancient Glyph you find will allow you to access the corresponding elite chest which can contain valuable loot to help you perfect your greatsword build. There are also some end-game activities that can’t be started unless you have found all 26 Ancient Glyphs scattered throughout the world.

    While it won’t be an easy task to find them all, the rewards are more than worth the effort you will have to put in to hunt them down. Here is a full list of all the Ancient Glyphs you need to find as well as where to find them at.
