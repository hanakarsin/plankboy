    Are you wondering if you can watch the Super Bowl on FuboTV? FuboTV is a great streaming platform because it allows watching live sports, entertainment, and news channels with a great DVR system, so you never miss anything important.

    Recommended Videos

    In addition, Super Bowl LVIII will feature the Kansas City Chiefs against the San Francisco 49ers in what should be one of the best Super Bowls in recent history. So then, can you watch the Super Bowl and its funny commercials on FuboTV, or will you need to invest in another streaming service? Here is everything you need to know about watching the Super Bowl on FuboTV.

    Will the Super Bowl Stream on FuboTV?

    Yes, you can watch the Super Bowl on FuboTV. You will be set if any of the packages include CBS. The critical part of this package is access to CBS, who owns the right to the Super Bowl broadcast in 2024. While FuboTV is on the expensive side compared to other streaming services, they offer a free trial, so if you ever wanted to try out FuboTV, now would be the time.

    Where Can I Watch the Super Bowl?

    Suppose you don’t want to subscribe to another streaming service like FuboTV or already have a Live TV package with another subscriber, then you can watch the Super Bowl as long as it includes CBS in its live channel package line-up. Here are the best examples of popular streaming platforms with CBS included in their packages:

    • Hulu + Live TV
    • Paramount +
    • YouTube TV
    • NFL Plus
    • DirecTV Stream

    Most streaming services offer free trials and discounts, so now is the best time to subscribe if you need to change which streaming service provider you use since the NFL season is over. Otherwise, setting up a watch party with family or friends who have access to CBS will be your next best option for watching the Super Bowl. Who doesn’t love an excuse for an impromptu Super Bowl party?

    Super Bowl LVIII is Sunday, February 11, 2024, and will air on CBS at 6:30 PM Eastern.
