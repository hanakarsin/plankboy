    Are you wondering how to unlock the Dark Arts Battle Arena in Hogwarts Legacy? The Dark Arts Battle Arena is an environment where all witches and wizards can safely practice the Dark Arts in a controlled environment. You will learn how to use spells against abilities like Charm Shields when facing off against waves of enemies. However, if you start playing Hogwarts Legacy but find that you cannot access the Dark Arts Battle Arena, it is most likely for a specific reason. What is that reason exactly? Here is everything you need to know about unlocking the Dark Arts Battle Arena in Hogwarts Legacy.

    Recommended Videos

    Why Can’t I Access the Dark Arts Battle Arena in Hogwarts Legacy?

    The Dark Arts Battle Arena is not included in the base game and is exclusive to the Deluxe and Collector’s editions. However, if you purchased the Standard Edition of Hogwarts Legacy, you can unlock the Dark Arts Battle Arena separately by purchasing the Dark Arts Battle Pack in-game. Unfortunately, no official details have been provided on how much the in-game pack will be, but we will update this guide once we know more.

    Note: There are currently no other items for purchase in the game.

    What is the Dark Arts Battle Arena in Hogwarts Legacy?

    The Dark Arts Battle Arena is described in-game as a place to practice the Dark Aarts against waves of enemies using Unforgivable Curses, Avada Kedavra, Imperio, Crucio, Blasting Curse, and Confringo. Basically, any of the spells would be frowned upon being used by the wizarding world. You will also be able to complete specific challenges, which allow you to earn additional rewards upon completion.
