    Mortal Kombat X has been out over a month and the release of the second DLC character is on the horizon, following the release of the latest patch update over the weekend. We have known about a number of costumes that would be coming as part of the gameâ€™s Kombat Pack, but now we have learned about a very strange upcoming pack that it would have been hard to have seen coming.

    Ed Boon uses his Twitter account almost more than any other game developer, most of the time responding to questions by fans, but he has also used it often to make new reveals, which is exactly what he just did. In the following tweet, Boon revealed a brand new costume pack and a picture showing off the costumes.

    • Mortal Kombat X has a number of additional costumes coming, including this Brazil Costume Pack!

    This is definitely a kind of surprising pack to have, with it looking to have a soccer theme in part, especially with costumes by Liu Kang and Johnny Cage. Kung Lao is the third character to get a costume in the Brazil Pack, though it is harder to tell just what that costume consists of in this picture.

    The Mortal Kombat X Brazil costume pack has no set release date, but likely will be coming sometime in the next few months alongside one of the DLC characters. Stay tuned for more details as soon as we hear them.
