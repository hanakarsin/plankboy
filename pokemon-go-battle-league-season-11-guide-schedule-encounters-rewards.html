    11 Jan 2024, 00:00

    After Season of Alola will be Season of GO and with it comes season 11 of the Pokemon GO Battle League. The new season of Pokemon GO’s Battle League is scheduled to start in early June. The Battle League will be the first feature available for fans of the game. Over the course of the next three months, players will get to enjoy new rewards with each new rank in the league.

    Pokemon GO Battle League Season 11 Schedule

    Here is the schedule for Season 11 of the Pokemon GO Battle League:

    • June 1 – June 8: Great League, Great League Remix
    • June 8 – 15: Ultra League, Catch Cup – GO Fest Edition
    • June 15 – June 22: Master League, Fossil Cup
    • June 22 – June 29: Great League, Retro Cup
    • June 29 – July 6: Ultra League, Kanto Cup
    • July 6 – July 13: Great League, Ultra League, Master League
    • July 13 – July 20: Great League, Flying Cup
    • July 20 – July 27: Ultra League, Little Cup Remix
    • July 27 – August 3: Master League, (There are no details on the second league introduced for this week)
    • August 3 – August 10: Great League, Element Cup
    • August 10 – August 17: Ultra League, Summer Cup
    • August 17 – August 24: Great League, Fighting Cup
    • August 24- September 1: Great League, Ultra League, Master League

    As we can see, there is something for any fan of the game no matter if you’re new or a veteran to the series. Down below, we will show the Battle League rewards for this season.

    Pokemon GO Battle League Season 11 Rewards

    Here are the guaranteed rank-up encounters you will face as you rank up this season. On top of these encounters, players will also earn four avatar item pieces with each rank providing another layer to the Bea-Style outfit.

    Guaranteed rank-up encounters:

    • Rank 1 – Machamp
    • Rank 6 – Mienfoo
    • Ace Rank – Axew
    • Veteran Rank – Noibat
    • Legend Rank – Pikachu Libre

    Standard reward Encounters:

    • Rank 1 – Poliwag, Wingull, Meditite, Litwick, Skwovet, Skarmory
    • Rank 6 – Mienfoo
    • Rank 11 – Lickitung, Hitmontop, Karrablast, Frillish, Shelmet
    • Rank 16 – Absol, Pancham, Falinks
    • Rank 20 – Five-star Raid Boss
    • Ace Rank – Axew
    • Veteran Rank – Noivern

    Pokemon GO is available now on mobile devices.
