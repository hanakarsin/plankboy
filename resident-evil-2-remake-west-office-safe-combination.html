    The RPD in Resident Evil 2 is full of safes. These treasure troves hold useful items, often different ones depending on which character you choose. The safe in the West Office is not like this though, holding one of the more valuable items in the whole game for both Leon and Claire. To get at it though, you need to know the combination. Here’s the West Office safe combination in Resident Evil 2 Remake.

    To open the West Office safe, just head inside and select it with the action button. Enter the following combination, by pushing the analog stick in the appropriate direction: Left 9, Right 15, Left 7. This is the same combination for both characters, so if you’re on your first or later playthrough, you can use this to get inside and grab the valuable item hidden within. What is that item?

    A Hip Pouch. These little bags may not seem all that important, but trust us that they are. They expand your inventory by two spaces, letting you carry more items around with you as you explore RPD and areas beyond. Want to get more of them? Check out this guide that lays out all of their locations.

    That’s the West Office safe combination in Resident Evil 2 Remake. If you have any other safes you need cracked, or secrets explained, be sure to check out our many other guides.
