    Captive Firefighter Ashley

    Now, we’re going to go back into the heart of Bleake Island with one that is closer to the 2nd firefighter we saved. In fact, you might spot this one on the way to #2 if you were doing that one later in the game. This one takes place up on a rooftop, with a drop in numbers to 8 thugs, but some are packing knifes this time.

    Captive Firefighter Green

    The next one takes us towards Miagini Island, just off of Mercy Bridge’s eastern side. This is a really easy one with only 7 enemies to fight. Make quick work of them and move along.

    Captive Firefighter Hill

    This is yet another easy group of guys to take down southwest of the last location. These thugs don’t have anything special about them, so take them down like the last with ease.

    Captive Firefighter Richardson

    This is one of the harder to find at first, because they are located inside a building. The enemy count is low at only 4, but you have 6 sentry drones to worry about. There is a hackable panel that can allow you to take your Batmobile inside, but if you drop down in the middle and are careful, you can easily disarm all of them and take down the enemies without much trouble.

    Captive Firefighter Daniell

    This one is rather easy to find as it is literally on the southeast side of Wayne Tower. Just go to that area and prepare to fight nine enemies, some of which are armed.

    Captive Firefighter Bliss

    This is where it starts to get difficult, due to the introduction of the ever annoying brute type enemies. Go to the location over on the northwest side of Founder’s Island and you must face off against 8 enemies, with 2 brutes included. The best strategy is to try and take them down first by hitting circle and then attacking on PS4, while countering others and then getting back to the bigger threat.
