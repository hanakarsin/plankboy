    My Hero Academia Chapter 390 Spoilers

    My Hero Academia Chapter 390 spoilers and raw scans have been released. Here is the chapter summary:

    • Chapter 390
    • Title: Todoroki Shoto: Rising
    • The chapter begins with Iida’s legs reaching their limit and him telling Shouto that he can only take him so far. He thanks him and propels himself using Phosphor. The cops are shocked because he has reached transonic speed.
    • Tsukauchi remembers All Might’s words about the two students being the key and realizes that he has finally become a good teacher. Iida then thanks his friend for allowing him to become his ideal self.
    • And that’s why he wishes for Shouto to become his ideal self. This is when a narration shows what he wrote in the letters to his mother: he says that he is giving his best every day to reach his classmates’ level. He’s even been practicing conversation.
    • These conversations have led to many conflicts, but the resolution of said conflicts has allowed him to understand other people’s feelings. His friends feel anger and cry even for strangers, and he also wants to be like them.
    • He then finally gets close enough to the rest of the family and thinks to himself that if Dabi’s origin was really something so simple, they could have solved it long ago. This is when he uses Great Glacial Aegir again, the same move he used in their previous fight.
    • Everyone is still watching and the Masegaki children are smiling. Shouto realizes that the rest of the family was also there, saying that this is a good thing. His ice attack alone wouldn’t have been enough to stop Dabi, since his fire was even hotter than in their fight.
    • He ends by saying that this just proves that he can’t do everything by himself, so he’s not a “masterpiece” at all. Touya regains consciousness, says “you guys…”, and then starts saying that everyone should die, including himself.
    • Natsuo sees the ice inside his chest and asks what he’s going to do next, because all that awaits him is a hellish life. Endeavor apologizes for not going to Sekoto Peak. Toya then says that he hates all of his family, and Endeavor asks him to tell him everything he feels.
    • He then apologizes to Rei for all the abuse, to Fuyumi for pushing all the responsibilities onto her, and to Natsuo for abandoning him. When he goes to say something to Shouto, he just mumbles, “I’m sorry…” and apparently loses consciousness..
    • Cut to Toga and, as she sees that the flames have gone out, she remembers Dabi’s words saying that they should all smile. Her eyes get teary, and she wonders, “Touya, were you able to smile?” In front of her, Ochako looks determined.
    • End of chapter.

    My Hero Academia Chapter 389 Recap: Can Shoto Save the Day?

    The Counter-Force has provided an update on the rising temperature of Dabi’s inferno, which has slowed down due to the Todoroki Family’s intervention. However, the temperature is not decreasing, indicating that an explosion is inevitable. Despite this, Rei, Fuyumi, and Natuso continue to use their Frost Quirks to cool Dabi down. Dabi finds pleasure in the attention he is receiving and reflects on Endeavor’s presence, wondering why this had not happened sooner.

    As the firebomb intensifies, the battle between the Heroes and the Twice Doubles heats up. Tiger urges everyone to retreat to avoid being caught in the blast zone while Uravity remains behind to confront Himiko. The Heroes and evacuated civilians evacuated the area on robots and vehicles, attempting to escape the explosion. Komari Ikoma assures the Masegaki Primary School students that everything will be okay, and Tamashiro happily agrees while watching the live broadcast on their tablet. Meanwhile, Hiroshi Tameda and others across the city watch the broadcast, praying for the Heroes’ success. Shoto and Ingenium race towards the location in their Engine-powered ice jet, with Shoto preparing to use his Phosphor.
