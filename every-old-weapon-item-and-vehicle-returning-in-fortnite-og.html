    Every Old Weapon, Item, and Vehicle Returning in Fortnite OG

    Prepare for a trip down memory lane with Fortnite OG, a month-long season dedicated to reliving the nostalgia of Fortnite Chapter One. This means the return of fan-favorite weapons, vehicles, and items from the past, all of which weve listed in this article!

    09 Feb 2024, 00:00

    Prepare for a trip down memory lane with Fortnite OG, a month-long season dedicated to reliving the nostalgia of Fortnite Chapter One. This means the return of fan-favorite weapons, vehicles, and items from the past, all of which we’ve listed in this article!

    Recommended Videos

    All Returning Weapons in Fortnite OG (Chapter 4, Season 5)

    Many weapons in Fortnite Season OG will be sporting their original models from Chapter One, such as the Assault Rifle and Pump Shotgun, while others will keep their modern redesigns.

    All of the following classic weapons will be featured in Fortnite Season OG:

    • Assault Rifle (original)
    • Burst Assault Rifle
    • Suppressed Assault Rifle
    • Scoped Assault Rifle
    • Light Machine Gun
    • Pump Shotgun
    • Tactical Shotgun
    • Heavy Shotgun
    • Submachine Gun
    • Suppressed Submachine Gun
    • Pistol
    • Hand Cannon
    • Semi-Auto Sniper Rifle
    • Bolt-Action Sniper Rifle
    • Hunting Rifle
    • Rocket Launcher
    • Grenade Launcher
    • Heavy Assault Rifle
    • Double Barrel Shotgun
    • Six Shooter
    • Minigun
    • Drum Gun
    • Combat Shotgun
    • Tactical Submachine Gun
    • Flint-Knock Pistol
    • Dual Pistols
    • Suppressed Sniper Rifle
    • Quad Launcher

    Items, vehicles, and weapons will be implemented across Fortnite OG’s four major weekly updates. Items will be rotated out and replaced with “new” ones regularly, keeping the loot pool fresh and far less confusing.

    Weapons like the Heavy Sniper Rifle and Pump Shotgun can one-shot once again at high rarities with headshots. Keep your head down, look out for sweats, and think twice about landing at Tilted Towers — just like old times.

    All Returning Items in Fortnite Season OG

    Almost every item from Chapter One is returning in Fortnite OG, including the iconic Jetpack and, my personal favorite, Mounted Turrets. Some items from newer Chapters, like the Grapple Glove, may also be making an appearance based on info data mined in the initial update.

    All of the following classic items and traps will be featured in Fortnite Season OG:

    • Grenade
    • Impulse Grenade
    • Boogie Bomb
    • Stink Bomb
    • Smoke Grenade
    • Grappler
    • Port-A-Fort
    • Rift-To-Go
    • Bush
    • Chug Jug
    • Damage Trap (Builds only)
    • Launch Pad (Builds only)
    • Cozy Campfire (Builds only)
    • Chiller (Builds only)
    • Mounted Turret (Builds only)
    • Clinger
    • Dynamite
    • Port-A-Fortress
    • Grapple Glove
    • Buried Treasure
    • Balloons
    • Redeployabel Glider
    • Poison Dart Trap (Builds only)
    • Bouncer (Builds only)
    • Jetpack
    • Air Strike
    • Junk Rift
    • Storm Flip

    Some of these items haven’t been seen in Battle Royale for years. Remember the wearable Bush? That hasn’t been in the game for over four years. I know I shouldn’t be this excited for a bush, of all things, but nostalgia is one heck of a thing.

    Related: Fortnite Only Up Map Code: How to Play Only Up for Free in Fortnite

    Shield Potions, bandages, and medkits have never been removed from Fortnite, so we haven’t included them in the list, but they are still available.

    All Returning Vehicles in Fortnite OG

    Fortnite OG has less of a focus on cars and more on fun and unique vehicles like ATKs, Driftboards, Stormwings (planes), and Shopping Carts.

    All of the following classic vehicles will be featured in Fortnite Season OG:

    • All Terrain Kart (ATK)
    • Shopping Cart
    • Baller
    • Driftboard
    • X-4 Stormwing
    • Quadcrasher
    • Pirate Cannon

    The great news is that vehicles don’t use fuel in Fortnite OG. You can ride to your heart’s content and blast tunes on the radio while you run into a squad on a Quadcrasher — or, if you’re me, a wall.

    This guide was written while playing Fortnite OG on Xbox Series X.
