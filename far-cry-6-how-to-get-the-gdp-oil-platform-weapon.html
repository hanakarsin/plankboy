    The GDP Oil Platform contains a unique weapon in Far Cry 6, but you’ll have to solve a puzzle involving one of the cranes to reach it. You’ll visit the GDP Oil Platform during the Diesel Daisy main story mission, but you can go to the location at any time due to Far Cry 6‘s open-world nature. It’s located in the Aguas Lindas region of Madrgugada, which is the northwestern section of Yara. Here’s how to solve the crane puzzle and get the GDP Oil Platform weapon in Far Cry 6.

    How to Get the GDP Oil Platform Weapon in Far Cry 6

    The weapon on the GDP Oil Platform is inside a shipping container near one of the cranes, but another container is blocking the entrance. In order to get the weapon, you have to move the crate. Head to the southwest corner of the platform and look for the room with the number 16 posted on the side. Go inside and you should find the crane controls.

    Press the red button by the window and the crane will lift one of the crates out of the way. Now, just go back to the shipping container with the weapon and you should be able to get inside. This shipping container has a few bundles of resources like Medicine and Metal that you can pick up, but the main draw is the weapon chest containing a unique shotgun called the Supercharger.

    The Supercharger is a unique variant of the SPAS 12 shotgun. It comes equipped with two attachment mods: Buckshot Rounds and Fast Reload. Buckshot Rounds make the weapon incredibly powerful at close range but harms its long-range capabilities. Fast Reload is fairly self-explanatory, improving the weapon’s reloading speed. It also has a suppressor, making it a great stealth option.

    Far Cry 6 is available now on PC, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X|S, Google Stadia, and Amazon Luna.
