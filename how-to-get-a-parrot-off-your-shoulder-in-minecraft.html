    Are you wondering how to get a parrot off your shoulder in Minecraft? Parrots are one of the many mobs in Minecraft that can be tamed. They come in various colors and make for great companions when you are playing alone, but they can get annoying as they constantly follow you around and sit on your shoulder without asking first. Unfortunately, the game doesn’t give explicit instructions on getting the parrot to go away but don’t worry. Here is how to tame and remove a parrot from your shoulder in Minecraft if you find it annoying.

    How to Get a Parrot Off Your Shoulder in Minecraft

    Before a parrot sits on your shoulder in Minecraft, you must tame it. We have provided you with instructions on how to tame a parrot if you are unsure how to. After it is tamed, it can sit on your shoulder. We have also provided you with instructions on how to remove a parrot from your shoulder.

    How to Tame a Parrot in Minecraft

    First, a parrot will only land on your shoulder and hitch a free ride if you have tamed it. Wild parrots will not sit on your shoulder. To tame a parrot, you need to feed it a seed first. Any seed will do, and each one has a 33% chance of taming the parrot. Once you have tamed a parrot, it will sit on the ground or fly and try to land on your shoulder. The parrot can end up on your shoulder if you walk through it, too.

    How to Remove a Parrot From Your Shoulder in Minecraft

    You need to decrease elevation quickly to remove a parrot from your shoulder. You can jump off a mountain into the water, go underwater, or even fly downward with an Elytra. Each of these methods will have the parrot get off of your shoulder. Once the parrot is off, tell it to sit so it won’t land on your shoulder again.
