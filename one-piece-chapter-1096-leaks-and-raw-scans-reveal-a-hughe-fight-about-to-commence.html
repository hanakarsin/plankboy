Keep reading to learn more about the leaks and raw scans of One Piece Chapter 1096.

    Beware of major spoilers for One Piece!

    One Piece Chapter 1096 Leaks and Rumors

    According to the information shared online by trusted leakers like @OP_SPOILERS2023, the chapter will continue right where the previous one left off. After saving Kuma from the Marines that captured him, Ivankov, Ginny, and the previously mentioned young man would formulate a plan to escape. They would steal the Devil Fruits that were supposed to be given as prizes for the hunt.

    Related: Every One Piece Arc and Filler Arc, in Order

    While the kids plan, two powerful pirate crews will arrive at God Valley, the Rocks Pirates and the Roger Pirates. Besides them, a young Garp will also be landing, called to take care of the criminals. Roger will attempt to stop his fellow pirate captain, only to be silenced.

    Before Ivankov can eat one of the two Devil Fruits on the island, Big Mom will take it. This will be Kaido’s Devil Fruit in the future. Kuma will be able to eat his Devil Fruit, being transported in front of Saturn, who attempts to kill him. The battle will not be shown, as the chapter will skip forward in time to the Sorbet Kingdom, where Kuma and Ginny will be shown eating together, hinting at the fact that Ginny is pregnant.

    Chapter 1096 Raw Scans

    As this upcoming chapter takes place decades before the beginning of One Piece, all the characters present in God Valley are shown as younger than we are used to. The Rocks Pirates will be seen almost completely, with Kaido, Big Mom, Whitebeard, and Rocks himself taking the spotlight. We will also see a young Roger, without his iconic beard and wearing the straw hat that Luffy will inherit.

    From the Marines’ side, fans will get a glimpse of a young Garp and Bogart, as well as the silhouettes of some of the God Knights. Lastly, Saturn will be seen in his human form, with clouds floating around him, similar to the ones that we saw recently. This chapter will be without a doubt one of the most pivotal entries to this final arc of the series.
