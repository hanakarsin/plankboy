    Best Haymaker Zombies Build in Modern Warfare 3

    Considered one of the most complete shotguns in Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3, the damage and high handling of the Haymaker make the weapon a dream pick for Zombies players looking to excel at close range.

    Recommended Videos

    But although the gun can be great at its default state, a few modifications are a must if you plan on turning it into a game changer. Here’s the best Haymaker build in Modern Warfare 3 Zombies.

    Best Haymaker Zombies Build in MW3

    Since the name of the game for shotguns is speed, the best Haymaker build in Modern Warfare 3 Zombies will make use of the Barrel, Muzzle, Underbarrel, Stock, and Laser mods below to majorly boost handling and mobility, all while also adding a little extra dose of damage to the weapon.

    • Muzzle: Flash Twist-90
    • Barrel: Imperator Long Barrel
    • Underbarrel: FTAC SP-10 Angled Grip
    • Laser: FTAC Grimline Laser
    • Stock: No Stock Mod

    Related: Best Striker Zombies Build in Modern Warfare 3

    As you may have noticed, the foundation of our build lies in using the No Stock Mod for a massive increase in mobility and handling. We then make use of the FTAC Grimline Laser, the Flash Twist-90, and the FTAC SP-10 Angled Grip to counter the decrease in both Accuracy and Recoil Control that comes with the choice. The FTAC Grimline Laser can be seen as the most important attachment for our build after the No Stock Mod, as it offers a massive boost in the weapon’s performance when shooting from the hip.

    For our final mod pick, I decided to go with the Imperator Long Barrel for its increase in damage at almost no noticeable on-field drawback.

    Related: Best Bruen Mk9 Zombies Build in Modern Warfare 3

    If you still haven’t acquired the No Stock Mod Attachment — which you can do by completing its Armory Unlock — I recommend that you go with the Blitz Tactical Grip.

    Although the grip won’t offer the massive increase in mobility our preferred mod will, using it will allow you to boost the gun’s Sprint to Fire Speed and Tac Stance Spread while preserving the Accuracy and Recoil Control increases provided by the remaining attachments in our build.

    This guide was made while playing Modern Warfare 3 Zombies on PS5.
