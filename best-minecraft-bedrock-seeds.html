    Minecraft players will never run out of seeds to use, and that choice can be a daunting prospect, so many will go to online websites to try and find the greatest out there. If you are looking for many exciting seeds, that task has become more accessible. I have created this list for you to peruse some of the best Minecraft Bedrock seeds.

    Recommended Videos

    All of the Best Minecraft Bedrock Seeds to Use

    The Classic Heart | Seed: 5480987504042101543

    This particular seed has been included in one of my other Minecraft seed lists, and it has to make another appearance here. When you spawn into the world, you can find a literal indented heart shape in a mountain nearby. This can be great for any survival world that you want to play on with an excellent starting area.

    Castle Potential | Seed: 712262452098460

    If you enjoy seeds more tailored toward the Creative mode, this seed will undoubtedly give you much to work on. When you spawn, you will be near a mountain (around plenty of resources), and there are deep sinking pits of water with rocky outcrops found throughout. It is ideal for creating a castle along the length of the mountain, and I know you will be able to get some stellar builds made.

    Water Village | Seed: 712225023315282

    This world has a village in the middle of a lake surrounded by a desert. There are no other pieces of land near this village; it is simply a village in the middle of a lake and a spectacle to behold. Look no further than this seed to start a dock in a survival Minecraft world.

    The beauty of the Snow Mountain | Seed: -7203549084156040623

    A few seeds have incredible mountains on display, and this seed contains one of the most impressive mountains. It is surrounded by much flatter land, and three majestic peaks pierce the sky. This is one of the worlds you will want to see sooner rather than later — it also contains a Cherry Grove near the mountain.

    Massive Mushroom Fields | Seed: 6624115505620038213

    If you head north from spawn and go across the water, you will find this incredibly vast Mushroom Field/Island, which takes up a lot of space in the water. It will be perfect for resource gathering and any other reasons you may want to visit the larger Mushroom Fields biome.

    Triangle Building Ground | Seed: 1604997789706447567

    When I first saw this seed, it reminded me of the Horizon game series because it also has many triangular structures. This seed contains a mountain range directly next to the spawn, encasing a triangular shape. It can be ideal for creative mode or simply surviving in a snow-caked area that seems slightly out of place. It could be said to have an Uncanny Valley feeling to it.

    Gorgeous Forests and Caves | Seed: 69420

    Hilarious Seed numbers aside, this world contains a breathtaking cave entrance with many trees, vines, and more all surrounding it. There are also snowy mountains nearby, but the leading beauty of this seed exists in the forest and cave areas, all held in an oasis of trees.

    Cherry Grove Village | Seed: 4669574483087448116

    When you spawn into this world, if you go to the east, you will quickly find a village, which is great for any survival mode runs. However, the fancy part of this seed exists because cherry groves surround it, and the town is found in a pit of the surrounding landmass. It is a brilliant environment to explore and well worth the time.

    Inversion of Expectation | Seed: -7099415484010312016

    This seed is more of a game glitch but creates sights you won’t want to miss out on. Out of all the seeds on the list, this will be the most unique for you to experience. It towers as a skyscraping waterfall with land even above that — plenty of opportunity to explore everything about it. Travel to the coordinates of “725, 128, -400,” and you can make your discovery.

    Woodland Mansion in a Cave | Seed: 3602394602727634323

    This Mansion extends into a cave; from the outside, it appears to be nestled nicely into the mountain. This is a beautiful Mansion location, and plenty of survival players will likely benefit highly from utilizing its resources. Of course, this can be renovated for Creative mode players into something entirely new. The coordinates you will find it at are “-696, 392, 100”.

    Treasure Hunter’s Dream | Seed: 5092820108002433452

    Treasure hunting and Archeology were some of the main draws of Minecraft’s Trails & Tales update, and this seed gives you all the tools you need to jump into these new professions quickly. There are two Temples, a Traisl Ruin, a Dester Temple, and a Destroyed Portal within walking distance of your spawn point, and you should be able to find the tools and resources you need to become a top-tier archeologist lying around as well.

    Cave of Wonders | Seed: -6118459231602779665

    Caves are always fun to explore in Minecraft, and this seed gives you a massive cavern you can delve into at your leisure. Hidden just beneath the snowy Taiga Biome you spawn in, this expansive cave is full of Abandoned Mineshafts and Ancient Cities full of treasure and danger. There’s also a magnificent Lush Cavwe biome that will provide some much-needed beauty and comfort as you delve into the perilous depths below.

    Now that you have looked through this list of the best Minecraft Bedrock seeds, you can explore every one if you wish. I recommend starting with the Cherry Mountain one because of its excellent atmosphere.
