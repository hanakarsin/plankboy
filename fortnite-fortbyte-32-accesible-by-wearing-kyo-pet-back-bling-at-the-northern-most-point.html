    31 May 2024, 00:00

    Fortbyte #32 has been added to the Fortnite map, and this collectible item requires that players do two things to snag it.  The first is that you find the northern most point of the map.  The second is that you are wearing the Kyo Pet Back Bling when you do. Fortbyte #32 isn’t very hard to find, but unless you’ve progressed far enough into the Battle Pass, you may or may not have met the requirement of having Kyo.  This guide will explain exactly what you need to do and where you need to go to get Fortbyte #32.

    Fortbyte #32 Location is north east of Junk Junction

    This Fortbyte is at the northern most point of the map.  This is actually in the northwest corner, just northeast of Junk Junction.  At the spot you will find a sign that marks it as the northern most point on the map.  There you will also find a group of rocks, and within them will be the Fortbyte you are looking for.  When you see the rocks and the sign, like the image above, you are in the right spot.

    Once you’ve found Fortbyte #32 the only other requirement is that you have the Kyo Back Bling.  This is an Epic Pet item that is unlockable at Tier 28 of the Season 9 Battle Pass. If you’ve not collected this item yet, you will not be able to collect Fortbyte #32.  If you don’t have it yet, don’t worry.  You’ll be able to go back to this location once you’ve reached this tier to collect the Fortbyte.  If you are past level 28, simply head into the Locker in the main menu to make sure you have the Kyo Back Bling equipped before you head out to collect this Fortbyte.
