    With the release of the latest season in Fortnite Epic Games has given players a limited time to gift a Battle Pass to friends.  To gift a Battle Pass in Fortnite you’re going to want to head into the Battle Pass menu option which is the screen which has all of the new characters sitting around the challenge table.  In this menu you will see the option to explore the Battle Pass.  It’s the big door in the center of the screen.  Simply open this item and then you will see the option to gift a battle pass.   Once you press the button prompt you will be taken to a new screen that allows you to select the recipients of the Battle Pass.

    How to Gift Battle Pass in Fortnite

    You’re going to want to use this option and follow the on screen prompt to start the gifting process.  You can select your recipient of the Battle Pass in this screen.

    You can give up to 5 daily gifts to players in this menu.

    Gifting friends a Battle Pass is a good way to help them out with a one-time kick start for Fortnite.  Once players have a Battle Pass, they can earn enough V-bucks by completing challenges in the pass to earn enough V-bucks to purchase their own pass when Season 11 rolls around.  The Battle Pass also gives players a chance at unlocking unique skins, emotes, items and more by leveling through all the tiers.  A gifted Battle Pass will give your friend the same benefits that buying it normally would.

    Battle Pass Gifting Option has been activated for Fortnite Chapter 2 Season 1, and Chapter 2 Season 2.
