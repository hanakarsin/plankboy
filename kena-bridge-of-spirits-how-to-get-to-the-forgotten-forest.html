    Early in Kena: Bridge of Spirits, you’ll be sent to meet Rusu in the Forgotten Forest. Reaching this area is easier said than done, however, because there’s a tricky door puzzle that’s preventing you from accessing the forest. To open this door, you have to use your newly acquired bow to shoot crystals hidden around the door. Here’s how to open the Forgotten Forest door and meet Rusu in Kena: Bridge of Spirits.

    How to Get to the Forgotten Forest in Kena: Bridge of Spirits

    To open the door to the Forgotten Forest, you have to shoot the nearby crystals using your bow. There are three of them hidden around the door, and you have to shoot them in the correct order. Here are their locations:

    • Facing the door, look up and to the left. There’s a crystal on a diagonal tree above the path to the left.
    • Shooting the first crystal will release a trail of light to the next crystal to the left of the door. Shoot that crystal next.
    • Shooting the second crystal will release another stream of light to the crystal to the right of the door. Shoot that crystal next

    Basically, just shoot all three crystals from left to right starting with the one on the tree to the far left. If you shoot the correct crystal first, the particle stream that comes out of it will guide you to the rest. After you shoot the third crystal, the door will open and you can enter the Forgotten Forest. Rusu will be waiting for you inside.

    Kena: Bridge of Spirits has quite a few puzzles like this going forward, so always be on the lookout for crystals that may be out of your reach. The Forgotten Forest in particular has a tricky puzzle involving the Fishing Shrine, so be ready for that.

    Kena: Bridge of Spirits is available now on PC, PS4, and PS5.
