    Zelda Tears of the Kingdom Black Lizalfos Locations and Farming Spots

    Among the many droppable Lizalfos parts available in The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom, the Black Lizalfos Horn can be considered one of the best, especially for the early/mid-game. But where can you find the Black Lizalfos and thus get the item? Now, here are the best Black Lizalfos farming spots in Zelda Tears of the Kingdom, as well as a few more locations sure to feature the enemy.

    Recommended Videos

    Best Black Lizalfos Farming Spots in Zelda Tears of the Kingdom

    Although you won’t find areas swarming with the enemy in the game, the best place for Black Lizalfos farming in The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom can be found in the Depths, more specifically in the Great Abandoned Central Mine area.

    Once there, you will be able to find a series of four camps featuring from one to three Black Lizalfos each. You will be able to reach the camps easily by going to Nihcayam Lighroot and then heading to the spots marked on the map below.

    Related: Silver Lizalfos Locations in Zelda Tears of the Kingdom (TOTK)

    We were also able to find two camps featuring more than one Black Lizalfos on the surface. The camps will be located west of Tukarok Shrine and northwest of Morok Shrine, as you can see below.

    Black Lizalfos Locations: Where Else Can You Find Black Lizalfos in Zelda TOTK?

    We were also able to find the enemy, although in lower quantities, in the Necluda region, on Stinger Cliffs (under the coordinates 2780, -2565, and 0217), and in an area located between Mount Dunsel and Cape Cales (coordinates 3334, -3239, and 0171). If you still haven’t completed the Ruffian-Infested Village Side-Adventure, you will also be able to find one among the monsters there.

    On West Necluda, you can find a camp featuring a Black Lizalfo in Ovli Plain, under the coordinates 3184, -1831, and 0068. It is also possible to find one on a camp amidst the snow on Madourna Mountain, located on Mount Lanaryu, under the coordinates 4041, -1683, and 0344.
