    Fast Travel boards in Forza Horizon 5 are going to be one of the most sought after collectibles in the game.  There are 50 Fast Travel Boards in Forza Horizon 5 and getting them all will allow you to fast travel without having to pay any credits.  Getting these boards might seem like an insurmountable task, but with this guide you should be able to identify the locations and find them pretty easily.

    How to Find All Fast Travel Boards

    While we could make a map with every fast travel board it’s going to just be easier to tell you how to use the in-game map.  Playground Games has done the work here and given you a map with the locations of all the fast travel bonus boards.  You simply need to open the map by the method of your choice and then access the map filter function. You can access the map filter by pressing either the RB Button (Xbox Controller) or Page Down on PC.  ‘

    Once in this settings menu you can unselect all other icons on the map except for the Bonus Boards.  This will take everything off of the in-game map except the boards you are looking for.  However, once you filter these you will see all bonus boards, including the XP ones.  You will need to look for the boards with a lightning bolt on them for the fast travel locations.

    From there getting these boards is as easy as plotting a course on your GPS and finding the location.  Some might be in hard to reach locations and you can plot out a strategy accordingly.

    Once you’ve got the 50 boards clear you will no longer need to pay for fast travel in Forza Horizon 5.
