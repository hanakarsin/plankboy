    V Rising has numerous bosses that you will be able to eliminate while surviving within the meticulously crafted world that you are offered as the player. While you’re searching for resources, you will have to be prepared for any creatures you may encounter and of course any bosses. One such ‘boss’ is the Alpha Wolf and this guide article will take you over everything you need to know about the V Rising Alpha Wolf encounter including where to find it and how to eliminate it quickly.

    How To Find And Defeat The Alpha Wolf In V Rising

    The Alpha Wolf can be found in the Farbane Woods in the Wolf Den area so preparing for venturing into that territory will certainly be a benefit for you. You can also track the boss’s location with the ‘Blood Alter’ construction. It should be noted that the Alpha Wolf can specifically be found in the south section of the woods so bring whatever you deem is necessary for the battle with the wolf.

    When you have reached the Alpha Wolf’s location, you will notice that it is a level 16 boss so if you are still leveling up early on it may be a little bit of a challenge for your character to battle against. However, with the correct movement strategy while fighting it you will no doubt breeze through this boss in no time.

    When you are fighting the Alpha Wolf, make sure to keep on the move and avoid as many attacks as you can from the boss. Further, crafting some armor for your character or even eliminating the boss with some other players will all be an advantage to your battle. Make sure to bring along the necessary supplies for healing also. It’s now time to get ready for the Alpha Wolf boss battle in front of you, time to make your presence known in the lands.

    V Rising is available now with Early Access for Steam.
