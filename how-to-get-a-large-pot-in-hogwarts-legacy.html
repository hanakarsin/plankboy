    Are you wondering how to get a Large Pot in Hogwarts Legacy so you can plant seeds for flora like Fluxweed Stem for use in potions? Once you unlock the Room of Requirement, you will need to purchase Spellcrafts, which allow you to place crafting tables used for growing seeds and brewing potions.

    Recommended Videos

    One such item is a Potting Table, which comes in small, medium, and large sizes. It may not be clear where to get the Spellcraft for a Large Pot for several reasons, mainly because the game calls them something else entirely. Whether you’re a budding herbalist or just looking to learn Flipendo, here is where you can obtain a Large Pot in Hogwarts Legacy.

    Where to Find a Large Pot in Hogwarts Legacy

    You can obtain a Potting Table with a Large Pot at Tomes and Scrolls shop in Hogsmeade. A table with a single Large Pot costs 1000 Galleon, while a table with two Large Pots is 3000 Galleon. The best way to get to the Tomes and Scrolls shop is by using the South Hogsmeade Floo travel point.

    From there, you will want to open your map and look for the Scroll icon in the southern part of Hogsmeade. Then, set your waypoint and head toward the purple icon. You will be there in no time!

    Once at the shop, you can speak with Thomas Brown to purchase either the Potting Table With A Large Pot or Potting Table With Two Large Pots Spellcraft. After your shopping trip, don’t forget to acquire the Hogsmeade Field Guide page floating around by the roof inside the shop.

    How Do You Use a Large Pot in Hogwarts Legacy?

    Now that you have a Large Pot Spellcraft, head back to the Room of Requirement. Equip your Conjuring spell and go to the Herbology section. Navigate through the various submenus until you find the one that contains Potting Tables. Select the Spellcraft you purchased and place it wherever you choose.

    To plant something, head over to the Potting Table and press the button displayed to open the planting menu. Choose the seed you wish to plant and return to the table once the timer has finished. Once seeds have been planted, the table will continue to grow the plant infinitely until different seeds have been chosen.

    Now that you know where to buy Spellcrafts for Potting Tables and how to use them in the Room of Requirement, you should head over to the Dogweed and Deathcap shop to purchase fertilizer and seeds. You may even want to invest in a Dung Composter to produce plant fertilizer and increase your yield.
