    Are you wondering about all trash can locations in Belobog in Honkai: Star Rail? This is one of two questions asked by a trash can in Belobog. Yeah, you read that right. A trash can is now quizzing you. First, you earned a trash can profile picture, and now you are on the trashiest game show in Honkai. However, don’t throw this guide away because we have the answers you need to win a “prize,” if you can call it that.

    All Trash Can Locations in Belobog

    While you don’t need to find each individual trash can in Belobog to solve the puzzle, you can visit them all to grab yourself some extra items. We’ve highlighted the location of all twenty trashcans above.If you only care about answering the mysterious trash can puzzle, we’ve included all of the answers below.

    How Many Trash Cans Are in Belobog?

    You can find Honkai: Star Rail’s Trash Can Puzzle in the center of Belobog City in Jarilo IV. Head to the location marked on the map above and interact with the garbage can you find there to begin the puzzle. Don’t question why you’re talking to it; we don’t know either.

    Related: How to Get the Trash Can Player Icon in Honkai Star Rail

    You can solve the Trash Can puzzle in Honkai-Star Rail by answering two questions about the number of trash cans in Belobog and the Town. As much as you’d undoubtedly love running around Belobog City counting trash cans, we’ll save you the effort!

    The first question from the trash can is:

    • Guess: How many trash cans are there in Belobog? Attention: trash cans put side-by-side together are considered as one. But those that you can see but cannot be reached do not count.

    The answer to this question is: 20

    The second question from the trash can is:

    • Guess: How many trash cans are there in this own? The rules are the same: trash cans together are considered one. But those you can see but cannot be reached do not count. Count only these: Cylindrical trash cans.

    The answer to this question is: 5

    Don’t you feel better knowing there are twenty trash cans in Belobog, and five are cylindrical? You’re welcome; we’re delighted to have improved your life so significantly.

    So then, what is the trashtastic prize you will win by answering both of these questions correctly? First, you will earn one Pleasant-Looking Trash consumable, which will recover 4 Technique Points for the team. Besides this consumable item, there are no other rewards for solving the Trash Can Puzzle other than bragging rights that other trash aficionados like yourself probably don’t care about.

    Your newly gained Pleasant-Looking Trash can come in handy during challenging fights. Free instant Technique Points are always welcome, even when your team has to stop mid-battle to chow down on garbage. Just don’t think about it too much.

    Honkai’s trash can

    If you were thinking that Honkai: Star Rail’s many references to trash cans lead to some sort of elaborate secret, you aren’t alone. Unfortunately, that isn’t the case; HoYoVerse, and now their fans, simply seem to adore trash cans. So much so, in fact, that they released an official Trash Can figure as a bonus to those who order the Honkai: Star Rail chibi figure set.
