    Pokemon Lets Go: Where to Find Eevee

    26 Jan 2024, 00:00

    While the formula may have changed a lot over the years, the core of any good Pokemon game is all about catching them all. This means you need to catch everything you can, which often requires searching far and wide for rare spawns. And sometimes these spawns aren’t even all that rare, like with Eevee. This fan-favorite can be obtained right away if you buy the right version, but for those with the Pikachu variant, they can be harder to find. Here’s where to find Eevee in Pokemon Let’s Go Pikachu and Eevee.

    If you bought Pokemon Let’s Go Eevee, then you’ll have your first Eevee right away. You’ll get it at the start of the game, and they will quickly become your trusty companion throughout your journey. However, this Eevee cannot be evolved, so you’ll need more. And if you bought Let’s Go Pikachu then you won’t get that initial Eevee, making it even harder. Luckily, no matter which version you own, there’s one place to get them.

    Eevee spawns pretty regularly on Route 17, which is encountered much later in the game. By this point you will have four gym badges, the Silph Scope, and the Poke Flute, so if you aren’t at that point yet, just be patient. Eventually you will make it to this road though, which sits to the west of Celadon City. Here you will encounter a number of trainers sporting their evolved Eevee. If you wander through the grass here long enough, you should start seeing Eevee spawns.

    And that’s where to find Eevee in Pokemon Let’s Go Pikachu and Eevee.
