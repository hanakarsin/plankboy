    Are you looking for a list of all Demiguise statue locations in Hogwarts Legacy so you can upgrade your Alohomora spell? You need your Alohomora spell to pick the locks of doors, which hold valuable loot. The locks become increasingly higher in level and the only way to unlock these is by upgrading your Alohomora spell. There are a total of 31 Demiguise Statue locations in Hogwarts Legacy. You will get Alohomora level 2 by turning in 9 Demiguise Statues and level 3 by turning in 13 of them. This may seem like a daunting task, but don’t worry because we have all locations below. Here are where to find all Demiguise Staute Locations in Hogwarts Legacy.

    Where to Find all Demiguise Statue Locations in Hogwarts Legacy

    Gladwin Moon will tell you that he is being tormented by statues spread throughout Hogwarts and will give you an upgrade to your Alohomora spell if you can return them all to him. You will need to progress the game until you are given the main story quest: Prepare For Your Search For The Final Keeper to unlock the ability to turn in Demiguise Statues.

    Remember that the Demiguise statues can be found during the day but only collected at night. We recommend you use your Revelio spell because it highlights them in blue, which will help you locate them more quickly, especially if they are locked behind doors. You can also check the number of Demiguise Statues you have left to find in a particular area by pulling up your map. The total number will be in the legend on the right side of the map.

    All Demiguise Statue Locations in Hogwarts

    There are a total of 10 Demiguise Statues for you to find in Hogwarts:

    • Gryffindor Toilets (Southern Wing): You will find this Demiguise Statue in the toilets near the Gryffindor Tower. Head down the stairs, turn left, and the toilets will be the second door. The statue is in the third stall.
    • Defence Against The Dark Arts Class (Astronomy Wing): You will find this Demiguise Statue sitting on the desk of the Defence Against The Dark Arts Classroom.
    • Muggle Studies (Astronomy Wing): You will find this Demiguise Statue sitting on a desk inside the Muggle Studies classroom. This classroom can be found in the basement underneath the courtyard.
    • Boggart Cabinet Room (Astronomy Wing): You will find this Demiguise Statue sitting on a dresser near a Boggart cabinet. The room is at the bottom of the Astronomy Wing behind a door with a symbol on it.
    • The Great Hall (Great Hall): You will find this Demiguise Statue behind a locked door at the end of this location next to the statues. It is sitting on a table next to a fireplace and chairs.
    • Divination Classroom (Library Annex): You will find this Demiguise Statue sitting on a desk inside of the Divination classroom.
    • Restricted Section (Library Annex): You will find this Demiguise in the lower area of the Restricted Section of the library.
    • Potions Store Room (Library Annex): You will find this Demiguise sitting on a crate inside of a room in the corridor filled with portraits. Start at the Potions Classroom, walk through the doors, head left down the spiral staircase, turn left down the corridor, and you will find the Potions Store Room.
    • Beasts Class (Bell Tower): You will find this Demiguise behind a door in the Beasts classroom. You will need your Alohomora spell to level 2 so you can unlock this door.
    • Castle Ramparts (Bell Tower): You will find this Demiguise sitting on a crate in the castle ramparts next to an exit of Hogwarts grounds.

    All Demiguise Statue Locations in Hogsmeade

    There are a total of 10 Demiguise Statues to find in Hogsmeade:

    • Three Broomsticks Inn: You will need to use your Alohomora spell to unlock a door on the inn’s top floor. Take the stairs and head to your right to find the Demiguise Statue.
    • Tomes and Scrolls: You will find this Demiguise Statue sitting on a dresser drawer in the back room of Tomes and Scrolls.
    • Gladdrags Wizardwear: You will find this Demiguise Statue inside the Gladdrags Wizardwear shop. It is sitting on a table between a cash register and an anvil.
    • Hog’s Head: You will find this Demiguise Statue in the back room, which is located behind Hog’s Head’s front counter.
    • Cassandra Mason’s House: You will find this Demiguise Statue in Cassandra Mason’s house, located in the North East part of Hogsmeade. It is sitting on a table under a painting. Remember that this house is where you began the haunted shop quest.
    • Near Three Broomsticks Inn: You will find this Demiguise Statue in the house to the east of Three Broomsticks Inn. You will need to use your Alohomora spell to unlock the door. Then, head to the second level, and you will find it next to the stairs on a table.
    • Near the Waterwheel: You will find this Demiguise Statue in the house next to the waterwheel, located in the North part of Hogsmeade.
    • Near the Pond: You will find this Demiguise Statue in a house located across the pond, located near the waterwheel.
    • Near the Quidditch Shop: This Demiguise Statue is close to the Quidditch Shop in Hogsmeade. You will want to go down the short path until you see a food cart. The statue is located on the top of the building with two front doors.
    • Near J. Pippins Potions: You will find this Demiguise Statue in the house to the right of J. Pippins Potions. You will need your Alohomora skill to be level 2 to access this location.

    All Demiguise Statue Locations in Highlands

    There are a total of 11 Demiguise Statues to find in Highlands:

    • Aranshire: You will find this Demiguise statue in a house to the right of the floo travel point. It is sitting on the floor by a bed and desk on the second level. You will need your Alohomora spell to level 2 so you can access this house.
    • Bainburgh: You will find this Demiguise Statue inside of a building with a bunch of cauldrons stacked on its exterior.
    • Brocburrow: You will find this Demiguise Statue in a house in the middle of town next to a vendor. It is sitting on a bookshelf below a teapot.
    • Cragcrofts You will find this Demiguise Statue on the top level of a building behind a vendor at the center of town.
    • Feldcroft: You will find this Demiguise Statue inside a house, located to the east, that is covered with vines. You will need your Alohomora spell to level two, so you can access this building.
    • Hogsfield (Lower): You will find this Demiguise statue in the Bickel house. It is sitting on the floor next to the door of the house’s interior.
    • Hogsfield (Upper): You will find this Demiguise Statue in the house, which has a wheel on its exterior, in the center of town.
    • Irondale: You will find this Demiguise statue in the house closest to the Irondale floo travel point. The statue is sitting behind a counter on the floor next to a stove.
    • Keendale: You will find this Demiguise statue in a house sitting on top of a hill. The house has pumpkins on its exterior, which should make it easier to identify. You will find the statue sitting on a table to the right of the stairs.
    • Marunweem: You will find this Demiguise Statue inside a building with a triangular roof. You will need your Alohomora spell to level two to access this building.
    • Pitt-Upon-Ford: You will find this Demiguise statue in a house next to the large tree and carriage. You can find this house by taking the path into the village right before you reach the bridge. The statue is sitting on the floor next to a lamp on the top level.
