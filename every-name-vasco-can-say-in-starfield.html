    Starfield’s massive size extends far beyond its environment. Bethesda has put a great level of detail that applies not just to the worlds players will explore, but also to the dialogue they’ll hear. An early companion, Vasco, is a prime example of this massive pool of voice lines. Anyone creating their character might want to check out every name Vasco can say in Starfield, as the robotic ally will state those names through its voice instead of simple text.

    Full List of Every Name Vasco can Say in Starfield

    The names Vasco can say are taken from a predetermined list found within the game’s files. Some dedicated fans have leaked the list online, clearing up any specific words that are or aren’t allowed. Long-time Bethesda fans might recognize this feature from Fallout 4, which allowed a similar robotic companion to voice one’s name.
