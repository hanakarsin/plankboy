    NBA 2K23 has some challenging quests and The Classic is just one of them. In this quest, Herschel and Edgar will ask you to answer correctly some basketball trivia. Whether you are new to basketball or just need a refresher, here are all of the answers to The Classic quest in NBA 2K23.

    All The Classic Quest Answers in NBA 2K23

    After you create your MyPlayer and progress through the Welcome to the League quest, you’ll inevitably get The Classic quest. This quest will require you to answer three basketball trivia quests correctly in order to continue.

    Once you sit down to play chess with Edgar and Herschel, they will begin to grill you on historic basketball trivia. In order to complete the quest, you’ll need to answer these questions correctly.

    Here are all of the answers to The Classic quest:

    • Q: How many championships did Bill Russel win?
    • Q: Which team has lost the most NBA Championships?
    • Q: Which player was taken number one overall in the 1998 NBA Draft?
            • A: Michael Olowokandi

    After answering all of these questions correctly, you will continue to progress through The Classic quest and the MyCareer story. You will be granted some MVP Points and some VC for your troubles and be ready for the next challenge.

    All Objectives for The Classic in NBA 2K23

    In case you were wondering how many objectives the Classic entails — we have them listed in this section. Below you will find the complete list of all objectives for the Classic questline in NBA 2K23 and the rewards you receive for completing them all.

  • Meet Mila at the office to see what she wants to discuss.
  • Win the Business, Music, and Fashion districts.
  • Win over fans and hoopers at Moss Point, Robert Miles Park, and Dreamer Court.
  • Head to Hammond Park to get Edgar and Herschel’s stamp of approval (quiz).
  • Play NBA Games until you hear from DJ.
  • Play NBA Game
  • Get to Hammond Park ASAP to meet Kenny Cruz.
  • Talk strategy with your team back at the office.
  • Play your next 2 NBA Games.
  • Play NBA Game
  • Go to Hammond Park. The Classic is about to begin.
  • Tell Kenny Cruz you’re ready to play.
  • Play in The Classic game.
  • Head back to the office to debrief with your team.

    • Rewards for Completing the Classic in NBA 2K23

    Once you have completed all the objectives mentioned above, you will be rewarded for your hard work and dedication. Below are the rewards you will receive for completing The Classic questline.

    • 60,000 MVP Points
    • 3,500 VC
    • 500,000 Fans
    • Championship Green Jersey
    • Championship Belt

    Now that you have completed the Classic, you have made a name for yourself in the NBA. Keep pushing to show the league what else you’re made of to acquire more fans, MVP points, and VC.
