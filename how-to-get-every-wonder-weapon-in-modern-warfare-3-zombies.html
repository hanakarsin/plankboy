    True to that, getting any of the available wonder weapons can be seen as a true testament of both commitment and ability. Here’s how to get every Wonder Weapon in Modern Warfare 3 Zombies.

    All Wonder Weapons in MW3 Zombies and How to Get Them

    Currently, the Ray Gun, the Wunderwaffe DG-2, and the Scorcher are the only Wonder Weapons available in MW3 Zombies. All Wonder Weapons can be acquired by trying your luck with the Mystery Box, as well as by completing both high-threat contracts and the Legacy Fortress Stronghold. If you want to avoid the RNG, you will get one Acquisition Ray Gun by completing the Act 3 Tier 4 Holdout mission.

    Related: All Mystery Box Locations in Modern Warfare 3 Zombie

    Upon getting the Acquisition form of any Wonder Weapon, equipping it will cause it to be permanently removed from your possession after a successful exfill. You can also get the Ray Gun and the Wunderwaffe DG-2 by crafting them after acquiring their schematic blueprints.

    How to Get the Ray Gun and the Wunderwaffe DG-2 Schematics in Modern Warfare 3 Zombies

    You will get the schematic for the Wunderwaffe DG-2 Wonder Weapon in Modern Warfare 3 Zombies during the Act 3 Tier 4 Holdout mission.

    The Ray Gun schematic, on the other hand, can be acquired by completing the Mega Abomination bounty contract. Although it’s possible that completing other high-threat contracts will reward you with the item, I was not able to confirm that in-game.

    Upon the completion of a contract, you will be able to check if you scored the Schematic by interacting with its Reward Rift. Upon getting both schematics, you will be able to craft one Ray Gun and one Wunderwaffe DG-2 every 48 hours.

    This guide was made while playing Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3 on PS5.
