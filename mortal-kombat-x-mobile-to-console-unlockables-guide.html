    29 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Mortal Kombat X may have just released this week for PS4, Xbox One, and PC, but that isn’t the only Mortal Kombat X to come out lately. Just like how NetherRealm gave us a mobile companion app to Injustice: Gods Among Us two years ago, they have once again done the same this time around.

    The mobile game is very basic but can be fun in bursts, by having it basically be a collectable style game where you gain card and card packs that can then be used to form a team and battle enemies. The more important aspect found here for many however are the unlockables found within the game. By connecting to your WBID, you can tie the console and mobile games together, where you can then unlock special costumes and such for the other version of the game. As a result, we thought it would be a good idea to provide you with a guide of just what you can unlock for the console and mobile games respectively.

    Also, many people do not have the device capable of playing the game, so just like with Injustice there is bound to be sharing of login information to help others unlock the costumes they wish. Feel free to let anybody know in the comments about any usable WBID that can help those without the ability or time to do it themselves.

    We will give you the rundown for both of the unlocks, with the first group being the actions you must complete in the mobile game to then unlock in the console. The second group will of course be the exact opposite, with the actions being done in the console game to unlock for mobile.
