    Fortbyte #51 has been added to Fortnite and this collectible has players looking for Peely’s Banana Stand to snag it. This Fortbyte does have another requirement as well in that players must have the Cluck Strut Emote as well. This guide will explain exactly what you need to do to get Fortbyte #51 in Fortnite Season 9.

    The first thing you’re going to need if you want to get Fortbyte #51 is to have unlocked the Cluck Strut Emote. This is a Rare Emote that is unlockable as part of the Season 9 Battle Pass. You will need to have progressed to Tier 31 to unlock the Cluck Strut emote. Once you have it, you’ll want to make sure you equip it to your character in the Locker Menu before heading out to search for Peely’s Banana Stand.

    Peely’s Banana Stand is located in Neo Tilted

    Assuming you’ve got the emote ready to go, Peely’s Banana Stand is located in the Southwest corner of Neo Titled. It’s a bright yellow building in bottom corner of the area and it should be pretty recognizable. There is a crosswalk in front of the building. In this crosswalk is Fortbyte #51. Simply do the Cluck Strut emote in front of the Fortbyte and you’ll be able to collect it.

    Once you’ve got the item you can head back into the Challenges>Fortbytes tab to see which piece of the puzzle Fortbyte #51 reveals. There are plenty of other Fortbytes found across the Fortnite map. We’ve compiled a list of all Fortbytes in Season 9 if you need help finding any of them.
