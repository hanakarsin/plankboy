    Industrial Circuits are a very important resource in Far Cry 6, and you can use them to craft advanced Supremo improvements and several weapon improvements at a Workbench. They’re quite hard to find, but there are a few ways to get them reliably. An improved Supremo and enhanced arsenal will make the fight against Anton Castillo’s forces much easier, so you’ll want to stock up on these and other resources as much as you can. These are the best ways to get Industrial Circuits in Far Cry 6.

    Best Ways to Get Industrial Circuits in Far Cry 6

    Industrial Circuits can be acquired in two ways. The first method to obtain them is to purchase them from Lola, the Black Market merchant located in most Guerilla Camps. The secondary method is to trade in animal meat at any vendor. Since Industrial Circuits are so rare, though, you’ll only be able to get them with top-quality meat from dangerous predators.

    If you want to purchase some from Lola, you’ll need a special currency called Moneda. You can earn Moneda by playing online co-op or by completing Special Operations. Once you finish the Far Cry 6 campaign, you can also earn Moneda from a new endgame activity called Insurgency. Lola sells other materials as well, so it may be worthwhile to farm Moneda by playing co-op.

    If you want to go the animal meat route, you’ll have to hunt Alpha Crocodiles, Alpha Jaguars, and Alpha Coyotes. These are the only three types of animal meat that you can trade for Industrial Circuits. When hunting, make sure to use a bow or carefully aim your shots if you want to use a gun. If you don’t get a headshot while using a gun, then you’ll only receive damaged animal meat. If you have an upgraded Hunter’s Lodge at any Guerilla Camp though, you can get the Animal Anatomy guide which allows you to get good meat from animals killed in any way.

    Once you get enough animal meat, you can go to any vendor and trade it in. You can also trade in more common materials for Yaran Pesos and other things. If you’d rather earn Industrial Circuits in the background, then you can also get it from Los Bandidos operations. This isn’t a guaranteed reward though, so you could get Medicine, Metal, or other resources instead.
