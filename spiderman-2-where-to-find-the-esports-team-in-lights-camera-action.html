    How to Find the Esports Team During the Lights, Camera, Action Mission in Spider-Man 2

    Players can find the Esports team in Spider-Man 2 inside a glass window playing games on computers during the Lights, Camera, Action mission. The easiest way to find this location is to start from the football field (where the Drone Club is hanging out) and head straight toward the campus building. The game will notify you to open your camera when you get close to the team.

    Check out the image below showing the Esports Team’s exact location and where it resides near the football field. The Esports Team is in the glass room on the right.

    If you prefer a world map view, we have you covered with an image below.

    How to Find the Drone Club in Lights, Camera, Action

    The Drone Club is the easiest to find as they are located on the large football field at the edge of campus. They can be seen flying a drone from the sidelines. Take a photo of them with your camera.

    How to Find Greenhouse in Lights, Camera, Action

    While standing on the football field and looking toward campus, you will want to swing to the left of the campus building to find the Greenhouse. You will notice solar panels and a bunch of students hanging out on a roof, land on it, and take a picture of the plants for the mission.

    After taking all the photos of the students, you will be tasked with one more objective before completing the Lights, Camera, Action mission. You’ll need to fly or swing around campus following the drone so the students can get one cool-looking continuous shot for their project for school. This is simple as all you need to do is to keep up with the drone, where I found that swinging is much easier than gliding since it’s faster.
