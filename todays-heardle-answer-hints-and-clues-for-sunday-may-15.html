    Another day, another new Heardle. There are countless Wordle alternatives out there, but Heardle has caught the attention of the music-loving crowd by offering a new song for them to guess every day. While some of the songs are classic hits that are instantly recognizable from just a single note, others are more obscure. If today’s Worlde doesn’t sound familiar to you, then don’t worry. We’ve got your back with hints, clues, and the answer for the May 15 Heardle.

    Heardle is the audio version of Wordle, giving you six guesses to figure out what the mystery song of the day is. You start by hearing one second of the song’s intro, and the game ultimately gives you 16 seconds of audio for your final guess. Here are some clues for today’s song.

    Clues for May 15 Heardle

    Here are a few clues to help you figure out today’s song.

    • This song was released in 1986.
    • According to Wikipedia, this song’s genre is dance-pop/worldbeat.
    • This song is the lead single on the album Graceland.
    • This song is by Paul Simon.

    If you still can’t quite figure out what the song of the day is, then keep reading below to see the solution for today’s Heardle.

    Heardle Answer for May 15

    Today’s song is “You Can Call Me Al” by Paul Simon from 1986. You can listen to the full song below.

    That’s all you need to know about the May 15 Heardle! If you’re into any of the other daily puzzle games, don’t forget to check out what the solution is to today’s Wordle puzzle. You may also like Knotwords, the latest word game that is slowly gaining more popularity across the internet.
