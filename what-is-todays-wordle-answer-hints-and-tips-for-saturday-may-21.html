    How Does Wordle Work?

    Wordle is a word-guessing game that came out almost a year ago where players have six tries to figure out the five-letter word of the day.

    When a player guesses a word, the game returns clues to find the Wordle of the day. If a player puts in a word and a letter has a green box around it, that means that the green letter is in the Wordle of the day and it is in the correct position. If the letter has a yellow box around it, that means that the letter is in the word, but not in the correct position. And if the letter has a gray box around it, that means that the letter is not in the word at all.

    Try out some alternative Wordle games if you want to guess more than just one word per day.

    You can play Wordle for free via the New York Times’ website on a smart device or PC.
