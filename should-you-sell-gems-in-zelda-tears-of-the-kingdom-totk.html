    Should You Sell Gems in Zelda Tears of the Kingdom? (TOTK)

    Early on in the Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom, youll find gems in caves and wonder if you should sell them in exchange for Rupees. Heres my best advice on whether you should sell or save gems in Zelda Tears of the Kingdom.

    Early on in the Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom, you’ll find gems in caves and wonder if you should sell them in exchange for Rupees. Here’s my best advice on whether you should sell or save gems in Zelda Tears of the Kingdom.

    Zelda Tears of the Kingdom: Should You Save or Sell Gems?

    While selling gems can be a great way to acquire a hefty amount of Rupees in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom, I wouldn’t recommend selling them consistently. There are two main reasons you should keep your gems instead of selling them: upgrading armor and increasing attack power.

    While each shot is expensive, Fusing a gem to an arrow can deal a lot of damage to a target. You can also Fuse a gem to your melee weapon to boost its attack power by a reasonable amount. I don’t recommend doing this because selling gems is one of the best ways to get Rupees, and many other objects in Hyrule make for a better Fuse, like the Flux Core 1.

    The second reason not to sell gems is because you’ll need them to upgrade your armor later. Each armor piece requires different gems, but it is worth keeping them because upgrading your armor makes you more difficult to kill. Depending on where you’re at in the game, I recommend keeping your gems if you’ve unlocked armor upgrading.

    Should You Save or Sell Diamonds in TOTK?

    Diamonds are the rarest gems in Zelda Tears of the Kingdom. Because of this, they fetch a high amount of Rupees when sold, typically around 500 Rupees at most shops but Goron City’s Ramella will buy 10 Diamonds for 5,500 Rupees. However, Diamonds can also significantly increase attack power when Fused with a melee weapon.

    In my opinion, you should always sell Diamonds. There aren’t many ways to get Rupees in Zelda Tears of the Kingdom, which makes Diamonds one of the best ways to get Rupees fast. If you want a lot of Rupees, check out the infinite Rupee trick. If you are looking for an excellent object to Fuse to boost attack power, use a Stone Talus heart.

    What Gems Should You Save and What Gems Should You Sell in TOTK?

    In my opinion, it is better to sell your gems and get good gear now than hold onto them for Fusing and armor upgrades. You can always farm for Amber and other gems later, but you’ll need to sell your gems to get good armor, which allows you to survive for longer.

    In short, yes, you can and should sell your gems, but know that you’ll want gems later for armor upgrading. By then, you can farm for whatever specific gem you need and feel confident doing so because you sold your gems to buy better armor. While Fusing gems to weapons is good, I recommend avoiding doing this, as plenty of other Fuse combinations are less expensive and more effective.

    Easy Methods to Make Money Fast in TOTK

    The absolute best way to make money fast is selling certain items. The items you want to sell in Zelda Tears of the Kingdom are construction parts, unwanted armor, monster parts, or Diamonds. While these items can sometimes help upgrade armor or deal with high attack power, they are incredibly easy to farm by simply killing the game’s enemies. In other words, you can farm them quickly for the purposes mentioned or for selling whenever you please. On the other hand, gems and diamonds are rare, so you should keep them instead of selling them.

    Meanwhile, selling unwanted armor is a no-brainer. There is no reason to keep armor that you no longer want or use unless you are someone who wants to keep it for collecting purposes. Armor pieces sell for a high amount of Rupees, so I recommend selling them.

    What is the Most Valuable Gem in Zelda Tears of the Kingdom?

    As previously mentioned in this guide, Diamonds are the rarest gem in Zelda Tears of the Kingdom, meaning they are also the most valuable. When we speak of “highest value,” we mean they go for the highest price at stores when selling. Selling Diamonds in Tears of the Kingdom will reward you around 500 Rupees!

    In addition to the selling value, Diamonds offer the highest damage boost, increasing each weapon by 25. That number sounds fantastic and can be very helpful against the game-challenging bosses, but regardless, you should always sell these rare commodities. The number of Rupees you receive when selling diamonds outweighs its damage benefits, and you can always find a boost in damage elsewhere by fusing other items.
