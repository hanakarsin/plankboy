    During your journey in Last Epoch, you’ll collect various items and materials from different locations, some of which you may need to share with your teammates. However, despite how much it would benefit players in the long run, is that something you can do in Last Epoch? Let’s dive in!

    Can You Link Items in the Chat in Last Epoch?

    In Last Epoch, you can link up to three items in a chat message by pressing Ctrl and right-clicking. To view a linked item, simply open the chat and hover over it. This feature was highly anticipated and one of the most requested by many players. The developers had promised its release between patches 0.9.0 and 0.9.1, and indeed, they delivered. However, due to some technical issues, it wasn’t fully operational until the release of the patch 0.9.1c in May 2023.

    What Does Linking Items in Chat Do?

    In multiplayer online games like Last Epoch, linking items in chat is a pivotal feature. It improves communication by enabling players to efficiently discuss specific items, share comprehensive information about an item’s attributes, and simplify trading between players. For those who play solo, this feature might not be of much use. However, for group play, it significantly eases everyone’s experience. Personally, I find this feature as crucial as autocasting.

    Looking forward, I am optimistic that the developers will continue to introduce additional quality-of-life features like this one. That said, it’s clear that Last Epoch has a bright future. The developers’ commitment to their game and community is commendable and typically a strong indicator of a game that will provide long-lasting entertainment to dedicated players.
