    One Piece Chapter 1075 Release Timeline and Spoilers

    We have provided an easy-to-follow release guide so you know precisely when One Piece chapter 1075 is expected to be released in your time zone. In addition, we have also provided all the details regarding the spoilers of One Piece chapter 1075.

    One Piece Chapter 1075 Spoilers

    Brief spoilers have been released. Here is what we know about Chapter 1075 so far:

    • Title: Labo Phase Death Game
    • Cover: Vegapunk Meets the Five Elders
    • Someone is destroying all the Den Den Mushi in the lab. Shaka can’t see what’s happening.
    • Straw Hat and Vegapunk Clones form 4 groups. One of the group sis attacked by S-Snake.
    • S-Bear and S-Hawk attack the control room. Shaka gives them an order to stop, but it doesn’t work.
    • Lucci and Kaku wake up and ask Luffy and Zoro to free them to fight together.
    • Lucci wants to fight alongside Luffy because two Seraphims are attacking them, and both he and Kaku can’t do anything being handcuffed with kairouseki (sea stone).
    • Kinbe is with Sanji and Stussy
    • Robin and Chopper are together, while Nami is with Brook. S-Snake (Seraphim Boa) attacks Franky’s group.
    • The rumor “spoiler” that S-Hawk has Diamond Devil Fruit’ is not true.

    There will be more spoilers to follow, so check back often.
