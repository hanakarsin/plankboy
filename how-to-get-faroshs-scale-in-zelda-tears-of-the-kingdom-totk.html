    How to Find Farosh’s Scale in The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

    Farosh typically occupies the skies of Hyrule between Gerudo Desert and Lake Hylia. To get Farosh’s Scale in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom, you should head to the location of the Gerudo Chasm where my marker is in the picture below. This is where he usually comes out of, and for me, he came out of this Chasm at approximately 2:00 PM. This Chasm is located directly south of Gerudo Canyon Skyview Tower.

    Since the time that Farosh spawns can change — I waited for Farosh to come out of the Chasm by heading to the sky platform shown in the picture below.

    Related: How to Do the Duplication Glitch in Zelda Tears of the Kingdom (TOTK).

    This location gives you the perfect view of the Chasm, where you can use your Purah Pad and zoom in on it until you see the Dragon spawn.

    When he spawns, wait for him to get closer to you and make your jump off the sky platform towards Farosh. You will want to aim for the Dragon’s back — preferably the center as the closer you get to the front, the more likely the chance of getting electrocuted.

    Once you make it onto his back, all you need to do is strike his back with a sword, causing a Farosh Scale to drop. It is important to note that the Scale only drops when the Dragon is glowing, so if it isn’t glowing by the time you land on it — wait patiently until it does. Only one of Farosh’s scales or claws can be harvested within a certain period of time.

    What Does Farosh’s Scale Do in Zelda Tears of the Kingdom?

    Farosh’s Scale is a crafting ingredient in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom. You can use it for several things, such as selling it for a hefty price, upgrading armor pieces, and creating yellow dye at the dye shop in Hateno Village. I used my Farosh’s Scale on a sword, which adds a huge increase in damage. Make sure not to add it to ingredients, as putting a Farosh scale into recipes will always result in dubious food.

    How to Get Each Body Part From Farosh in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

    Farosh’s Scale is not the only material to gather from the electric dragon. Each dragon in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom have five materials to acquire from them — each with its purpose and sell value. These five materials for Farosh include Scale, Horn, Spike, Claw, and Shards. Below you will learn how to obtain each material.

    • Farosh Scale – Strike the dragon’s back with a sword.
    • Farosh Horn – Strike Farosh’s horn by the dragon’s head.
    • Farosh Spike – Found around the spikes of the dragon’s back.
    • Farosh Claw – Jump off the dragon and shoot an arrow at its claw. This will cause the claw to fall below.
    • Farosh Fang Shard – Jump off the dragon and shoot an arrow at its mouth where the fang is. This will cause a Fang shard to fall below.

    Remember that most of these parts can only be acquired when the dragon is glowing. Dragon’s glowing essence occurs every ten real-world minutes, so patience is necessary.
