    If you’re looking to bring the fight to your enemies in the best way possible, you’ll need to know which Familiars are going to be the best in Ni No Kuni Cross Worlds. A Familiar is a small creature that can help you out in battle in a plethora of different ways, from magic attacks to getting up close and personal with big attacks.

    We’ll go through and name off the most powerful Familiars in the game, alongside the type of damage they can inflict, and their overall capture power. Let’s see which ones you need to get onto your team as quickly as possible!

    Best Familiars In Ni No Kuni Cross Worlds – Get These Familiars Quickly

    You’ll want to start drawing for these familiars as soon as possible, so you can make the strongest team possible to take down your enemies with ease:

    Bringing any of these powerful Familiars to your next fight will make sure that you come out on top every time. If you’re taking complete control of your movements and battles, you’ll be able to coordinate your attacks even better with these monsters to ensure that you’re causing the most damage possible. Since Ni No Kuni Cross Worlds works on a gatcha system to get these characters, it’s a bit of luck to get one of them.

    • 4-Star Familiars – 4% Summon Rate
    • 3-Star Familiars – 36% Summon Rate
    • 2-Star Familiars – 60% Summon Rate

    If you’re having a hard time with the battle system, and don’t want to invest in different Familiars, you can always invite your friends to join you on your globe-trotting adventure. If you have friends across the world, you’ll want to make sure that you take advantage of this workaround for the lack of cross-region gameplay. If you find that you’re needing some more items, we’ve also got a handy list of available codes for the game, too.

    If you do decide to start purchasing these monsters, you’ll be able to carry them between any system that you play on, as the game does support cross-save. This goes for all of your items, your mounts, and everything in between, so you’ll be able to display your pre-registration bonuses on PC or mobile.

    Ni No Kuni Cross Worlds is available now on mobile devices, and PC.
