    Genshin Impact‘s version 2.1 brought, as part of the conclusion of its Inazuma arc, a new and epic weekly boss fight where Travelers face no one other than Rosalyne-Kruzchka Lohefalter, also known as La Signora, The 8th of the Eleven Fatui Harbingers. With that said, we will now tell you how to defeat La Signora and complete the Narukami Island: Tenshukaku domain.

    Recommended Characters / Teams to defeat La Signora

    The boss fight is divided into two distinct phases, the first one in which La Signora makes use of Cryo and the second one in which she reveals her true power as the Crimson Witch of Embers. For that, it is advised that you take at least one character capable of dealing high damage to her on both stages of the fight, like Xiao or the Electro Shogun. Making use of both a Cryo/Hydro and a Pyro DPS can work even better.

    It is also key that you bring potent healers, such as Bennett, Qiqi, Diona, Jean, and Barbara, even if she is not the best for it thanks to La Signora’s Cryo phase. Remember, your biggest priority needs to be keeping your DPS healthy and ready to deal massive damage. If you have Bennett’s sixth constellation, you can turn any DPS’ into a Pyro one (kinda) which is a plus. Using supports capable of generating shields, like Zhongli, is also advised in most cases.

    How to Defeat La Signora and Complete the Narukami Island: Tenshukaku Domain

    To be able to win the fight, it is necessary that you know how to place yourself and how to use the environment to your advantage. With that, focus on making frequent use of both the Eyes of Frost and the Hearts of Flame placed in the four corners of the boss arena so that you can counter the frequent elemental damage. During the first stage of the fight, stay mobile to dodge the spikes and the projectiles thrown by La Signora. After most of her health is depleted, she will conjure a giant ice cocoon. To destroy it, make use of Pyro and of attacks imbued with Crimson Lotus Moths, which will spawn around the room to accelerate the process. This is also a great time to heal and prepare for the second stage.

    In the second stage of the fight, La Signora will reveal her true identity as the Crimson Witch of Embers. To defeat her, make use of Elemental Reactions to increase your damage. It is vital that you keep your DPS healthy, for that, when it is time to destroy the Eyes of Frost make sure to change characters. Other than that, focus on dealing frequent damage to her while keeping your DPS alive.

    Genshin Impact is currently available for PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, mobile devices, and PC.
