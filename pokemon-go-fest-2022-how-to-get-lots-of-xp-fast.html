    If you’re looking for a fast way to get your Trainer Level up during Pokemon GO Fest 2022, you’ve come to the right spot. We’ll walk you through all of the best ways available to start earning xp fast, so you’ll be able to level up faster than ever before. While there is not any XP Boosts taking effect, there are still plenty of great ways to earn lots of XP during the event, so let’s get right into different ways to do so!

    How To Earn XP Fast During Pokemon GO Fest 2022

    Invest In The Event Box

    You’ll be able to start things off right with the Event Box, which grants you 1 Star Piece, 1 Poffin, and 1 Lucky Egg for the low price of  85 Pokecoins, or less than $1USD. If you’re looking to boost your XP to the max, you’ll want to utilize the Lucky Egg to its full potential, as it helps you double the current XP that you’re earning. If you need to invest in bulk, you’ll be able to get 8 Lucky Eggs for $5USD. If you’ve already put down the cash for GO Fest Tickets, what’re a few extra dollars?

    Hatch Eggs While Walking

    If you’ve been saving your incubators for a rainy day, now is the best time to start putting them to use. Since there are many different types of eggs, you’ll be able to determine which kind of incubator to use, so you can make sure that you’ve got a surefire way to start earning extra candies for specific Pokemon, as well as large amounts of XP, depending on the distance it takes for you to walk and hatch them.

    Practice Your Throws

    If you’re looking to catch as many Pokemon as possible, you’ll want to make sure that you’ve gotten the idea of tosses down pat. If you’ve been practicing not only your curveballs but also the timing of your throws. The better the toss, the more XP you’ll earn, so get out there and start practicing your throws to make sure you can keep hitting great or excellent to earn as much XP as possible! It may sound silly recommending this, as well, but catching as many Pokemon as possible is a great way to keep earning XP through the day, and pairing this option alongside a Lucky Egg will make sure that you’re continuing to grow your trainer level quickly.

    Take Part In Raids, Alone Or With Friends

    If you’re looking for a great way to showcase the power of your Pokemon, you can always take part in different types of Raids that will be happening during the event. You’ll want to make sure that you’ve amassed quite a few different items that can help heal your partners back up to full health, such as revives and potions, so visit as many PokeStops as possible before the event and also consider upgrading your item bag storage a few times before the event, as well. Once you have prepared, get involved in different raids, so you can start racking up massive XP, and maybe make a few new friends along the way.

    Those are a few great ways to make sure that you’re on the right path towards leveling up during GO Fest 2022! Make sure that you’re keeping yourself up to date with all things Pokemon GO related, especially with GO Fest right around the corner. If you’re looking to get into the excitement, you still have time to get your hands on a ticket, where you’ll have enhanced chances to start catching shiny Pokemon. You’ll also have a great opportunity that free users may miss out on, with the chance to catch a Shaymin!

    Pokemon GO is available now on mobile devices.
