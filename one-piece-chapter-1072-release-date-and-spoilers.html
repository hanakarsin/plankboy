    One Piece Chapter 1072 Spoilers

    We were just given three teasers in the form of cryptic tweets by redon. The first tweet is a gif of the cover for Star Wars: Episode II – Attack of the Clone. Fans are still trying to figure out what this means exactly, but they have decided it will most likely deal with the Seraphim or one of the Vegapunk clones. A third fan theory is that Zoro is a clone of Ryuma that Vegapunk created, which will be confirmed in the next chapter. What do you think?     We were finally given the One Piece chapter 1072 spoiler! All three of the cryptic clues make sense now. Here is everything they were alluding to:

    • The queen, judge, and Caesar are creating weapons on the cover.
    • Flashback: Vegapunk explains that Kuma’s power turns intangible things into tangible things, as he did with Luffy’s “Pain” in thriller bark.
    • Kaku uses his awakened form against Zoro, although Zoro defeats him.
    • It is revealed that Stussy is a clone of a former member of the rock pirates.
    • No breaks next week
