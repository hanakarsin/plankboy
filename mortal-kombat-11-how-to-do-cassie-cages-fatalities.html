    Cassie Cage was introduced to the series back in Mortal Kombat X as the daughter of Sonya Blade and Johnny Cage. With her being one of the main characters of that game, there was little doubt we’d see her return in Mortal Kombat 11, in which she in fact did. She had one of the funniest Fatalities in Mortal Kombat X, so there was little doubt she’d keep that going in the followup. This guide will detail how you can pull off Cassie Cage’s Fatalities in Mortal Kombat 11.

    How To Do Cassie Cage’s Fatalities In Mortal Kombat 11

    Being the daughter of both Sonya Blade and Johnny Cage, Cassie features a moveset that mixes both along with some unique moves as well. For the purposes of this guide, the following Fatality inputs are based on the Xbox One version of the game.

    Cassie Cage’s first Fatality is known as I <3 U and is pulled off from Mid distance by pressing Down, Down, Forward, B. This has Cassie use her dad’s patented Shadow Kick right into the chest of the enemy, knocking their heart right out of the chest cavity. She then goes up and puts her hand where the heart would be and makes a heart with her hands that are covered with blood.

    Her second Fatality is known as #GirlPower and is done from Mid distance by pressing Back, Down, Down, Back, Y, which has Cassie running towards her opponent by firing her two handguns at their torso before kicking them in the crotch and sending their spine and skull straight out of their body and into the air.
