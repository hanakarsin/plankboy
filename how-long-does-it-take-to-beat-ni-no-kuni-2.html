    How Long Does it Take to Beat Ni No Kuni 2

    Ni No Kuni 2: Revenant Kingdom takes approximately 40 hours to beat. There are nine chapters in total, and to complete all nine chapters players will need to unite the lands of the world throughout the course of the game, while building the Kingdom of Evermore.

    09 Feb 2024, 00:00

    Ni No Kuni 2: Revenant Kingdom takes approximately 40 hours to beat.  There are nine chapters in total, and to complete all nine chapters players will need to unite the lands of the world throughout the course of the game, while building the Kingdom of Evermore.

    Before the game allows you to take on the final battle and finish the game from a story perspective, players must complete all of the story missions, while also progressing the Kingdom of Evermore to Level 3.  To reach a Level 3 Kingdom players must meet certain requirements in the game.  This includes recruiting at least 50 citizens to the Kingdom of Evermore.

    For more details on how to recruit citizens to Evermore, head to this guide for Ni No Kuni 2.

    This 40 hour run time takes into consideration an approximation of the amount of time it will take to do just the core story missions and make enough progression to unlock the final chapter.  Of course, this can be quite more if players choose to do more of the side missions, kingdom building, and other activities found within the game.

    For more questions answered about Ni No Kuni 2: Revenant Kingdom, head over to our Royal Handbook Wiki page for help in recruiting citizens, boss fight walkthroughs, and other tips for the game.
