    One of the multiple endings in Freddy Fazbear’s Pizzeria Simulator is the Lorekeeper Ending, which can be achieved by revealing all of the lore bits hidden in some of the game’s minigames, as well as salvaging all salvageable animatronics. Specifically, the items that you will want to buy and place in your restaurant to get this ending are Fruity Maze Arcade, Midnight Motorist and Security Puppet.

    Fruity Maze Arcade

    To find the lore in Fruity Maze Arcade, you need to beat the minigame twice and then play it a third time. To beat the game, you will need to collect all the fruits before time runs out and without going out of bounds. After you have beaten the game, play it a second time. This time, many of the fruits will be replaced with yellow animals (it is under contention whether these animals are cats or dogs or rabbits or something else). Regardless, collect all the fruits and animals to beat the game again. Now play the game a third time. It doesn’t matter if you beat the game or not this time; just playing it again will unlock the bit of lore you need.

    Midnight Motorist

    To find the lore in this game, you’ll want to keep to the bottom of the screen during the game. On the fourth lap, there will be a hole at the bottom of the screen you’ll want to go through. Do this, and you’ll be taken to another minigame. In this one, you pretty much drive forward along a road. Eventually, you’ll come to a fork in the road. You can go left to explore that area, but ultimately you’ll want to head downward. You’ll eventually come across a house. Go up to and enter the house. You’ll want to go as deep into the house as you can and interact with the door that you’ll find. You’ll be prompted to back and forth between the door and the hallway a couple of times before being prompted to go out to the back of the house. Once there, you’ll find a broken window with footsteps heading upwards. Follow the footsteps to finish this bit of lore.

    Security Puppet

    Aside from the Security Puppet being so expensive, the minigame you can play from it is probably the easiest in Freddy Fazbear’s Pizzeria Simulator. Upon playing the game, you’ll be asked to block the green child from exiting the building. However, the first three times that you play the game, the green child won’t appear, so you’ll win the game by just standing there. Upon playing the game for the fourth time, you’ll see that the green child is already outside the building. You will then gain control of the Puppet. Exit the building and continue right until you find the child.

    Lorekeeper Ending

    At first, it will seem like the Lorekeeper Ending in Freddy Fazbear’s Pizzeria Simulator is the same as the Good Ending, which you get by salvaging all the animatronics that you have the option to salvage. However, after the credits, you will be treated to an image of a graveyard with six graves. Four of the names on these graves can be made out as “Gabriel,” “Fritz,” “Susie” and “Jeremy.” Upon completion, you will receive the Lorekeeper Certificate, as well as the Certificate of Completion, which you get for completing the Good Ending, if you don’t already have it.
