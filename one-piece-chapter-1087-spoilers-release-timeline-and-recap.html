    One Piece Chapter 1086 Recap: Introduction to the Five Elders

    Chapter 1086 of One Piece is titled “Five Elders.” The chapter begins with a narrator introducing significant names, including the remaining members of the Five Elders, who each hold the title of Warrior God. Dragon expresses disbelief at Vegapunk’s invention of a weapon that destroyed Lulusia, which has never been used before. Ivankov speculates that Imu may possess immortality due to Law’s fruit, Ope Ope no Mi, which belonged to an old user. The chapter features appearances by S-Gecko, S-Doffy, and S-Crocodile, as well as Big News Morgans and Vivi. Morgans makes an appearance in the final page, and the narrator hints that he is preparing for something that will shake the world.

    One Piece Chapter 1087 Spoilers

    One Piece Chapter 1087 spoilers have been released:

    • Chapter 1087: “Battleship Bag”
    • 15-Page Chapter
    • Reader Request Cover: Ace uses his fire to heat water for Capybara bath.
    • The chapter starts at Marine Base G-1. Brannew is showing Jango and Fullbody the ruins of two huge battleships. Brannew explains they are “battleship bags,” like traditional punching bags, but actual ships. Garp uses them to practice his punch.
    • Brannew: “And there are two ships because one of them was used by Aokiji. Garp and Aokiji had a special rule: They never used Haki or special abilities when punching these ships.”
    • Cut to Hachinosu (the “Pirate Island”), Garp is still fighting Blackbeard Pirates. Garp has thrown San Juan Wolf into the sea (we can’t see how he did it, only that he is already in the ocean). Blackbeard’s men try to help Wolf since he will die from drowning.
    • Vasco Shot spits fire at Garp using an attack called “Heavy Drinker: Furnace Flame”. Garp blocks the attack using Blackbear Pirates’ beaten bodies as a shield, and then he throws the burning bodies around, which burns the town. Garp calls this technique “Pirate Fireballs”.
    • We then see that the ship carrying word’s members and prisoners are already off the coast. Only Garp, Koby, and Grus are fighting the entire island by themselves. Grus asks something to Garp, but Garp doesn’t appear to hear it.
    • Grus: “Garp-san… Am I the “future of Marine” too?”
    • Kuzan gets back up after Garps’ Blue Hole” (the same attack from chapter 1081). Garp tells Koby and Grus to run while he stops the entire pirate army by himself.
    • Koby sees a woman that a pirate is attacking. When he tries to help her, Koby discovers they have tricked him because the woman is a pirate too. Shiryu (in invisible form) appears and is about to stab Koby, but Garp blocks the blade and gets stabbed in his body instead (similar to what happened with Whitebeard in Marineford).
    • Garp grabs Shiryu and throws him to the ground. Shiryu is injured but laughing. Koby says sorry to Garp, but Garp says Shiryu’s aim was to get him from the beginning.
    • Garp is weakened, so all Blackbeard Pirates continue to attack him. According to Cross Guild, Garp’s bounty is worth 3 Crowns (3,000 million Berries, which is the same about as Marine Admirals.
    • A new flashback starts, we see the first time Garp met Kuzan. The chapter doesn’t say when this takes place, but Garp looks very young (like long before Roger’s Execution).
    • Young Kuzan asks Garp to be his disciple, but Garp told Kuzan to go and train with the Marine Instructors instead (maybe Garp talked about Zephyr?). Kuzan refused and tried to imitate Garp by punching the battleships like him. As days gone by Kuzan’s punch became stronger and stronger, until the sound of his punch was as loud as Garp’s.
    • Over time, Garp gets closer to Kuzan, even complaining about his family to Kuzan while they were at snacks together.
    • Garp: “My son became a revolutionary, dammit! And my grandson said he will become a pirate! Like I’d let him!”
    • Kuzan gives Garp an annoyed look.
    • Kuzan: “What does that have to do with me?!”
    • Back to the present, we see Kuzan covering his hand with an “Ice Glove” technique. Then Garp and Kuzan punch each other’s faces with haki, creating a massive explosion (we can see black lightning from the impact). Both of them are thrown away.
    • Avalo Pizarro brings two huge arms from the island with his powers and is about to crush Garp’s ship.
    • At the end of the chapter, we see Garp lying on the ground bleeding (we don’t see what happens to Kuzan).
    • Garp says to Koby: “Koby, don’t panic… Justice will prevail!”
    • End of the Chapter
    • Next week there will be a Color Spread for One Piece Chapter 1088 to celebrate the 26th anniversary of One Piece!
