    How to Redeem Codes in Zone Rush 

    To redeem codes in Zone Rush, follow the steps given below. 

  • Launch Zone Rush on Roblox. 
  • Click on Rewards on the upper left side of the screen. 
  • Next, click on the Codes button. 
  • Type in your working code in the Enter Code Here textbox.
