    Blight Survival: Release Date, Platforms, Trailer, and Everything We Know

    According to the game’s description on the official Steam store page, Blight Survival is a 4-player co-op horror rogue-lite set in a chaotic no man’s land between two states in the middle of a war that does not seem to stop. Besides the gritty and captivating setting, Blight Survival will offer players a large selection of weapons and armor and a huge map filled with secrets and currency, all with the help of up to three players.

    Blight Survival Release Date

    Right Now, Blight Survival does not have a release date. IGN’s trailer does not show anything regarding a release date, and neither does the game’s Steam page nor Discord server.

    What Platforms is Blight Survival On?

    According to the game’s Discord server, the developers will focus on PC for now, although Haenir Studio has expressed some interest in expanding its reach to the next-gen console market.

    The developers are designing the combat system as a hybrid between Dark Souls and Mordhau, which is evident upon watching the latest gameplay reveal trailer.

    Customization in Blight Survival

    The game will also feature customizable characters and weapons that will affect both the visuals and gameplay.

    Even though the game’s Steam description only mentions co-op gameplay in Blight Survival’s Discord server, the developers hint at a potential invasion mode. Allowing players to engage in Player versus Player combat.

    Right now, there is not a lot of information about Blight: Survival, but this recent gameplay trailer has sparked gamers’ interest, and at the time of writing this article, the gameplay reveal trailer has almost one million views on IGN’s Youtube channel.

    Blight Survival does not have a release date. For more information about the game, go to the official Blight Survival Twitter profile.
