    Minecraft is always thriving with players venturing into the worlds it offers time and time again. Of course, many players opt to spend a lot of their time within the game on various player-created servers that tailor to specific interests of the community. Due to the sheer number of servers that are on offer; it can be daunting at first to work out what server to start with and indeed what servers are actually the great ones to try. This article will list some of the best Minecraft servers that you can experience at the moment.

    Best Minecraft Servers to Play

    It’s time to delve into the beating heart of Minecraft servers and even try new servers that you may not have played before. All of these picks will certainly be worth your time and they are as follows:

    • Hypixel
    • Mineplex
    • PirateCraft
    • CubeCraft
    • Fallen Kingdom

    Keep reading onwards to find out more about each of the servers listed.

    Hypixel

    Hypixel is one of the most popular Minecraft servers and with its expansive player base, there are many modes for you to play through. Whether you want PVP or PVE, this server will have everything that you need to indulge yourself with the diverse array of common Minecraft modes that players like to enjoy. From Skyblock to building minigames, you can immerse yourself in the thriving atmosphere of one of the largest Minecraft servers there is.

    Mineplex

    Up next is Mineplex and if you merge an MMO and Minecraft together, this server is surely what you would get. Thousands of players can be in the world at once for this server and there is a myriad of modes and things to take part in throughout the lands. There is even what can be seen as a recreation of Mario Kart in Minecraft so whether you’re wanting to simply explore the world or shred up some tracks. This will certainly offer you a lot of opportunities.

    With all of the frequent updates that Minecraft gets, the server will be sure to grow with new players for a long time yet.

    PirateCraft

    Do you like the open seas? Do you like pirate ships? If you answered yes to any of those questions you will no doubt enjoy the PirateCraft server which will offer you a chance to create ships, sail, and even take part in combat with other players through the ships. Of course, you can explore islands on land too so if you’ve been wanting to live out a pirate’s life within Minecraft then this server will allow you to do just that.

    CubeCraft

    CubeCraft has a range of modes on offer similar to Hypixel, however, CubeCraft tends to be well-known for its great parkour maps that you can work your way through. If you’re looking for a server that has a cozy atmosphere with meticulously crafted modes and experiences, then this server will be great to experience. Have a go at the parkour maps and enjoy everything that it has to offer.

    Fallen Kingdom

    Fallen Kingdom will take you back to medieval times due to this server-based around conquering areas in the world and you can of course work together with other players. You can be a King or a Queen and lead many adventures with others through the lands. Stunning castles await to be observed and it is your time to thrive in this world. All of these servers can offer you the same enjoyment as mods can.

    Now that you know more about all of the key servers listed, you can dive into Minecraft today and try them for yourself if you like the sound of any of the Minecraft servers listed in this guide.

    Minecraft is available now on PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, PC, mobile devices, and many other platforms.
