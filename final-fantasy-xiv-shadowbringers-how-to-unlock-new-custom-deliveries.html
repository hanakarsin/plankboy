    Final Fantasy XIV: Shadowbringers How to Unlock New Custom Deliveries

    Its not exactly a secret that custom deliveries are a great way to level and gain scrips for gatherers and crafters in Final Fantasy XIV. With patch 5.1, were finally getting a new person to assist in Shadowbringers.

    02 May 2024, 00:00

    It’s not exactly a secret that custom deliveries are a great way to level and gain scrips for gatherers and crafters in Final Fantasy XIV. With patch 5.1, we’re finally getting a new person to assist in Shadowbringers.

    Make sure you’ve completed the new main scenario quests, and the previous scrip side quests.

    You can’t access the new custom deliveries from Kai-Shirr until you’ve unlocked the Boutique of Splendors proper. You can start this process after completing the new main scenario quests, and have completed the level 50 quest Inscrutable Tastes in Ishgard. If you’ve done that and the level 60 follow-up quest Go West, Craftsman in Mor Dhona, you’ll unlock the side quest The Boutique Always Wins in Eulmore. Complete this quest to unlock the scrip exchange in Eulmore, and access to the side quest Oh, Beehive Yourself.

    Talk to Kai-Shirr to unlock his custom deliveries.

    Once you have the scrip exchange in Eulmore unlocked, talk to Kai-Shirr to get the ball rolling. He’ll give you the quest Oh, Beehive Yourself (Koji still can’t resist placing puns in quest names). Complete it to access Kai-Shirr’s custom deliveries. These work exactly like previous custom deliveries, with you being sent out to gather, craft, or fish up a required item and return it to him. The more of his deliveries you complete, the higher his satisfaction will rise. You have a max of 12 deliveries you can do a week, so remember that.

    At satisfaction levels 4 and 5 you’ll unlock additional quests to perform, but it’s nothing you haven’t seen before.

    That’s how to unlock the new custom deliveries in Final Fantasy XIV: Shadowbringers.
