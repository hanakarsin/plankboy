    Tover Tokens are littered through the many POIs in Fortnite Chapter 3 Season 3 including Logjam Lumberyard. In order to unlock all of the parts to the first-ever customizable skin in Fortnite named Snap, you’ll need to collect all of the Tover Tokens throughout the map. Here are all of the Tover Token locations in Logjam Lumberyard.

    Tover Token Locations in Logjam Lumberyard

    Like Condo Canyon or Sleepy Sound, there are 3 Tover Tokens in Logjam Lumberyard. As you are completing quests and working you way up the Battle Pass to unlock Snap and eventually Darth Vader, you’ll benefit from some help. Whether you are looking for all of the Tover Tokens in each area or how to use the new vehicles and gadgets, we’ve got it covered in our Fortnite guides.

    Logjam Lumberyard Tover Token #1

    The first Tover Token in Logjam Lumberyard is an easy one because it is directly in the middle of the area. In the dead center of Logjam Lumberyard, outside next to a curved wooden fence, is where you’ll find the first Tover Token.

    Logjam Lumberyard Tover Token #2

    The second Tover Token in Logjam Lumberyard is on the dock just outside of the small boathouse. It is sitting at the edge of the dock right next to the water where a motorboat should be parked.

    Logjam Lumberyard Tover Token #3

    The last Tover Token in Logjam Lumberyard in Fortnite is on the east side of the entire area. Once you see the wooden stairs leading up, you’ve gone too far. The third Tover Token is behind one of the piles of logs. Once you’ve grabbed it, you’ll have collected all of the Tover Tokens in Logjam Lumberyard. Good luck out there!

    Fortnite is available now on PC, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X|S, Nintendo Switch, and mobile devices.
