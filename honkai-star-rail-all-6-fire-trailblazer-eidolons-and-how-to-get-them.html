    The Trailblazer is the main protagonist of Honkai: Star Rail, and while they begin as a Physical damage attacker on the Path of Destruction, they’ll eventually unlock a new form that places them on the Path of Preservation and enhances their attacks with Fire damage. This form is unlocked after completing the Belobog storyline, the first major arc of the game’s story, and the Fire Trailblazer is one of the best free-to-play tanks in the game outside of March 7th. Unlocking their Eidolons can be a bit confusing though, so here’s how you can get all 6 of them and get the Preservation Trailblazer to E6.

    Recommended Videos

    How to Get Fire Traiblazer Eidolons in Honkai Star Rail

    The good news is that activating all 6 Eidolons is straightforward — although it will take some time to complete. You’ll need a Shadow of Preservation to unlock a new Eidolon level for the Fire Trailblazer, and these can be obtained via main story progression or by purchasing them.

    Related: How to Link Twitch Account to Honkai Star Rail

    How to Get Fire Trailblazer Eidolons from Quests

    Two Shadows of Preservation can be obtained for free by completing Trailblaze Missions. The first free Shadow of Preservation is rewarded for completing the Level 34 Trailblaze Mission “Humming Antlers, Entwined Horns,” which is the final quest of Honkai Star Rail version 1.0. The second free Shadow of Preservation is unlocked by completing the Trailblaze Mission “A Dragon Gallant, Its Ocean Distant.” This is the final quest of the Xianzhou Luofu storyline and the final quest of Honkai Star Rail version 1.2.

    Where to Buy Shadows of Preservation

    The remaining 4 Shadows of Preservation can be purchased with Strale, the Xianzhou Luofu regional currency, at the Jeweler’s Pagoda store in the Central Starskiff Haven part of the Xianzhou Luofu. Each one will set you back 250 Strale, so you’ll need to save up a whopping 1000 Strale if you want all of them. To get more Strale, simply complete activities in the Xianzhou Luofu region.

    All Fire Trailblazer Eidolon Effects in Honkai: Star Rail

    Below you can find what each level of Eidolon does for your Preservation Trailblazer — making them stronger than ever before. I recommend taking good note of all of these because they really can enhance your overall combat efficiency of the Fire Trailblazer by a lot.

    Earth-Shaking Resonance

    When using the Fire Trailblazer’s basic attack, they will deal additional damage that equals 25% of their defense level. When using the enhanced basic attack, that percentage increases to 50.

    Time-Defying Tenacity

    Shield applied to all allies from the Treasure of the Architect will block additional damage equal to two percent of the Fire Trailblazers’ defense plus 27.

    Trail-Blazing Blueprint

    Skill level gains plus 2, with a maximum of 15. The same buff applies to the Talent.

    Nation-Building Oath

    Fire Trailblazer gains four stacks of Magma Will at the beginning of the battle — dealing additional damage.

    Spirit-Warming Flame

    Fire Tralblazer’s Ultimate level is increased by two, with a maximum of 15. Also, the Basic Attack level is increased by one, with a maximum of 10.

    City-Forging Bulwarks

    Fire Trailblazer’s defense increases by 10 percent whenever an enhanced Basic Attack or Ultimate is used.

    Now that you know about all of the Fire Trailblazer Eidolons, you can make your way back into the game and begin gathering the Shadow of Preservation materials for the Eidolon level upgrades — time to get busy!
