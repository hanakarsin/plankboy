    Darkest Dungeon II has launched into early access earlier this week and players have been eagerly playing through the experience with a sense of splendor as they delve into the enrapturing world that the series offers. While venturing through the landscape, you will undoubtedly pick up items, trinkets, and other rewards along the way. You may be wondering how to sell items in Darkest Dungeon 2 and if it’s actually possible. This guide article will take you over everything you need to know about the topic.

    Can I Sell Items in Darkest Dungeon 2?

    It is currently not possible to sell items in Darkest Dungeon 2, this means that for every trinket and item you pick up, there is no way to sell it. There’s even no option to sell them at ‘the Hoarder’ and you can’t, unfortunately, store any items at the Inn either so you’ll always be walking with the shiny trinkets and items you have discovered along the way of your playthrough.

    This, therefore, means that your character will be hauling around the memories of every journey you’ve ever gone on in Darkest Dungeon 2, quite the adventure indeed!

    Will I ever be able to sell items in Darkest Dungeon 2?

    Given that the game is still in early access there is a significant likelihood that many features are still to be implemented and added in when player feedback reaches the development team. In turn, there may be things such as a trading system added into the experience in the future and a selling system that can be added for NPC’s.

    This would assist players who enjoy managing their trinkets and items to the largest degree in Role-playing games.

    Darkest Dungeon II is available in early access now on PC with a release date still to be revealed for the main launch of the experience.
