    Stash boxes can come in handy when you’re carrying a lot of items and need to unload some of them.  Stash boxes allow you to remove any heavy inventory and store it in a safe place, which you can access later on.  The stash is good place to put your junk or your crafting materials for later use.  The problem is that Stash Boxes in Fallout 76 are found all over the world and they can be created at your camp.  Stash Box locations are a good base area to explore from because you won’t ever be too far away to store the items that you find.
