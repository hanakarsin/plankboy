    Are you wondering what the best Black Pearl Cookie toppings are in the Cookie Run Kingdom? The Legend of the Duskgloom Sea update just debuted, bringing to the game the debut of new mechanics and challenges, as well as that of both Legendary Black Pearl Cookie and Epic Captain Caviar Cookie. But what are the best toppings for Black Pearl Cookie? Now, to answer that and more, here are the best toppings for Black Pearl Cookie.

    Best Black Pearl Toppings Build in Cookie Run Kingdom

    As Black Pearl Cookie’s biggest damage lies in using her Duskgloom’s Sovereign skill, which allows her to take her true form and then deal massive elemental (lighting) damage to all enemies while also afflicting them with Terror of the Abyss. There is a possibility of three different types of toppings you can use with Black Pearl:

    • x5 Searing Raspberry: Attack Increase
    • x5 Solid Almond: Damage Resistance
    • x5 Swift Chocolate: Cooldown Reduction

    The 5-piece Searing Raspberry topping is our main choice, as equipping it will greatly increase ATK. Given the nature of her set, using a 5-piece Swift Chocolate can also work out great, as it will allow you to considerably lower her 15 seconds skill cooldown. Equipping her with a 3-piece Searing Raspberry + 2 Solid Almond can also work well if you need either more damage (situational) or more resistance.

    When using this build, we recommend using treasures focused on increasing your team’s overall ATK, such as Old Pilgrim’s Scroll, and those focused on decreasing cooldown, like Squishy Jelly Watch. Using treasures focused on debuffing targets can also work well when using Black Pearl Cookie against bosses.

    • It’s important to point out that enemies affected by Black Pearl Cookie’s skill will also have their healing decreased by 35%, while those affected by Terror of the Abyss will have their ATK speed/movement speed decreased by 20% and their ATK decreased by 15.5% for 15 seconds.

    Now that you know the best toppings for Black Pearl Cookie don’t forget to check out the best toppings for Cotton Cookie and Pomegranate Cookie, as well as how to defeat Pitaya and complete Cookie Run Kingdom’s 12th chapter.
