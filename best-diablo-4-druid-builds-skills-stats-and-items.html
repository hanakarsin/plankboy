We now have access to the Druid class, which is expected to bring a lot of fun builds based on its different animal forms. The Druid specializes in shapeshifting as well as Earth and Storm Magic.

    Best Diablo 4 Druid Build

    This melee shapeshifting build uses Claw, Shred, and Companions to improve your Critical Strike Chance, increase your Attack Speed, and provide Poison utility. The Lacerate Ultimate ability will allow you to one-shot bosses if done correctly. This build will allow you to hit level 25 very quickly in the beta. Afterward, you can adjust this build to hit the max level and begin farming items.

    Best Diablo 4 Druid Stats

    The three most important stats for the Druid are as follows:

    • Strength: +1 Armor per point
    • Intelligence: +0.05% All Resistance per point
    • Willpower: +0.1% Healing Received and +0.25% Overpower Damage per point
    • Dexterity: +0.025% Dodge Chance per point

    Best Diablo 4 Paragons for Druid Class

    Once you reach level 50 with your Druid, the Paragon Board will unlock. The Paragon Board is essentially the endgame skill tree that is more in-depth and offers tremendous customization for your build. Instead of gaining a skill point when leveling up — players earn a skill point for each quarter of a level, adding up to four skill points per level. There are a few Paragons that players should focus on for the Druid build. Below you will find the recommended Paragons and their in-game descriptions.

    • Constructing Tendrils – Lucky Hit: Nature Magic Skills have up to a 15% chance to entangle the enemy with vines, immobilizing them for 2 seconds and Poisoning them for 120% of the base damage dealt over 4 seconds.
    • Inner Beast – After shapeshifting, your Spirit costs are reduced by 10% for 5 seconds, up to 30%
    • Ancestral Guidance – After spending 75 Spirit, you deal 30% increased damage for 5 seconds.

    These three Paragons are some of the best on the Druid Paragon Board. Then again, there are so many options that you might find one that fits your playstyle more.
