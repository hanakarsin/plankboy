    Mortal Kombat 11 offers an incredibly fun story mode as we’ve always come to expect from the series, especially in recent years. That is not the only place to find story content in the game though, with each character having their own distinct endings available in the game as well. This guide will explain how you can unlock these different character endings.

    How To Unlock Character Endings In Mortal Kombat 11

    Every Mortal Kombat game dating back to the original has some equivalent of arcade ladders for you to play through and beat in single player. Mortal Kombat 11 is no different with the introduction of Klassic Towers into the mix.

    The Towers of Time modes are online based and change over time to earn different items, but Klassic Towers are the ones you want. These comes in five different forms, three normal ones with difficulty based on how many enemies you have to face known as Novice, Warrior, and Champion, as well as an Endless and Survivor tower.

    To try and unlock character endings in Mortal Kombat 11, your best and quickest bet is to take on the Novice Klassic Tower. This is because you only have to face five enemies total including Kronika at the very end. Once you defeat Kronika, you will get the character ending for the character you are currently fighting with.

    You can go with the more time consuming options if you so choose and you will also unlock that character’s ending, but Novice is the way to go if you’re trying to unlock everybody’s ending as fast as possible.

    Once you have unlocked a character ending, you do not have to play through their Klassic Tower again just to view it a second time. Once you view it once, you can access it again from the Kollection section of the game at anytime.
