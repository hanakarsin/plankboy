    Deathloop Gold Weapons Locations &amp; List Where to Find Gold Weapons

    There are a handful of gold weapons in Deathloop. These weapons have the highest rarity level associated with them and can only be found on certain levels at certain times. These weapons have unique random weapon perks. In this guide well explain how to find gold guns in Deathloop.

    12 Jun 2024, 00:00

    There are a handful of gold weapons in Deathloop.  These weapons have the highest rarity level associated with them and can only be found on certain levels at certain times.  These weapons have unique random weapon perks.  In this guide we’ll explain how to find gold guns in Deathloop.

    Gold Weapon Locations Deathloop

    Gold weapons are extremely rare in Deathloop.  You might run into one or two when playing the game without knowing where to look, because you have to both go to the correct location and go there at the correct time.  Below you’ll find the gold weapons list in Deathloop and the times you can find them.

    Constancy Automatic

    • The Constancy Automatic has two clips which both hold bullets.  Because of that you can fire and reload the gun at the same time.  The only thing that will stop the Constancy Automatic from firing shots is if you run out of bullets.
    • Location – Defeat Frank to get the Constancy Automatic
    • Tier – Gold

    Sepulchra Breteira

    • The Sepulchra Breteira is Deathloop’s sniper rifle.  As you would expect, the Sniper Rifle will allow you to take out enemies at a distance with an adjustable zoom level.
    • Location – Updaam
    • Time – Afternoon
    • Tier – Gold

    Strelak Verso

    • The Strelak Verso is actually two guns that can be dual-wielded or combined together to create a powerful burst firing weapon.
    • Location – Complex
    • Time – Noon
    • Tier – Gold

    Heritage Gun

    • The Heritage Gun has multiple modes.  The first is distance rifle and the second is a shotgun.  Long or short range is the name of the game and Heritage offers the flexibility to be used anywhere.
    • Location – Karl’s Bay
    • Time – Night
    • Tier – Gold

    At that’s all you need to know about getting the gold tier weapons in Deathloop.  When looking to collect these items make sure you head to the locations and the times of day if you want to find them.
