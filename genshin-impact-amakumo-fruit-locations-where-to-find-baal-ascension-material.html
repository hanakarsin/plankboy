    Genshin Impact Amakumo Fruit Locations: Where to Find Baal Ascension Material

    Genshin Impacts version 2.1 Floating World Under the Moonlight is here, and many have already managed to pull for the games new 5-star Electro Polearm user Baal. With that said, we will now tell you where to find Amakumo Fruit, Baals ascension material, in Genshin Impact.

    05 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Genshin Impact‘s version 2.1 “Floating World Under the Moonlight” is here, and many have already managed to pull for the game’s new 5-star Electro Polearm user Baal. With that said, we will now tell you where to find Amakumo Fruit, Baal’s ascension material, in Genshin Impact. 

    Where to Find Amakumo Fruit in Genshin Impact

    To get the ingredient, you first need to unlock the game’s new Serai Island region. You can unlock the new region by taking your Waverider and sailing south of Kannazuka. Once there, you will be able to get a total of 90 units of Amakumo Fruit per run.

    With that out of the way, the biggest concentration of the ingredient can be found all over Amakumo Peak, which is located at the center of the new Island. You can check out the exact location of each item present in the said location in the map below, courtesy of Genshin Impact’s official interactive map:

    Once you are done gathering the ingredients present in Amakumo Peak, you can head over to Fort Hiraumi, where you can find 12 more units of Amakumo Fruit. If you want, you can also head out to Koseki Village, where you can find 2 more. You can check out the exact location of each unit in the map below, courtesy of Genshin Impact’s official interactive map:

    It is important to point out two things, the first is that the Serai Island region is filled with high-level enemies, and the second is to remember to use the portals located all over the newly discovered island to increase the speed of your travels between the gathering regions. You can check out the placement of all the enemies present in Serai Island below, courtesy of Genshin Impact’s official interactive map:

    Remember, preparation is key, so even if you have not managed to get Baal it is vital that you prepare yourself and gather her materials and artifacts beforehand, for doing that will allow you to get her at least to a high level and into your main team composition fast. With that in mind, don’t forget to check out our Baal build guide, as well as our guide on how to get The Catch’s ascension materials.

    Genshin Impact is currently available for PC, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, and mobile devices.
