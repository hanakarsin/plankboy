    If you’re looking to get your hands on some free Pokecoins in the world of Pokemon GO, one of the best ways to do that is by defending a gym, where you can earn up to 50 coins per gym, once your Pokemon are returned to you.

    If you’re looking to keep a gym long enough to start raking in those Pokecoins, you’ll want to make sure that you’ve got a monster that can stand tall and properly defend it, as well as survive an onslaught of attacks from people trying to take it over for their team. Make sure that you’ve got some of these Pokemon on your team if you’re looking to keep your hands on a gym for as long as possible!

    Best Defenders In Pokemon GO

    You’ll want to make sure that you’ve got different monsters with a High Defensive Stat, Great Stamina, as well as a powerful attack to make sure that you’ll be able to survive the oncoming storm of opponents trying to get you out of the gym. Power up any of these in our list to make sure that you’re ready to keep earning Pokecoins quickly and easily.

With Pokemon such as Blissy, you’ll find that their stamina stat makes up for their general lack of power, but pairing it with the right attacker to help you out can ensure a victory is won every time.

    If your Pokemon do succumb to their injuries and fall, you’ll need to make sure that you have plenty of potions, revives, and more. Spinning Pokestops can make sure that you’ve always got the items you need, so having a few different accessories to help you out with that task can help you take your Pokemon GO game to the next level.
