    Farming bosses in Borderlands 3 is probably easier than it’s ever been.  With each boss having the potential to drop unique legendary items to use, it’s going to be worth it farm these bosses.  Whatever boss you are looking to farm the process is relatively the same.  Just follow the steps below to farming any boss in Borderlands 3.

    How to Farm Bosses in Borderlands 3 : Step by Step

  • Beat the Boss the first time and complete the mission
  • Head back to the boss location
  • Beat the boss again
  • Get the Loot
  • Quit out of the game
  • When you restart your character save you will be at the closest save point to the boss
  • Beat the boss again

    • All you need to remember is that you will want to clear any loose ends that you have on the mission before trying to farm.  Whether that’s heading back to Sanctuary to speak with an NPC or something else, you will want to complete the story mission completely.  Once you have you can fight the bosses of Borderlands 3 over and over and they will drop loot and cash each time.
