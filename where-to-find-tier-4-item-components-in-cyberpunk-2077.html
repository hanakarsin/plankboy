    One of the biggest changes the 2.0 update of Cyberpunk 2077 brought to the game was the revamping of its crafting and upgrading system, which now makes use of components divided by tier instead of by rarity. But where can you find Tier 4 Item Components? Here’s where to find and how to farm Tier 4 Item Components in Cyberpunk 2077.

    Recommended Videos

    Where to Find and How to Get Tier 4 Item Components in Cyberpunk 2077

    You will be able to get Tier 4 Item Components in Cyberpunk 2077 by mainly dismantling Items like weapons, clothing, and junk, as well as find them by opening random caches/looting enemy corpses throughout both Night City and the Badlands. Enemies will only start to drop Tier 4 Components as loot after you reach Level 30+.

    Related: How to Parry and Counter with Melee Weapons and Fists in Cyberpunk 2077

    Can You Buy Tier 4 Item Components?

    As Christian Bognar showcased in our How to Get Tier 5 Item Components in Cyberpunk 2077 guide, although CD Projekt Red revealed on the patch notes for version 2.0 that vendors would no longer trade them, it is still possible to purchase components within Night City.

    How to Farm Tier 4 Item Components in Cyberpunk 2077

    The best way to farm Item Components in Cyberpunk 2077 can be done by dismantling both clothes and weapons and then crafting the components from their parts. A Tier 4 component will only cost 5 Tier 3 ones, but you will only be able to craft them after unlocking the Grease Monkey Technical Ability. You will be able to get a massive array of guns and clothes by completing Hideouts, Gigs, and Random Encounters.

    How to Dismantle Items in Cyberpunk 2077

    You can dismantle items in Cyberpunk 2077 by simply heading to your backpack, going to the items you wish to dismantle, and then holding the dismantling key.
