    Darkest Dungeon 2 is here, and many players are already diving into its world, which is filled with danger, blood, and death. In the game, you will take control of a party of heroes as they face the odds and try to work as a unit in their battle against unknown dangers. With that said, and taking into consideration that to be able to pull that off you need to have as much variety as you can on your roster, we will tell you how to unlock every character on Darkest Dungeon 2.

    How to Unlock All Characters in Darkest Dungeon 2

    Currently, the game features 9 distinct Hero classes, all featuring their own weakness as well as their own strengths, on and off the battlefield. You can check out below how to unlock all the characters in Darkest Dungeon 2.

    • Graverobber: Unlocked since the beginning.
    • Man-At-Arms: Unlocked since the beginning.
    • Highwayman: Unlocked since the beginning.
    • Plague Doctor: Unlocked since the beginning.
    • Hellion: Reach profile level 3.
    • Runaway: Reach profile level 6.
    • Jester: Reach profile level 9.
    • Leper: Reach profile level 12.
    • Occultist: Reach profile level 15.

    How to Raise Your Profile Level on Darkest Dungeon 2

    You can level up your Profile Level on Darkest Dungeon 2 by accumulating ”Hope”, a currency that is given to players at the end of each run. Gather enough Hope and you will raise your Profile Level.

    It’s good to remember that, at the time this guide is being written, Darkest Dungeon 2 is on its early access phase, which features, according to its developers, two-thirds of the final game. Also, according to its developers, the game will still receive more playable heroes, enemies, items, as well as four more final bosses. They also revealed, on the game’s FAQ in the Epic Games Store, that it’s in their plans to keep the game in early access for about a year.

    Darkest Dungeon 2 is available right now, exclusively on the Epic Games Store.
