    Karlee doesn’t really associate herself too much with the rest of the Cleaners in Back 4 Blood. Though, by having no real other option to survive the Ridden threat, she has chosen to fight with them. She might be a lone wolf, but definitely has the means of providing huge benefits to her teammates in combat.

    What role does Karlee play in Back 4 Blood?

    Karlee is an intel/recon character. You may wonder why a recon class would be necessary for a game where you essentially just bash through hordes of zombies. However, there are many things to look out for, specifically for players who are a bit too valiant and kill-hungry. If you are good with callouts, scoping the battlefield, and like to play a utility support role, Karlee is your gal.

    Karlee’s abilities

    • Karlee can sense nearby hazards and mutations
    • +1 Quick inventory
    • Team Effect: +50% Team Use Speed

    The best weapons to use for Karlee

    Karlee is best used as a mid to long range Cleaner, so her ideal weapons would be assault rifles and snipers (if you have incredible aim). She starts off with a Tec 9 auto pistol, so make use of that when enemies get too close for comfort. Of course, this is just a recommendation. Karlee doesn’t benefit from using any weapon type in particular, so it is preferential. She just should not be played as a character who charges the front lines.

    The best cards to equip for Karlee

    Since Karlee is a recon class of sorts, you’d want to use cards that would increase her specific stats. Anything that boosts use speeds or scavenging abilities would greatly help her. Pair any of these cards in decks of 15 to make the most out of her.

    Reflex Cards

    • Screwdriver
    • Utility Scavenger
    • Killer’s Instinct
    • Discipline Cards

    Combat Training

    • Inspiring Sacrifice
    • Charitable Soul
    • Hunker Down
    • Avenge the Fallen
    • Trigger Control
    • Down in Front!
    • Group Therapy
    • Medical Professional
    • Ammo Scavenger
    • Support Scavenger
    • Weapon Scavenger
    • Marked for Death
    • Knowledge is Power

    Brawn Cards

    • Overwatch

    • Chemical Courage

    Fortune Cards

    • Copper Scavenger
