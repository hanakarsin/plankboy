    Is Kaveh a 4 or 5-star in Genshin Impact?

    Currently, there’s no way to know for sure whether Kaveh will be a 5 or a 4-star in Genshin Impact, given that the first solid leaks seemingly regarding his kit have just started to surface, and the fact he is rumored to debut on the game’s still a little far away and unnamed version 3.6.

    With that said, given the fact that it has been rumored for a long time that Kaveh will be a 4-star, it is highly possible that he will indeed be one. It is also very likely that, if confirmed to be a 4-star, Kaveh debuts as part of Baizhu‘s highly probable upcoming banner, in a matter similar to how Yaoyao debuted on Alhaitham’s, and Mika is very likely set to be released as part of the Dehya banner on version 3.5.

    It’s also important to point out that the game’s version 3.6 may also be one of if not the last patch to arrive before the arrival of the Hydro region of Fontaine, which is set to feature the debut of a wide array of new characters.

    As we mentioned above, if you still have not met Kaveh in the game, you can do so and thus check out more about his personality and his relationship with the Sumeru cast by completing the main and character quests featured as part of the Sumerru storyline. You can also check out more on him, Cyno, Tighnari, and Alhaitham as they hang out during the Alhaitham character demo.

    Genshin Impact is currently available for PC, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, and mobile devices.
