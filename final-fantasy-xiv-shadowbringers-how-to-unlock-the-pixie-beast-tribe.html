    Final Fantasy XIV: Shadowbringers How to Unlock the Pixie Beast Tribe

    Now that the launch of Shadowbringers has settled down, its time to introduce some expected activities into the game. The beast tribes have always been an important facet of Final Fantasy XIV, so you probably want to know how to unlock the pixie beast tribe.

    28 Feb 2024, 00:00

    Now that the launch of Shadowbringers has settled down, it’s time to introduce some expected activities into the game. The beast tribes have always been an important facet of Final Fantasy XIV, so you probably want to know how to unlock the pixie beast tribe.

    Complete the main scenario quest The Wheel Turns, then return to Il Mheg.

    The pixie beast tribe quests are not too hard to access, seeing as once you beat Titania in the main scenario you can talk to the Pink Pixie in the Crystarium (13.2, 15.3). Talk to the pixie to gain the side quest Manic Pixie Dream Realm. Once this quest is completed you will have access to the new daily pixie beast tribe quests.

    These quests will work like previous beast tribe quests, where you’ll run daily missions to increase your reputation with the pixies. The higher your rep, the more side quests you will have access to. The daily allotment of beast tribe quests is shared among the pixies and other tribes, so be mindful of that if you are looking to catch up with previous beast tribes.

    The vendor for the pixies’ will become available after you complete Manic Pixie Dream Realm, and they can be located in Il Mheg at X:12.4, Y: 32.9, just south of Lydha Lran.

    That’s how to unlock the pixie beast tribe in Final Fantasy XIV: Shadowbringers. Considering they are the most mischievous foe the fae-folk, expect all sorts of tomscrewery to occur as you assist them
