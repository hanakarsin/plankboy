    Remnant 2 shines in its dual Archetype system, allowing players to combine classes for some unstoppable builds. Once this feature becomes available, though, the game only gives you a little information on how to do it. Instead, all the game tells you is that you are required to find an Engram for a second Archetype. If you have been struggling with this, we got you covered. Here is how to find Engrams for second Archetypes in Remnant 2.

    Where to Get Engrams and Unlock a Second Archetype in Remnant 2

    To equip an Engram for a second Archetype, you must reach the max level on your starting Archetype. Remnant 2 Engrams can be purchased by your great friend Wallace in Ward 13. The catch is that these items will only be available to purchase from him if you acquire a different item first. Each Archetype has a unique item you must trade to Wallace for him to turn into an Engram. Below you will find all items for each Archetype and where you can get them.

    • Hunter – Purchase the Rusty Medal from Brabus in Ward 13.
    • Challenger – Purchase the Old Metal from Reggie Malone in Ward 13.
    • Medic – Purchase the Medic Pin from Dr. Norah in Ward 13.
    • Handler – Purchase from Mudtooth in Ward 13.
    • Gunslinger – Becomes available by just progressing through the main story.
    • Engineer – Obtain the Alien Device in the Eon Vault in N’Erud.
    • Alchemist – Obtain the Philosopher’s Stone by defeating Manticora in Losomn.
    • Summoner – Obtain the Faded Grimoire from the Bloodmoon Altar.
    • Explorer – Obtain the Broken Compass by defeating Annihilation at the end of the main story.

    Related: What Does Reroll Campaign do in Remnant 2? Explained

    Once you get the appropriate item, bring it to Wallace in Ward 13. Wallace can be easily found in his little hut by the waterside.

    Next, trade that item to Wallace, who will give you an Engram in return. A heads up, though, the price to purchase an Engram can be steep. For example, adding on the Medic Archetype costs me 1x Medic Pin, 10 Lumenite Crystal, and 1000 Scrap. This will differentiate between Archetypes, so keep that in mind.

    Wallace is a handy vendor in Remnant 2, offering essential items for your journey. For example, he provides Relic upgrades and the Orb of Undoing for resetting all spent Trait Points. He is one of the most critical vendors in the game who can help increase your chances of surviving the brutal world of the game.
