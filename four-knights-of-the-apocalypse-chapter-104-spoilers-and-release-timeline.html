    Four Knights of the Apocalypse Chapter 104 Spoilers

    Four Knights of the Apocalypse Chapter 104 spoilers have been released:

    • Percival’s group has arrived in the Demon realm. The midget suddenly remembers his interaction with Zeldris as a little kid. The Demon king proposes that the kid stays here with him and Gelda (mispronounced as Geldoh and Gehda) until they figure out which hole he came from. Thanks to this, Percival tells the others this thing connects to every place in Britannia, and that surprises them.
    • Suddenly a wind blows (a mechanism to prevent the holes from clogging), sending all of them out in the sly to fall to the ground. Everyone eventually manages to stop falling, and a monster comes near Sylvan and starts sniffing. It recognizes a magic power and calls Percival the savior.
    • NotBellion’s unit arrived and takes everyone (at least Percy Team) to what looks like the prison. And NotBellion brings Percival to them.
    • Then an overwhelming bloodthirsting aura is felt. It is the Demon King’s. Everybody is frightened but Percival who recognizes this feeling, this voice. The midget walks up to the Demon King, calls him by the mangled name before being corrected by Zeldris.
