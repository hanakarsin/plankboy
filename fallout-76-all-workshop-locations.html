    Fallout 76 has 22 different workshop locations that players can seek out and claim for their own.  Workshops in Fallout 76 work similarly to how a player’s camp works.  However, they are public spaces that are accessible by anyone in the game world.  Workshops can be found all over the world of Fallout 76 and each one of them has unique properties that come along with claiming them for your own.

    In these workshops players will find a single large red workbench that can be used much like the portable version of the C.A.M.P. device.  Players can build and modify the world around a workshop and they’ll need to because people can contest your claim on the landmark by challenging you in PvP combat.

    You’ll need to spend caps to unlock an unclaimed workshop

    Each of the workshop locations in Fallout 76 have different resources and the value of those resources will make the workshop more difficult to claim.  Below you’ll find the location of all of the workshops in Fallout 76
