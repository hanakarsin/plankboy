    If you want to be the last player standing while playing Battle Royale in Fortnite, knowing by heart where to find a few Vending Machines is a great first step.

    Recommended Videos

    But given how the machines can be used to get top-tier weapons from the get-go, it’s a given that finding them is no easy task. To help with that, here’s the location of all vending machines in Fortnite.

    All Fortnite Vending Machine Locations

    According to the values showcased on the Fortnite GG interactive map, you can currently find a total of 14 Vending Machine spawn spots in the game, with two being located on Rebel’s Roost, three in the mountains surrounding Classy Courts, as well as three in the area surrounding Grand Glacier. The remaining six can be found around the portion of the map featuring Hazy Hillside and Reckless Railways.

    You can check out the location of each of the mentioned vending machine spawn points below. Differently from the always available Mending Machines, Vending Machines will not spawn when playing on Zero Build.

    • Table of Contents:

    All Rebel’s Roost Vending Machine Locations

    The first Rebel’s Roost Vending Machine spawn point is located by a seaside shop west of the region’s main building, while the second can be found by another one north of it. You can check out the exact location of both spawn points in the map below, courtesy of Fortnite GG.

    All Classy Courts Vending Machine Locations

    According to Fortnite GG, the first Vending Machine located around Classy Courts can be found by the side of a house northwest of it. The other two can be spotted by heading to a house located at the center of a mount north of the POI and to a cabin east of it respectively.

    Related: How to Use Fortnite Jam Tracks in Battle Royale as Emotes

    All Fortnite Grand Glacier and Reckless Railways Vending Machine Locations

    According to Fortnite GG, the vending machines close to Grand Clacier can be found on a fluctuating house on the sea north of it, in a structure east of the hotel, and by a house located northwest of it. The vending machines in Reckless Hailways can be found in a gas station west of the area and at a car dealership by its entrance.

    Although the two possible vending machine spawns points in Reckless Hailways are close to one another, the area is both the home of the Syndicate Boss Valeria and one of the most popular landing spots in the game. So be careful when going there.

    All Hazy Hillside Vending Machine Locations

    Taking into account the data featured in Fortnite GG’s Interactive Map, the first Hazy Hillside vending machine can be found inside the area’s manor, while the remaining two can be spotted by heading to houses north of it. You can check out the exact location of both houses in the image below, courtesy of Fortnite GG.

    This guide was made while playing Fortnite on both PC and PlayStation 5.
