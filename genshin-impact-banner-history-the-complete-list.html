    Genshin Impact Version 4.2 Banner Leaks

    According to a post now deleted made on the PlayStation Blog, Genshin Impact version 4.2 will have Furina and Baizhu as the stars of its first wave of Event Wish Banners, as well as Kamisato Ayato and Cyno as the stars of its second. The 4-star characters set to be featured in each banner wave were not fully revealed, but Charlotte will featured as part of the Furina and Baizhu banners.

    This will be Baizhu’s second rerun since his debut on version 3.6, as well as Ayato and Cyno’s third since they debuted in Genshin Impact’s versions 2.6 and 3.1 respectively.
