    Genshin Impact‘s version 2.2 brought to the game not only the return of beloved characters but also a brand-new region to be explored, filled with treasure chests, quests, and of course, lots of new puzzles. With that said, we will now tell you where to find and how to solve the game’s new 6 Pyro Torches Puzzle. Ready?

    Where to Find and How to Solve Genshin Impact’s New 6 Pyro Torches Puzzle

    First, to be able to unlock the new puzzle, you will need to complete the second part of the game’s new ”Through the Mists” questline and get the Peculiar Pinion gadget. With that out of the way, to be able to find the puzzle you need to head towards the ruins below Shiriroko Peak, located on Tsurumi Island, and go to the spot featured on the map below:

    Once there, you just need to search for the mural below, which will, just like all other murals featuring the Thunderbird, open a secret pathway after players activate the Peculiar Pinion near it.

    After the pathway opens, you just need to follow it, and you will find the wall featuring the six torches. Just like all the other torch-related puzzles around the ruins, you will be able to find the solution to the puzzle in a mural, which will be, this time, in the selling of a room above the one you will currently be in. To see it, you just need to go towards the Electro puzzle and look up from the root connecting both rooms. To solve the 6 torches puzzle you just need to follow the mural’s instructions and light up the torches marked in the image below.

    After completing Genshin Impact’s new 6 Pyro Torches Puzzle you will receive an Exquisite Chest. After solving the puzzle don’t forget to keep exploring the ruins since the area is filled with other secrets made to be discovered with the Peculiar Pinion.

    Genshin Impact is available now on PC, PS4, PS5, and mobile devices.
