    GTA Online May 26 Update: The Vespucci Job (Remix), 2X GTA$ and RP on CEO Work, and More

    11 Feb 2024, 00:00

    GTA Online has had a massive influx of content and bonuses this week for players to enjoy working their way through. One of these is of course the all-new Vespucci Job Remix that players will no doubt be flocking to with a flurry of speed as they begin to pursue or become the person being pursued in this remix of a classic mode for the experience. This news article will take you through everything you need to know about the May 26th update for the week and about the GTA Online Vespucci Job Remix.

    There has been a long history of weekly updates with bonuses for players who choose to take part in certain activities throughout the week. Of course, these updates range in the variety of content and what is actually included. This week however has a bundle of content for players and lots of bonuses on the table for fans to work through.

    Vespucci Job Remix Explained

    Firstly, the main focus of the update of course appears to be centered on The Vespucci Job Remix, detailed in the official blog post. The core concept of the mode itself will not be changed compared to the non-remix version. Nonetheless, the Remix is going to be across seven maps “inspired by Vinewood blockbusters”.  Furthermore, the “Issi Classic” is now replaced for the mode by a variety of other fancier vehicles such as classic racing cars.

    The interceptors even have access to attack helicopters to try and stop the escape of the opposing team. A lot of chaos will certainly ensure and it will be a joy to experience. There are going to be double GTA cash and RP point payouts from completing matches of this mode through the week.

    2X GTA$ and 2X RP Bonuses

    Double GTA$ and RP points are also applied to all of the following through the week:

    • Clubhouse Contracts
    • Competitive MC Challenges
    • MC Work
    • VIP/CEO Work (including a double cash bonus for bodyguards)
    • Bunker Series
    • Truck Off
    • Mobile Operations

    Then following that players can also take advantage of the discounts that are available this week. From Warehouses and Vehicle Warehouses being 30% off to business supplies also being 50% off among many other discounts. There certainly is something for everyone with the discounts and content this week!

    GTA+ Benefits

    If you have access to the GTA+ subscription, the member benefits this month are as follows:

    • The Agency in Rockford Hills which will have access to The Contract
    • Grotti Brioso R/A, including a Hao Special Works Upgrade with an extra 2 exclusive liveries being included.
    • 2X GTA Cash for Security Contracts
    • Free clothing and accessories for your character

    In terms of the Diamond Casino, “The Übermacht Revolter” is the podium vehicle for the week so be sure to try and snatch that up if you’re looking for a fancy car to bring along on your journeys through Los Santos!

    GTA V is available now and playable on PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 3, Xbox Series X/S, Xbox One, Xbox 360, and PC.
