    Genshin Impact‘s version 2.1 brought to the game not only new enemies and quests but also a new one-time domain called Palace in a Pool. With that said, we will now tell you how to unlock the game’s new one-time domain.

    Palace in a Pool Domain Location

    First, to find the domain, you just need to head to Watatsumi Island. Once there, head northwest towards Suigetsu Pool. You can check out the domain’s exact location in the map below, courtesy of Genshin Impact’s official interactive map.

    How to Unlock the Palace in a Pool Domain in Genshin Impact

    To unlock the new domain, you need to, first, make sure that all the arrows located at the region are pointing towards the Electro Pillars. An easy way to do that is by making use of the Electrograna to quickly traverse between the points. After that, you just need to light up all pillars since doing that will lower the water level. To travel between the points, we recommend that you make use of Keqing. To light up the pillars, using both Fischl and Sara is advised.

    After that, you just need to jump into the water and head towards the small path located in front of the domain. Swim through the gap, but get ready, since it’s a long swim. After reaching the other side, go up to the stairs located there and head towards a room located above it. Once there, turn on all switches located in the area. After that, just go up, and you will find the device showcased in the picture below. To open the patch to the domain, you just need to activate it.
