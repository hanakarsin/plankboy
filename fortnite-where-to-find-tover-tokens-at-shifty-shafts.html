    If you’re looking to find all the Tover Tokens at Shifty Shafts, then you’ve come to the right place. Fortnite Chapter 3 Season 3 has just begun and Tover Tokens are the new collectibles available this season, unlocking new styles for the customizable Snap skin. Snap is a fully modular skeleton that can have unique heads, arms, torsos, and more, so players will be able to fine-tune the skin to create something that’s truly their own. Tover Tokens can be found all over the island, but the ones at Shifty Shafts are particularly tough to find since they’re not where you’d think to look. Here’s where to find all 3 Tover Tokens at Shifty Shafts in Fortnite Chapter 3 Season 3.

    Fortnite Shifty Shafts Tover Token Locations

    There are 3 Tover Tokens in Shifty Shafts. This POI has not moved for the past few seasons, and it can still be found in the northern part of the map between Sleepy Sound and Logjam Lumberyard. All 3 Tover Tokens can be found in the northern outskirts of the POI, which is much higher in elevation than the rest of the area.

    Shifty Shafts Tover Token #1

    The first Tover Token is located in the northwestern corner of Shifty Shafts along a broken railway that overlooks the entire area. It’s hidden behind a red mine cart that looks like it’s about to fall over. You can see its exact location in the image above.

    Shifty Shafts Tover Token #2

    The second Tover Token can be found a short distance to the northeast of the first one. If you continue through the mine shafts from the first Tover Token and continue down and to the right, you will find this Tover Token as you emerge outside. You can see the exact spot in the above screenshot.

    Shifty Shafts Tover Token #3

    The third and final Tover Token is north of the other two. Climb nearly to the top of the mountain on the northern edge of Shifty Shafts until you reach a small wooden structure with a boarded-up entrance. The Tover Token is inside the shaft behind the boards. You can see its exact position in the above image.

    To collect all 3 Tover Tokens in Shifty Shafts, you basically just have to make your way through the northern strip of mine shafts that are higher than the others. As you climb the mountain and explore those shafts, you will find all of the Tover Tokens in the area.

    That’s all the info you need to find the Shifty Shafts Tover Tokens in Fortnite Chapter 3 Season 3! While these will unlock some cool cosmetics for Snap, you can visit Rave Cave and Greasy Grove to unlock even more styles for the skin. There’s also a ton to do in Chapter 3 Season 3 besides collecting Tover Tokens, such as planting Reality Saplings or finding Sunbird, Moon Hawk, and Rustler to complete some of the season’s first challenges. That’ll give you a headstart on leveling up the Chapter 3 Season 3 Battle Pass, bringing you one step closer to unlocking Darth Vader.

    Fortnite is available now on PC, PS4, PS5, Xbox One, Xbox Series X|S, Nintendo Switch, and mobile devices.
