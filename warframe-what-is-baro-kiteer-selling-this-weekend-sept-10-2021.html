    17 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Our favorite Void Trader is back for a limited time in Warframe, and he did not come empty handed. He has new items this time around on PC, and some rather highly important ones on consoles. This is a weekend that may not want to be missed. Here are the following wares he has for both console and PC.

    Warframe Baro Locations 9/10/21

    • Kuiper Relay – PlayStation and Nintendo Switch
    • Vesper Relay – Xbox
    • Larunda Relay – PC

    A note for PlayStation and Switch players: You’ll have to be a minimum of Mastery Rank 8 to enter the Relay where Baro will be stationed at.

    The Rhapsody in Blue decoration is definitely a nod to the band Queen with their most popular song, Bohemian Rhapsody. Unfortunately this isn’t a glyph, but little references like this are really fun.

    Aside from that Easter Egg, other things to look out for or prioritize on this list are the Jolt and Primed Pistol Ammo Mutation mods. Those will come in handy. Then, perhaps prioritize the Halikar Wraith and Prisma Dual Cleavers for the mastery points. The Zanuka Beacon might be useful, but the weapon parts it drops have two counterparts which are much better than the original Detron it has. Finally, as the majority of everything else is cosmetic, have at it to your heart’s content.

    Console players should really start off with all the Primed mods, especially Primed Animal Instinct if they haven’t gotten it already. Archwing mods are slowly starting to become of use with the growing content requiring them. Additionally, the Mark of the Beast mod seems very niche, but should still be picked up. Then, get the Ignis Wraith and Prova Vandal. The former weapon is perhaps one of the better weapons in Warframe
