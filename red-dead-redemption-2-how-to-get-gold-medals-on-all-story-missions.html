    Red Dead Redemption 2: How to Get Gold Medals on All Story Missions

    16 Mar 2024, 00:00

    Recommended Videos

    Each story mission in Red Dead Redemption 2 will offer additional challenges outside of just completing the mission.  Some require that you get a certain accuracy percentage, some require that you complete the mission within a certain amount of time, some have other requirements that you must maintain to get a gold rating for the mission.  Below you’ll find a list of story missions broken down by chapter and mission and what the requirements are in gettting a gold medal for each mission.

    Simply complete all of the secondary objectives on any story mission and you’ll be rewarded with a gold medal upon completion.

    RDR2 Chapter 1 Story Missions and Objectives for Gold Medal

    Outlaws from the West

    • Take no damage during the shootout
    • Loot 6 or more items from Adler Ranch
    • Complete with at least 80% accuracy

    Enter, Pursued by a Memory

    • Kill all wolves without taking damage
    • Complete with at least 80% accuracy

    Old Friends

    • Catch and hogtie Kieran within 45 seconds
    • Kill 3 O’Driscolls in the same Dead Eye
    • Complete within 15 minutes 30 seconds
    • Get 15 headshots
    • Complete the mission without using any health items

    The Aftermath of Genesis

    • Kill each deer with one arrow
    • Do not spook deer
    • Get a clean kill on the second deer

    Who the hell is Leviticus Cornwall

    • Save Lenny when he is hanging from the train
    • After stopping the train, take out the guards within 1 minute 30 seconds
    • Take no damage during the shootout
    • Get 10 Headshots
    • Complete the mission without taking any health items

    Eastward Bound

    • Pick up Javier when entering horseshoe overlook
    • complete mission within 6 minutes

    RDR2 Chapter 2 Story Missions and Gold Medal Objectives
