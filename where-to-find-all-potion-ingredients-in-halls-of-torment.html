    After unlocking the ability to use potions and drinkables in Halls of Torment, you will be able to consume them in order to influence the effect of many traits, as well as their probabilities. But although Strong Wine will be available from the get-go. the Potion of Memories, Potion of Oblivion, and Reverberant Tinkture will only become available after you gather all of their ingredients, which is easier said than done. With that said, here’s where to find all Potion ingredients in Halls of Torment.

    Recommended Videos

    Halls of Torment Potion Ingredient Locations

    To start off, you will be able to find Amanita, Bluecap, and Dragon Nettle, which are needed in order to unlock the Potion of Oblivion, by heading to the Ember Grounds (Amanita and Dragon Nettle) and to the Haunted Caverns maps (Bluecap). Like all other ingredients in this article, they will spawn at chance in random spots on their set maps.

    Luckily enough, the ingredients needed to unlock the Potion of Memories —Coppergrass, Firepaw, and Truffle— can also be found in the Haunted Caverns (Coppergrass and Truffle) and on the Ember Grounds (Truffle) halls.

    Related: Halls of Torment Launch Options: Force Dedicated GPU and OpenGL Explained

    For the Reverberant Tinkture, you will need to gather Belladonna, Salsify, and Wallflower. You will be able to find Belladonna and Wallflower in the Forgotten Viaduct. The Salsify, on the other hand, can be found on the Ember Grounds.

    To recap, here is where to find all potion ingredients in Halls of Torment:

    • Amanita: Ember Grounds
    • Bluecap: Haunted Caverns map
    • Dragon Nettle: Ember Grounds
    • Coppergrass: Haunted Caverns
    • Firepaw: Ember Grounds
    • Truffle: Random chance.
    • Belladonna: Forgotten Viaduct
    • Salsify: Ember Grounds
    • Wallflower: Forgotten Viaduct

    How to Find the Location of the Potion Ingredients

    In order to mark the location of the available items and thus head straight to them from the get-go, you will need to make use of the Gatherer’s Charm, which will work like a compass leading you to the ingredients.

    You will be able to get the charm automatically after saving the Cupbearer and unlocking the ability to use potions in the game.

    How to Increase Your Potion Amount

    After unlocking the potions system, you will be able to increase the number of potions you can take to the field by defeating bosses and then selecting a bottle among the available items. Once you get the bottle, you will then need to retrieve it through the well and expend 2,000 Gold in order to get it from the Wellkeeper. After purchasing the item, your usable potion amount will increase.

    This guide was made while playing Halls of Torment on PC and will be updated as new potions or ingredients debut within the game.
