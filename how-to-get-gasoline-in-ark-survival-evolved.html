    As you make your way through the landscapes of Ark: Survival Evolved, you’ll need to make sure that you have more than enough materials and resources to continue to survive and build your villages up to the exact specifications that you would like. You’ll need to hunt, fish, craft, and survive to make sure that you’re able to see another day, and while you continue to fight against foes and other beasts that are trying to wipe you off of the face of this wasteland.

    However, if you’re looking to power many different machines, you’ll need to make sure that you’ve got a good supply of gasoline in your camp, so making sure that you know how to create gas, as whereas what you’ll be able to do with it. Let’s get into the details of how to get your hands on some gasoline, and what you’ll need to do to get your camp running properly.

    How To Make Gas In Ark: Survival Evolved

    If you’re looking to get your hands on some gasoline in Ark, the process to get it is quite simple. You’ll need to have a few different things before you can start making it, however, so make sure that you have crafted an Industrial Forge or a Refining Forge. You’ll need to place 5 oil and 3 hides in one of these forges to start making the fuel to power your different machines.

    However, you may want to create the Industrial Forge if you are needing to make as much gas as possible, as you’ll be able to place enough of these resources into this specific type to make up to 100 Gas at a time. Making as much gas as possible is something that can make sure that you’re able to power your Chemisty Bench, Electrical Generator, Fabricator, Industrial Cooker, Industrial Forge, and Industrial Grill for as long as possible without having to take a break.
