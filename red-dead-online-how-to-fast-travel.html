    There are a couple of different ways to fast travel in Red Dead Online, and it’s pretty similar to the way that you fast travel in the single player side of the game.  Red Dead Online does not feature fast travel by train, instead you need to look out for a different type of fast travel marker in the world.

    Anywhere that you find the broken wagon wheel with the ram’s skull on top of it, you can fast travel from this point.  You can also see fast travel spots on the overworld map and they look like a signpost.  You can find these Red Dead Online fast travel locations in Armadillo, Tumbleweed, MacFarlane’s Ranch, Manzanita Post, Blackwater, Strawberry, Valentine, Colter, Emerald Station, Rhodes, Wapiti, Van Horn, Annesburg, Saint Denis, Lagras, Tumbleweed, or you can fast travel back to your camp location.

    It does cost money to fast travel though and the prices can be quite steep depending on how far you are looking to go.  The further your destination, the more it will cost you to fast travel in Red Dead Online.

    Fast travel is also an instant process now.  Instead of being treated to a short cinematic that showed you riding in a stage coach or riding on a train, you will not just instantly be transported to the destination you choose.

    Can you Fast Travel from Camp in Red Dead Online?

    You can fast travel from your camp in Red Dead Online, but it is not something you will have access to right away.  It is an unlockable feature that will only be available after you reach level 65.  Once you do reach this level you will be able to purchase the fast travel feature which will add one of the broken wagon wheels to your camp which you can fast travel from.
