    In Ghost Recon Breakpoint, the Medic Drone can be a literal life saver.  When you don’t have the time to heal yourself or your teammates need healing the drone can restore your health in a pinch.  There are a couple of things to know about the Medic Drone before you try to use it.

    First, to be able to use the Medic Drone in Ghost Recon Breakpoint you’re actually going to have to be using the Medic Class.  Using the Field Medic class your Class Technique will be the Healing Drone.  This is a deployable drone that carries syringes that can be used to heal yourself or your teammates.

    To deploy the Medic Drone (Healing Drone), you actually need to press the right and left bumper buttons.  (RB+LB or R1+L1).  Then, once you’ve deployed the drone you need to click in the left thumbstick while close to yourself or your teammates that you want to heal to fire off the syringe and recover your health.

    This will be a one shot to recover all of your health when done correctly.
