    The latest iteration of Crash Team Racing is upon us with the release of Nitro Fueled.  It’s brought many things forward from previous versions, including the ability to use cheat codes in Crash Team Racing Nitro Fueled.  Regardless of what platform you play on, you can use cheat codes in the game on Xbox, PlayStation, or Nintendo Switch.  While the buttons are different for each console, you can still unlock everything from the main menu.

    One thing is consistent across all platforms.  You need to hold in the Front bumpers on the top of the controler, whether that be L1+R1 on the PlayStation 4, LB+RB on the Xbox One or L+R on the Nintendo Switch.  After that it’s just a matter of pressing button inputs on the face of the controller to unlock the cheats in Nitro Fueled.
