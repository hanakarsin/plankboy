    Borderlands 3 is all about getting loot, and Gearbox hasn’t made the game a huge grind to get it.  However, if you want to farm XP, weapons, or cash you can do that too if you want.  One of the best ways to get items or cash is to catch a Loot Tink when you find them.  These enemies have the potential to drop legendary and epic items as well as cash.

    The Loot Tink will run when you spot them and they can be hard to track in a crowded area with multiple enemies.  If you do manage to kill one it will drop way more loot than a common enemy.  It will be more like a boss drop as the Loot Tink explodes into many different pieces of loot.

    Head to Eden-6 and Follow This Map to Farm the Loot Tink

    There is a spot in Borderlands 3 where you can farm the Loot Tink.  This farming method can be done during the main story campaign or way after when you’re going back to play on Mayhem Modes.

    This farming method will require that you’ve reached Eden-6.  On Eden-6 you will eventually be introduced to the Jakob’s Estate and in this area you can find a Loot Tink that you can farm.  Just head to the location in the video below or the image above and you can kill the Loot Tink to farm its items over and over again.

    The Save Point is right next to the Loot Tink spawn

    This farm method is fast and easy because there is a save point very close to the Loot Tink and it spawns every single time you load in.  The only downtime you have is when you are waiting for the game to reload after collecting your loot.

    Better yet, if you turn on the Mayhem Modes you can make the loot 900% better each time you kill this character which takes about 10 seconds.
