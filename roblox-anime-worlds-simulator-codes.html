    Following its initial release on July 4th, 2021, and multiple updates since then, Anime Worlds Simulator by Macaw Games has already reached just under five million visits and 36 thousand likes! That’s quite an achievement on the massively popular Roblox platform and their success isn’t expected to stop there. The developers are super grateful for all the support and are giving out awesome codes that grant temporary bonuses such as boosts to coins, mythical and legendary rewards, and lots more. Here’s our list of all current Roblox Anime Worlds Simulator Codes!

    Roblox Anime Worlds Simulator Codes

    These Roblox Anime Worlds Simulator codes have all been tested and are confirmed to be working as of this month.

    New codes are added regularly.

    • 60kfruits – Redeem for x2 Fruits for 15 minutes
    • 60klegends – Redeem for x5 legendary for 15 mins
    • 30kmythical – Redeem for x5 Mythical for 15 mins
    • 30kcoins – Redeem for x10 coins for 15 mins
    • 15klikes – Redeem for x5 legendary from orb chance for 15 mins
    • 1klikes – Redeem code for 2x Coin Boost

    Note that the above codes must be entered exactly as shown above – in all lower case. While Anime Worlds Simulator seems to have not expired any of their codes just yet, remember that Roblox Codes often expire quickly so be sure to use them! The developer is also teasing an awesome new code if the game reaches 100k likes, so be sure to check back once that happens for the latest code! See below for how to redeem these codes in-game.

    How To Redeem Codes In-Game

    To redeem your free boosts in Anime Worlds Simulator, launch the game then click the Twitter icon (marked as codes) and enter any codes from the list above. They MUST be entered in lower case or they will not work as of the time of this writing. Click the enter button and if the code still works, a message will tell you what you got. Nice!

    Roblox is available now on PC, Xbox One, and mobile devices. For more Roblox codes, check out our other Roblox Guides.
