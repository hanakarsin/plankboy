    How to Get the Edgerunner Jacket in Cyberpunk 2077

    David is the main character in Cyberpunk: Edgerunners and because of that, you’ll likely want his jacket in Cyberpunk 2077. It’s an iconic jacket that’ll make your drip even drippier.

    David’s jacket is obtained through a side quest in Cybperunk 2077. That side quest is called “Over the Edge.” Here is how to start and complete “Over the Edge” to get David’s jacket in Cyberpunk 2077:

  • First, travel to Megabuilding H4 in Arroyo, located in the Santo Domingo district. This location is where you find David’s apartment in Night City.
  • The side job icon will take you to an alley where the green Edgerunners symbol is spray painted on the wall.
  • You will find Brain Dance inside a trashcan featuring a clip from Edgerunners, which starts the side gig, Over the Edge.
  • Text Muamar “El Capitan” Reyes.
  • Skip time by a few days, and you will receive a message from Falco that David’s crew is defunct, but he left you a gift.
  • Travel to the Badlands Dam at the Dam Viewpoint.
  • David’s jacket will be inside a case with the same green Edgerunners symbol, located on the edge of a wall overlooking the environment.

    • Congratulations, you now look snazzy, just like David did in the show.

    David’s Jacket Explained

    If David’s jacket could talk, it would have some of the craziest stories. If you haven’t watched Edgerunners and just want to know the lore behind David’s jacket, then we’ve got you covered.

    Here is the jacket’s transformation from a simple EMT outfit to a Night City icon.

  • David’s mother, Gloria, initially owned the jacket and wore it as an emergency medical technician in Night City.
  • David started wearing it after she passed away during the events of the first episode.
  • David decided to paint the green logo on the back of his jacket after Lucy was tinkering with it.
  • When David installed the Arasaka prototype exoskeleton, he left his jacket to his Edgerunner gang.
  • Falco and Lucy escaped the fight between David and Adam Smasher in Edgerunner, where David left his jacket.

    • Now that you can obtain his jacket, you can wear it on your journey through Night City. What new stories will it have to tell?

    I personally loved Cyberpunk: Edgerunners and am really happy to see David’s signature jacket in Cyberpunk 2077. It was a fantastic cross-over show that gets even better since there are in-game references and easter eggs to discover.

    David’s Jacket Item Details

    • Type: Outer Torso
    • Rarity: Legendary
    • Mod Types: Empty Clothing Mod Slot
    • Mod Slots: 2 x Mod slots
    • Weight: 3.0
