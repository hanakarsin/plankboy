    Spider-Man on PS4 has a ton of different suit options for players to use.  Though can’t just use any suit that you want in the game.  You’ll need to unlock each suit one by one and then use challenge tokens to actually purchase them once they are unlocked.  Most of the suits in Spider-Man PS4 are unlocked once you reach a certain level in the game.  Others are unlocked by completing specific missions.  The very best suits are unlocked after doing things like completing specific objectives or completion milestones in the game.

    Along the course of Spider-Man on PS4 you’ll encounter 28 suits in the game, all which have unique powers and abilities.  Here’s how to unlock each of the 28 suits in Spider-Man PS4.  Head here for a list of different suit powers and how to change suits.

    Spider-Man PS4 How to Unlock All Suits

    Advanced Suit – Complete Story Mission “Something Old, Something New”

    Cost:  1 Backpack Token, 2 Crime Tokens

    Classic Suit (Damaged) – Complete Story Mission “Something Old, Something New”

    Cost: Free

    Classic Suit (Repaired) – Complete Story Mission “Something Old, Something New”

    Cost: 2 Backpack Tokens, 2 Crime Tokens

    Noir Suit – Complete Story Mission “Something Old, Something New”

    Cost: 2 Backpack Tokens, 1 Base Token

    Scarlet Spider Suit – Reach Level 4

    Cost: 3 Crime Tokens, 2 Landmark Tokens

    Spider Armor MKII Suit – Reach Level 5

    Cost: 1 Base Token 1 Landmark Token, 2 Research Tokens

    Secret War Suit – Reach Level 7

    Cost: 2 Backpack Tokens, 1 Base Token, 2 Research Tokens

    Stark Suit – Reach Level 9

    Cost: 1 Base Token, 3 Crime Tokens, 1 Research Token

    Negative Suit – Reach Level 11

    Cost: 1 Base Token, 2 Landmark Tokens, 1 Research Token

    Electrically Insulated Suit – Reach Level 13

    Cost: 1 Base Token, 3 Crime Tokens, 1 Research Token

    Spider-Punk – Reach Level 13

    Cost: 2 Backpack Tokens, 3 Crime Tokens, 2 Landmark Tokens

    Wrestler Suit – Reach Level 19

    Cost: 2 Backpack Tokens, 2 Base Tokens, 2 Research Tokens

    Fear Itself Suit – Reach Level 21

    Cost: 2 Base Tokens, 6 Challenge Tokens, 3 Research Tokens

    Stealth Suit – Reach Level 23

    Cost: 2 Base Tokens, 4 Challenge Tokens, 3 Landmark Tokens

    Spider Armor MKIII Suit – Reach Level 26

    Cost: 2 Base Tokens, 4 Challenge Tokens, 4 Crime Tokens

    Spider-Man 2099 Black Suit – Reach Level 29

    Cost: 2 Base Tokens, 4 Crime Tokens, 4 Research Tokens

    Iron Spider Suit – Reach Level 31

    Cost: 3 Base Tokens, 3 Challenge Tokens, 4 Crime Tokens

    Velocity Suit – Reach Level 33

    Cost: 2 Backpack Tokens, 4 Challenge Tokens, 2 Landmark Tokens

    Spider Armor MK IV Suit – Reach Level 35

    Cost: 4 Base Tokens, 4 Challenge Tokens, 3 Landmark Tokens

    Spirit Spider Suit – Reach Level 37

    Cost: 6 Base Tokens, 6 Challenge Tokens, 6 Crime Tokens

    Spider-Man 2099 White Suit – Reach Level 39

    Cost: 4 Base Tokens, 4 Challenge Tokens, 4 Crime Tokens

    Vintage Comic Book Suit – Reach Level 41

    Cost: 4 Backpack Tokens, 4 Challenge Tokens, 4 Crime Tokens

    Last Stand Suit – Reach Level 45

    Cost: 20 Crime Tokens

    Homemade Suit – Complete Story Mission “Something Old, Something New”

    Cost: Find all backpacks in the game.

    Anti-Ock Suit – Complete “Pax in Bello” Story Mission

    Cost: Free

    Dark Suit – Complete Story Mission “Stakeout” staying hidden

    Cost: Complete all Black Cat Mission

    Equalizer Suit – Complete Story Mission “Pax in Bello”

    Cost: Complete all main missions, side quests, and 100% districts
