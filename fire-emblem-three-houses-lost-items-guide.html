    Fire Emblem Three Houses uses a unique system of character recruitment that allows you to sway characters into joining forces with you. To recruit characters you can recover lost items, give them gifts, and raise certain stats to make your house appealing and develop a bond with different characters or increase their motivation.

    During the course of the Fire Emblem Three Houses time will transpire and each month you will find new lost items to return to their rightful owners. These items can be found while exploring in the Monastery and then your job is to return these items to the correct person.

    In the guide below you’ll find a month by month breakdown of what items you’ll find and who to give them to. Lost Items can only be returned to the correct owners in Part 1 of the game. Simply search the Monastery for these items and then return them to their owners. Once you reach Part 2 of the game you will no longer be able to return them.
