    How to Find the Hydro Island Computer Key in Warzone 2 DMZ

    All keys are found the same way in Warzone 2 DMZ: by luck. It comes down to luck of the draw when you are trying to find the Hydro Island Computer Key in Warzone 2 DMZ. Our best advice is to loot as many crates as you can and defeat NPCs and check what they are carrying.

    Unlike other keys that require you to retreat to the newly unveiled Ashika Island, this key will only be found in Al Mazhrah. While this may be the larger map of the two, at least this means that there are more enemies for you to track down and defeat as quickly as possible. Be sure to snag some extra Contraband Weapons to take out when you extract from this map.

    If you want to be really devious, you could hunt down other online squads and steal their keys. Although some Faction mission reward keys like the Dead Drop mission for the Traveler’s Luggage Key, your best and only option to find the Hydro Island Computer Key is to loot crates, kill enemies and loot them, and hunt down online squads and loot their keys.

    Hydro Island Computer Key Location in Warzone 2.0 DMZ

    If you already have a Hydro Island Computer Key, all you need to do is pinpoint the location in which you can use the key. The Hydro Island Computer Key location is found in a building located on the middle peninsula of Zarqwa Hydroelectric.

    To get close enough to Zarqwa Hydroelectric, it is strongly recommended to either snag a boat or a Personal Watercraft, as they will allow you to get close enough without risking mowing through multiple enemies at a time.

    On the small island, either approach from the right-hand side, or enter through the exit, which is a small jump from a slightly less guarded building. Both entrances are shown in the slideshow above. Once inside, take a sharp left and head into the back room of the building.

    This Hydro Island Computer Key opens the PC tower which is underneath a desk. A quick scan of the room and you’ll find it pretty easily. Press and hold the interact button with the Hydro Island Computer Key in your inventory, and claim everything you can before getting out of there.
