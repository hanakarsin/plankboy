    How to Get Strawberry Milk in Boba Story

  • Unlock Magic Den: Save 1,000 Leaves and buy the Magic Den on your café’s left side.
  • Use Kokoro’s Gifts: After unlocking, interact with Kokoro’s Gifts daily for three free ingredients.
  • Get Ingredients: Ingredients from Kokoro’s Gifts are random. Buy one for 400 Leaves or watch an ad for three.
  • Patience: Strawberry Milk is rare, so keep trying until you get it.
