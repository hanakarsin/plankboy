    How to Redeem Codes in Call of Dragons

    Follow all of the instructions that have been listed below to redeem codes in Call of Dragons.

  • Boot up Call of Dragons on your chosen device, iOS, Android, or PC.
  • Get past the tutorial section if playing for the first time.
  • Press/click on your Profile image in the top left of the screen.
  • Press/click on the cogwheel in the bottom right of the panel.
  • Go to the “Other” section.
  • Press/click “Redeem Gifts” and enter the code in the typing box.
  • Press/click on “Exchange”.
