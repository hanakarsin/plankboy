    Suicide Squad: Kill the Justice League Cast, Explained

    From the above characters, we know that Captain Boomerang, King Shark, Deadshot, and Harley Quinn will be playable in single and multiplayer.

    All Confirmed Voice Actors in Suicide Squad Kill the Justice League: Who Plays Wonder Woman, Batman, and More

    A wide array of big voice actors are featured as part of the Suicide Squad: Kill the Justice League cast. Here are all the confirmed voice actors for Suicide Squad: Kill the Justice League, as well as who they will be playing in the game.

    • Tara Strong – Harley Quinn
    • Debra Wilson – Amanda Waller
    • Kevin Conroy – Batman
    • Krizia Bajos – Tianna Cortez
    • Liam McIntyre – Captain Boomerang 
    • Susan Eisenberg – Wonder Woman
    •  Samoa Joe – King Shark
    • Darcy Rose Byrnes – Poison Ivy
    • Bumper Robinson – Deadshot
    • Rick Pasqualone – Gizmo
    • Wally Wingert – The Riddler
    • David Bateson – Lex Luthor
    • Scott Porter – The Flash

    I don’t know about you, but the biggest highlight among the game’s confirmed cast for me is the late Kevin Conroy. Suicide Squad: Kill the Justice League will mark Conroy’s last appearance as the Dark Knight, so I believe I’m speaking for all fans when I say that I hope the game will live up to its hype and be a worthy closing statement to his legendary career while under the cowl.

    That is everything we know right now about the cast of Suicide Squad: Kill the Justice League, but there will be more information as the release date gets closer. Be sure to check back in for more information.
