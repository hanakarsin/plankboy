    Destiny has a lot to offer and keep up with out of the game, one of which is Public Events. Public events are random occurring events that take place at periodic times on each planet.

    They offer various rewards and are ranked on three tiers, gold, silver and bronze. Gold going to those who are able to fully complete the event. Rewards also await at the Postmaster for the daily rewards for completing your first event.  Each planet has events and some are unique to each planet and others are more of the same. They range from defending a WARSAT from enemy forces, to eliminating an enemy before he reaches the goal, and to preventing enemies to enter a certain area.

    Note: As long as you are in any of the areas below when the event starts, which is every 25-35 minutes, you will have an orange notification come above your screen and it will direct you to the event via an on-screen marker. Also keep in mind, events are random so if two different events occur in the same area, you have a random chance of getting either when the event notification pops up.

    Earth/Cosmodrome

    Defend The Warsat

    Locations: Mothyards, Forgotten Shore, and Skywatch
    When this event occurs players are to defend a Warsat once it goes online by activating a Ghost to it. The data is relayed and the more players helping out and defending it from Fallen the better and faster it goes. Enemies will continue to come until the Warsat is completed or destroyed. Players must hold the position until the ticker reaches 100%.

    Eliminate The Target

    Locations: Mothyards, Forgotten Shores, Rocketyard
    In this event, players must eliminate an Ultra Fallen Captain before he slowly makes his way to his destination. Failure if he arrives at his final checkpoint, simple enough.

    Defeat The Extraction Crews

    Locations:The Steppes, Mothyards, Forgotten Shores
    Fallen ships will fly overhead and will drop enemies at random locations. Players will have to go from location to location, clearing out the crews of Fallen for a total of three drop locations.

    Destroy Fallen Walker

    Location: The Divide
    This event only takes place in The Divide and spawns the giant walker from the Cosmodrome Strike event right into the battlefield for players to take down. If players want the gold tier reward for this, they will have to take it down within five minutes.

    Moon

    Defend The Warsat

    Locations: Archer’s Line, Hellmouth, Anchor Of Light
    Another Warsat event. Once a bright light flashes you know which event is in store for you. So get to the location and activate it with your ghost and prepare to defend until the timer fills up.

    Eliminate The Target

    Locations: Hellmouth, Anchor Of Light
    Players must eliminate a supped up Ultra before he reaches the fifth checkpoint. He has more health and is more aggressive than regular ultra characters so this may take some help.

    Defeat The Extraction Crews

    Locations: Archer’s Line, Hellmouth
    Fallen ships will fly overhead and will drop enemies at random locations. Players will have to go from location to location, clearing out the crews of Fallen for a total of three drop locations.

    Destroy Fallen Walker

    Location: Anchor Of Light
    This event only takes place in Anchor Of Light and spawns the giant walker from the Cosmodrome Strike event right into the battlefield for players to take down. If players want the gold tier reward for this, they will have to take it down within five minutes.

    Venus

    Prevent Vex Sacrifices

    Locations: The Citadel, Ishtar Cliffs
    Players will have to hold off the Vex for four minutes as they attempt to sacrifice themselves at a pillar of light on top of a hill. If ten Vex complete the sacrifice your mission is failed.

    Eliminate The Target

    Location: The Citadel
    Players must eliminate a supped up Ultra before he reaches the fifth checkpoint. He has more health and is more aggressive than regular ultra characters so this may take some help.

    Defeat The Extraction Crews

    Locations: Ember Caves
    Fallen ships will fly overhead and will drop enemies at random locations. Players will have to go from location to location, clearing out the crews of Fallen for a total of three drop locations.

    Destroy Fallen Walker

    Location: Ember Caves
    This event only takes place in Anchor Of Light and spawns the giant walker from the Cosmodrome Strike event right into the battlefield for players to take down. If players want the gold tier reward for this, they will have to take it down within five minutes.

    Mars

    Eliminate The Target

    Location: The Citadel
    Players must eliminate a supped up Ultra before he reaches the fifth checkpoint. He has more health and is more aggressive than regular ultra characters so this may take some help.

    Defend The Warsat

    Locations: Archer’s Line, Hellmouth, Anchor Of Light
    Another Warsat event. Once a bright light flashes you know which event is in store for you. So get to the location and activate it with your ghost and prepare to defend until the timer fills up.

    There’s all the events that are currently available in Destiny across all planets. We neglected to list the frequency of the events because a recent patch applied to all versions of Destiny, changes the frequency and makes them occur more commonly in the world.
