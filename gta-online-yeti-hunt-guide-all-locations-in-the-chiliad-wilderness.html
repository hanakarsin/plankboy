    GTA Online Yeti Hunt Guide: All Locations in the Chiliad Wilderness

    Festive Surprise 2023 brought to GTA Online a wide array of new Holiday features, going from the debut of new guns to a Yeti hunt which rewards all who complete it with an exclusive outfit.

    Recommended Videos

    But how can you complete the hunt for the mythological creature? Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to complete the Yeti Hunt in GTA Online.

    All GTA Online Yeti Hunt Chiliad Wilderness Clue Locations

    After receiving a text from Tanner and starting the Yeti Hunt in GTA Online, you will be tasked with finding a total of five clues within the marked area in Chiliad Wilderness — a Rugged Shirt, an Abandoned Camp, a set of Body Parts, a Dead Deer, and a Car Wreck. While the shirt and the camp can be found in the area’s north portion, you will be able to find the latter three by heading south.

    You can check out the exact location of all five clues in the image below.

    Related: Where to Buy the Battle Rifle in GTA Online

    You will be able to spot the body parts by a broken tree, the dead deer on a shore, and the shirt by an abandoned vehicle. The car wreck can be spotted by a small bridge. From all of the clues, the camp will be the easiest to find, as it will be located outside of the main body of the woods and in a relatively open area.

    How to Summon and Defeat the Yeti

    After examining all five clues during nighttime, the Yeti will then come to meet you at the spot where you spotted the last one. If they do not appear or if you found the last clue during the daytime, simply return to Chiliad Wilderness after sundown and walk around for a while to meet your foe. The Yeti will be marked on your minimap by a red skull icon and will start to chase you immediately after spawning.

    To defeat the Yeti in GTA Online, I recommend that you use the Up-N-Atomizer to startle the enemy before using a Minigun to quickly finish them off. If you don’t have the Atomizer or a minigun, using a highly damaging sniper rifle while the creature is still far away, and then shifting to either a Battle Rifle or a Shotgun for close range, is a safe bet.

    This guide was made while playing GTA Online on PS5.
