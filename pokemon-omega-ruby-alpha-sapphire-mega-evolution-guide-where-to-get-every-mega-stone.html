    Introduced in last year’s Pokemon X & Y, Mega Evolution is becoming a bigger and bigger part of the Pokemon games with every new release. This year we have Pokemon Omega Ruby & Alpha Sapphire which add in dozens of additional Mega Evolutions for old and new Pokemon. But, if you want to get to this stat-boosting state then you’ll need all of the Mega Stones in your Pokemon’s hands. This means you’ll need to do some digging, or just check out this guide, which will lay out exactly where to obtain every Mega Stone available right now.

    Found throughout the story

    • Absolite
      • Safari Zone
    • Aggronite
      • Rusturf Tunnel – Smash the rocks with Rock Smash
    • Alakazite
      • Slateport City – Found in the market
    • Altarianite
      • Lilycove City – Show Lisia’s fan an Altaria
    • Banettite
      • Mt. Pyre – Found at the summit
    • Beedrilite
      • Sea Mauville – Found in Storage
    • Blazikenite
      • Route 120 or 114
      • If you chose Torchic as your starter then get it from Steven on Route 120
      • Otherwise you can buy it from a seller on Route 114 after subduing the super-ancient Pokemon
    • Diancite
      • Visit a Pokemon Center after getting Diancie via a Special Event
    • Garchompite
      • Fortree City – Reach Platinum Rank in Secret Base Building then get it from Aarune
    • Glalitite
      • Shoal Cave – Found in the ice cavern
    • Gyaradosite
      • Route 123 – Play 123 Go Fish and scratch Poochyena
    • Heracronite
      • Route 127 – Found on an island
    • Latiasite
      • Alpha Sapphire only
      • Southern Island – Received during the story
    • Latiosite
      • Omega Ruby only
      • Southern Island – Received during the story
    • Lucarionite
      • Beat Lisia in any Master Rank contest
    • Manectite
      • Route 110 – Found near Cycling Road
    • Mawilite
      • Verdanturf Town – Found on the ground
    • Medichamite
      • Mt. Pyre
    • Pidgeotite
      • Verdanturf Town – Find the little girl’s Shrromish to receive the Intriguing Stone, show this to Mr. Stone in Rustboro City
    • Pinsirite
      • Route 124 – Found on the ground
    • Sablenite
      • Sootopolis City – Near the bridge to the Cave of Origin
    • Sceptilite
      • Route 120 or 114
      • Get from Steven on Route 120 if you chose Treecko
      • Get from seller on Route 114 after subduing the super-ancient Pokemon otherwise
    • Slowbronite
      • Shoal Cave – Received from the old man when he makes the player a Shell Bell
    • Steelixite
      • Granite Cave – Found in the lower levels
    • Swampertite
      • Route 120 or 114
      • Get from Steven on Route 120 if you chose Mudkip
      • Get from seller on Route 114 after subduing the super-ancient Pokemon otherwise

    Found after you complete the story

    • Abomasite
      • Route 123 – Found in the berry fields
    • Aerodactylite
      • Meteor Falls – Found on the ground
    • Ampharosite
      • New Mauville – Found on the ground
    • Charizardite X
      • Fiery Path – Found on the ground
    • Charizardite Y
      • Scorched Slab – Found on the ground
    • Gardevoirite
      • Verdanturf Town – Speak with Wanda in her home
    • Houndoominite
      • Lavaridge Town – Found on the ground
    • Kangaskhanite
      • Pacifidlog Town – Found on the ground
    • Lopunnite
      • Mauville City – Receive from the business man in Mauville Hills
    • Mewtwonite X
      • Littleroot Town – Found on the ground
    • Mewtwonite Y
      • Pokemon League – Found on the ground
    • Scizorite
      • Petalburg Woods – Found on the ground
    • Tyranitarite
      • Jagged Pass – Found on the ground
    • Venusaurite
      • Route 19 – Found on the ground

    Found during or after the Delta Episode

    • Audinite
      • Battle Resort – Speak with Looker
    • Blastoisinite
      • S.S. Tidal – Found on the ground
    • Cameruptite
      • Omega Ruby – Received from Team Magma during the Delta Episode
      • Alpha Sapphire – Received at the Battle Resort
    • Galladite
      • Fallarbor Town – Speak with Professor Cozma in his lab
    • Gengarite
      • Battle Resort – Found on the ground
    • Latiasite
      • Omega ruby – Speak with your mother in Littleroot Town
    • Latiosite
      • Alpha Sapphire – Speak with your mother in Littleroot Town
    • Metagrossite
      • Defeat Steven once in the Pokemon League
    • Salamencite
      • Meteor Falls – Speak with the old woman
    • Shapedonite
      • Omega Ruby – Received from Team Aqua at the Battle Resort
      • Alpha Sapphire – Received during the Delta Episode
