    15 Jun 2024, 00:00

    The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim release is fast approaching, and while most gamers are patiently waiting to get their hands on Bethesda’s latest, an ambitious group put together this unofficial trailer below.   The gang at TryForce Productions put together the trailer on what they described as a shoe-string budget, and that’s surprising considering how well made the video is.

    The Director said “Creating what we did on a budget of nothing more than lunch for the volunteer cast and crew plus a few pints of corn syrup was a great experience.”

    While team has it’s sights set on another project in the future, hopefully they take some time off with the rest of the gaming community to sift through the world of Skyrim when November 11th rolls around.
