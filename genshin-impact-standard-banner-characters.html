    All Standard Banner Characters in Genshin Impact 4.3

    Genshin Impact features a total of three character-focused banners, two limited-time ones that rotate during each patch and star banner-exclusive 5-Star characters, and the games standard one also known as Wanderlust Invocation.

    Genshin Impact features a total of three character-focused banners, two limited-time ones that rotate during each patch and star banner-exclusive 5-Star characters, and the game’s standard one — also known as Wanderlust Invocation.

    But which 5- and 4-Star characters are available as part of the Standard Wanderlust Invocation Banner? Here are all the characters featured as part of the Genshin Impact Standard Banner as of version 4.3.

    All Standard Banner Characters in Genshin Impact 4.3

    As you might expect, Genshin Impact‘s Wanderlust Invocation banner receives new characters in almost every new major update, although they are in their large majority 4-Stars. Currently, you can pull a total of 45 different characters from it.

    All Standard Banner 5-Star Characters in Genshin Impact

    Genshin Impact’s Standard or Wanderlust Invocation Banner currently features Jean, Diluc, Keqing, Mona, Qiqi, Tighnari, and Dehya as its 5-Stars. Dehya was the latest 5-Star to join the banner. She debuted on version 3.6 and joined the standard selection on 3.7.

    Here are all 5-Star characters currently featured as part of Genshin Impact’s Wanderlust Invocation Banner.

    • Jean
    • Diluc
    • Mona
    • Keqing
    • Qiqi
    • Tighnari
    • Dehya

    All Standard Banner 4-Star Characters

    The game’s standard banner features all 4-Star characters released from Genshin Impact 1.0 to 4.2. The banner currently features 37 4-Star characters, but the number will go up to 38 when Pyro Polearm Chevreuse joins the selection at the end of version 4.3, Roses and Muskets. It is also highly probable that Gaming — the game’s upcoming 4-Star Pyro claymore wielder — will join the banner at the end of version 4.4.

    The Pity System on Genshin Impact triggers when you hit 90 pulls. But keep in mind that hitting that number on the Wanderlust Invocation Banner will not guarantee that you will get a 5-Star character, as you may also receive one of ten 5-Star weapons. The pull count of the Standard banner does not carry over to the event-wish ones, so I highly advise you to refrain from using Primogems on it.

    Will Any Fontaine 5-Star Join the Standard Wanderlust Invocation Banner in Genshin Impact?

    Although there was a lot of speculation on whether or not Wriothesley would join the selection of 5-Star characters for the standard banner after the end of version 4.2, no Fontaine 5-Star character is expected to join the Standard Wanderlust Invocation Banner in Genshin Impact anytime soon.

    Is it Worth Spending Primogems in the Standard Banner?

    I believe that you should save your Primogems for the game’s version-exclusive banners and only pull in the Wanderlust Invocation banner if given Acquaint Fates.

    The main reason for that lies in how the 5-star characters and weapons featured in the event banners will very likely not join the Genshin Standard Banner pool. You will also have a way harder time getting your preferred 4-Stars from the standard banner, as you will have a 1 in 45 chance of getting each (if we exclude all of the 4-star weapons you can also get from it).
