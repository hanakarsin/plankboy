    Far Cry 6 has many Treasure Hunts to discover across Yara, and A Rising Tide is one of the most unique puzzles that players can encounter on their island adventure. Treasure Hunts reward unique weapons and gear if you can manage to solve a puzzle, and A Rising Tide is no exception. There’s a powerful assault rifle just waiting to be claimed if you can figure out the code by finding the right order of boats. Here’s how to solve the A Rising Tide Treasure Hunt in Far Cry 6.

    How to Start A Rising Tide in Far Cry 6

    A Rising Tide is located on the southern coast of El Este, the eastern region of Yara. It’s in an abandoned building in a small fishing village. Inside, you’ll find a note that will start the Treasure Hunt.

    The treasure is locked behind a door that requires a special code to open. To open the door, you have to press five buttons in the correct order. Each button is named after one of the Legends of ’67, and the nearby note explains how to find the right sequence.

    How to Find the Right Order for the Code

    The note explains that there are five boats nearby that are marked with the names of the Legends along with a number from 1-5. These boats tell you the order for the buttons. You could go find all five boats, or you could just keep reading below for the full code order.

    If you want to do this Treasure Hunt the normal way, you can go find all five boats. Otherwise, here is the correct order for the A Rising Tide code:

  • El Tigre
  • Papi Chulo
  • El Lucky
  • Clarita
  • Roja Victoria

    • After pressing all five buttons in the right order, the locked door will open and you can go inside and claim the rewards.

    Inside the room, you’ll find a handful of resources as well as a unique assault rifle called Camo Quinceanera. It comes equipped with a tactical scope and a laser sight as well as soft target rounds, making it a great weapon to add to your arsenal. It also has mods that improve its performance as well, including a mod that allows for faster reloads.

    Far Cry 6 has a lot of treasure hunts, so keep an eye out for them if you want to grab more unique weapons to grow your arsenal. The Liquid Courage Treasure Hunt is not too far from this one, and you can get a rocket launcher by completing it. There are a bunch scattered throughout other parts of Yara as well.
