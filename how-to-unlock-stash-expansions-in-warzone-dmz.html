    The highly anticipated season 4 of Warzone DMZ arrived together with a true platoon of new features and changes, such as the new Vondel map and the new Forward Operating Base feature. The feature allows players to unlock a series of new upgrades like a Wallet and the always handy Stash Expansions. Now, here’s how to unlock all Wallet and Stash Expansions in Warzone DMZ.

    Recommended Videos

    How to Unlock All Wallet and Stash Expansions in Warzone DMZ

    You will be able to unlock all the available base Wallet and Stash Expansions in Call of Duty: Warzone DMZ by increasing your reputation to levels 4,5,6,8, and 10 with White Lotus (Wallet) and Black Mous (Stash), and then completing each expansion’s set of tasks.

    You will also be able to unlock the Crown Wallet and Stash Expansions by increasing your Crown reputation level to the same levels and then completing its sets of tasks.

    To be more specific, here’s how to unlock all Wallet and Stash Expansions in Warzone DMZ.

    How to Unlock All Stash Expansions in DMZ

    • Stash Expansion 1: Reach Reputation Level 4 with Black Mous and then Extract 10 nuclear fuel mods and 2 Radiation block bottles.
    • Stash Expansion 2: Reach Reputation Level 4 with Black Mous and then complete the tasks showcased in the Upgrades menu.
    • Stash Expansion 3: Reach Reputation Level 6 with Black Mous and then complete the tasks showcased in the Upgrades menu.
    • Stash Expansion 4: Reach Reputation Level 8 with Black Mous and then complete the tasks showcased in the Upgrades menu.
    • Stash Expansion 5: Reach Reputation Level 10 with Black Mous and then complete the tasks showcased in the Upgrades menu.
    • Stach Expansion 1 (Crown): Reach Reputation Level 4 with Crown and then complete the tasks showcased in the Upgrades menu.
    • Stach Expansion 2 (Crown): Reach Reputation Level 5 with Crown and then complete the tasks showcased in the Upgrades menu.
    • Stach Expansion 3 (Crown): Reach Reputation Level 6 with Crown and then complete the tasks showcased in the Upgrades menu.
    • Stach Expansion 4 (Crown): Reach Reputation Level 8 with Crown and then complete the tasks showcased in the Upgrades menu.
    • Stach Expansion 5 (Crown): Reach Reputation Level 10 with Crown and then complete the tasks showcased in the Upgrades menu.

    The first two regular Stash Expansions will increase your available slots (for mission and key items) by 5 slots, while the third will offer a 4-slot increase. The remaining ones will offer a 3-slot increase to your stash. The five Crown expansions, on the other hand, will increase the number of slots in your stash by 3, 3, 2, 1, and 1 respectively.

    How to Unlock All Wallet Expansions in DMZ

    • Wallet Expansion 1: Reach Reputation Level 4 with White Lotus and then complete the tasks showcased in the Upgrades menu.
    • Wallet Expansion 2: Reach Reputation Level 5 with White Lotus and then complete the tasks showcased in the Upgrades menu.
    • Wallet Expansion 3: Reach Reputation Level 6 with White Lotus and then complete the tasks showcased in the Upgrades menu.
    • Wallet Expansion 4: Reach Reputation Level 8 with White Lotus and then complete the tasks showcased in the Upgrades menu.
    • Wallet Expansion 5: Reach Reputation Level 10 with White Lotus and then complete the tasks showcased in the Upgrades menu.
    • Wallet Expansion 1 (Crown): Reach Reputation Level 4 with Crown and then complete the tasks showcased in the Upgrades menu.
    • Wallet Expansion 2 (Crown): Reach Reputation Level 5 with Crown and then complete the tasks showcased in the Upgrades menu.
    • Wallet Expansion 3 (Crown): Reach Reputation Level 6 with Crown and then complete the tasks showcased in the Upgrades menu.
    • Wallet Expansion 4 (Crown): Reach Reputation Level 8 with Crown and then complete the tasks showcased in the Upgrades menu.
    • Wallet Expansion 5 (Crown): Reach Reputation Level 10 with Crown and then complete the tasks showcased in the Upgrades menu.

    The first two regular Wallet Expansions will increase your wallet’s capacity by 150K, while the latter three will increase it by 100K each. The first Crown Wallet Expansion will increase its capacity to 100K, while the others will offer a 50K increase.

    This guide was made during Call of Duty: Warzone DMZ’s season 4 and will be updated as we play or if there are any changes in the unlocking criteria of the expansions.
