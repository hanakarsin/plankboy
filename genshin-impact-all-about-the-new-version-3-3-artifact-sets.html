    Genshin Impact‘s highly anticipated version 3.3 “All Senses Clear, All Existence Void” will feature both the playable debut of The Wanderer, previously known as Scaramouche of the 11th Farui Harbingers as well as that of two new Artifact sets, Desert Pavilion Chronicle and Flower of Paradise Lost. But what do each of the sets do, and more importantly, how will you be able to get them? Now, in order to answer that and more, here’s all you need to know about the new Genshin Impact version 3.3 Artifact Sets.

    Recommended Videos

    Genshin impact: All About the New Version 3.3 Artifact Sets

    As we mentioned above, the new artifacts are set to debut together with the game’s version 3.3 on December 7, 2022, and are set to be available as part of a new domain. With that said, you can check out a brief introduction to each artifact set as well as an overview of their effects below, based on their official descriptions revealed during the version 3.3 Developer Livestream.

    Desert Pavilion Chronicle Effects

    The Wanderer’s tailor-made set, Desert Pavilion Chronicle will be focused on increasing both its wielder’s Anemo DMG and their overall damage after performing charged attacks.

    • 2-Piece Effect: Anemo DMG Bonus +15%.
    • 4-Piece Effect: Hitting an enemy with a Charged Attack will increase your ATK Speed by 10% as well as your Normal, Charged, and Plunging DMG by 40% for 15 seconds.

    Flower of Paradise Lost Effects

    Already considered by the community as the best artifact set for Nilou, the Flower of Paradise Lost artifact set is focused on massively increasing its user’s Bloom, Hyperbloom, and Burgeon damage.

    • 2-Piece Effect: Elemental Mastery +80.
    • 4-Piece Effect: Increases the damage of Bloom, Hyperbloom, and Burgeon by 40%. Triggering any of the mentioned reactions will further increase the buff by 25%. The secondary increase can be stacked to a maximum of 4 and can be triggered even if the character equipping the set is off the field.

    Genshin Impact is currently available for PC, PlayStation 4, PlayStation 5, and mobile devices.
