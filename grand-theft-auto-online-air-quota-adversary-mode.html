    24 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Rockstar Games continues to provide Grand Theft Auto V with new life via new content released for its multiplayer platform, Grand Theft Auto Online. Today the developer has announced a new update which incorporates a new adversary mode called Air Quota, which pits 16 players in teams of four, to fight in aerial battles aboard of different aircrafts like the P-996 Lazer, or the Thruster. Those to try the new mode from now until January 22 will receive double GTA$ and RP.

    For those who prefer to stay on the ground, there is a new beautiful vehicle to drive around Los Santos named Grotti GT500, which takes inspiration on Italian brands with its slick design and retro vibes. The new vehicle can be obtained exclusively at Legendary Motorsport. There are also some items from Maze Bank Foreclosures that will be offered at a lower price, including a security room and new vehicles:

    • Facility Styles – 25% off
    • Facility Graphics – 25% off
    • Security Room – 25% off
    • Buckingham Pyro (Plane) – 25% off (Buy It Now & Trade Price)
    • HVY Nightshark (Weaponized Vehicle) – 25% off

    Finally, players will get a chance to earn a nice sum of GTA$ and RP in the new “Plummet” Premium Race and “Tongva Valley” Time Trial, which will be available until January 22.

    With all these constant updates, it’s no surprise that the game continues to sell well even years after its original release, even to the extreme to return to the number 1 spot in the UK charts.
