    Baraka initially made his debut back in Mortal Kombat II and has been around here and there throughout the series. After being only available as an opponent in story mode for Mortal Kombat X, Baraka has been brought back in Mortal Kombat 11 and with that come some really cool Fatalities. This guide will detail how you can pull off Baraka’s Fatalities.

    How To Do Baraka’s Fatalities In Mortal Kombat 11

    Mortal Kombat is known for its blood and gore, but there are few characters that look more ready to inflict pain than Baraka. Complete with his blade arms, sharp teeth, and spikes all over his body, he is a force to be reckoned with for sure. The following information about Baraka’s Fatalities will be based on the Xbox One controls, since that is the platform we reviewed.

    Food For Thought is Baraka’s first Fatality, which can be executed from Close distance by pressing Back, Down Back, Y. This one is absolutely brutal form the start, as Baraka reaches out and rips off the face of the opponent before then ripping off the next layer. He then uses his blade to stab their brain and pull it out and start to eat on it.

    Baraka’s second Fatality is known as Rock, Paper, Baraka, which is pulled off by pressing Back, Forward, Back, A from Close range. He starts by stabbing the opponent with both blades in the torso before moving upward cutting through their entire body before crossing the blades and cutting off the head, sending it flying up in the air. Baraka then slices the head in half and spears both halves with his blades.
