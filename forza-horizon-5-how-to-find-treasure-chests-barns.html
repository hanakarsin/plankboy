    In Forza Horizon 5 you often have challenges which include tracking down treasure.  Completing these treasure challenges will reward you with in-game items that can help you along your way with credits to purchase new cars.  Sometimes tracking down these chests can be a bit tricky but with these tips you should have no problem securing the bag and finding the treasure chests in Forza Horizon 5.

    How to Find Treasure Chests Forza Horizon 5

    If you’re looking on the Festival Playlist and trying to uncover the treasure chest challenge you will have an orange circle on the map as a starting point to look.  First things first you will want to head to the orange circle to find the treasure as it is located somewhere within that boundary.

    Once you get to the treasure chest you will need to simply run over it with your car to collect and a screen will pop up to tell you what was inside.

    Once big tip on finding the treasure chest in the challenges is to look at the picture associated with the challenge.  Sometimes it will give you a hint on where to look for the treasure as the circled area can be somewhat large.  As you can see in the image above the white canopy is close by the treasure chest near the foot of the hill in the picture.  It is actually located there in game so using these clues can help you shorten the area you are looking in for the chest.  They can still be hard to see with so many rocks and bushes around, but using these tips should help you find it fast.

    How to Find Barns in Forza Horizon 5

    Barns are another one of the search mechanics in Forza Horizon 5.  As you progress in the game you will find new barns which hide a car within a purple circled area.  Once inside the area you are looking for a barn that houses a car.  These don’t have specific clues to look for, but what you are looking for is a structure of some sort.  These are always houses and there’s almost always nothing else close by.

    Simply keep an eye out for a man made structure and you’re likely on your way to finding the barn.  Once inside the circle and close enough to the barn itself you will get a small cutscene which will showcase what you’ve discovered.

    Using these simple tips in Forza Horizon 5 will allow you to find barns and treasure chests quickly instead of taking a long time to find them.
