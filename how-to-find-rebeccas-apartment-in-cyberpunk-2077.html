    If you’ve enjoyed Netflix’s popular series Cyberpunk Edgerunners, you may feel compelled to explore Night City and discover its hidden gems. One fascinating Easter egg is the opportunity to visit Rebecca’s apartment from the show in Cyberpunk 2077. However, it’s worth noting that another NPC now occupies the apartment due to the game’s timeline after the series’ events. Nevertheless, you can still access the apartment by meeting specific requirements within the game. Here’s a guide on how to find Rebecca’s Apartment in Cyberpunk 2077.

    Recommended Videos

    How to Access Rebecca’s Apartment in Cyberpunk 2077

    You can find Rebecca’s apartment in Cyberpunk 2077 by completing the Scrolls Before Swine gig in Northside Watson. You then must either kill or incapacitate Aaron to keep your access to her apartment. However, if you don’t have access to the Scrolls Before Swine gig yet, you can continue reading to learn more about what is required to gain access to it, the details of the job itself, and what you can do in Rebecca’s apartment. If you want to compare her apartment from the show to in-game here is the clip of her apartment from the show:

    Requirements to Find Rebecca’s Apartment in Cyberpunk 2077

    You must do two important things before the Scrolls Before Swine gig becomes available.

  • You must first reach Act 2 of the game. This can be achieved through natural progression by beating the game’s main story missions.
  • You must then complete 15 jobs for Fixer Regina Jones. Scrolls Before Swine is a Tier 6 gig that becomes available after completing the Last Login and Small Man, Big Evil gigs.

    • After completing the required gigs, you will gain access to the Scrolls Before Swine job located in the Northside subsection of Watson. Eventually, you will meet corrupt NCPD officer Aaron McCarison at his apartment through the normal progression of the quest. This apartment was Rebecca’s apartment during the events of Cyberpunk Edgerunners, which is a prequel to Cyberpunk 2077. Her apartment is on the second floor of an apartment building found next to the Longshore North terminal in Watson. To keep access to the apartment, you must kill or incapacitate Aaron instead of extorting him for more money to keep your access to Rebecca’s apartment so you can explore it.

    What Can You Do in Rebecca’s Apartment?

    First and foremost, enjoy it! This pretty cool easter egg ties together a fantastic game and television show. Both of which were created years apart. However, the only other interactable item in her apartment is Aaron’s computer, which has limited net access like other computers you find in the game.

    Related: How to Find Rebecca’s Shotgun in Cyberpunk 2077

    Rebecca’s apartment isn’t Rebecca’s only Cyberpunk Edgerunners easter egg you can find in Cyberpunk 2077. You can also find Rebecca’s shotgun, which is a potent weapon to help in your quest to rule the streets of Night City.
