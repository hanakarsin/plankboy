    CarX Drift Racing Online Update 1.16 Patch Notes

    Update 3.61 has arrived forCarx Drift Racing Online, and heres the full list of changes and fixes added with this patch. The update brings along with it a ton of brand new content including 6 new cars, 3 new D-Master bodies, 2 ProFlow bodies, and 2 Street X bodies as well.

    18 Feb 2024, 00:00

    Update 3.61 has arrived for Carx Drift Racing Online, and here’s the full list of changes and fixes added with this patch.

    The update brings along with it a ton of brand new content including 6 new cars, 3 new D-Master bodies, 2 ProFlow bodies, and 2 Street X bodies as well.

    The update also includes various fixes including the position of wheels in arches on a few cars, wrong hand position for the custom lever, as well as UI fixes.

    Here is the full list of changes and updates:-

    •  New location: Mondello Park
    •  Added new car Equator D
    •  Added new car Interstate
    •  Added new car Hummel
    •  Added new car Kitsune
    •  Added new car Rattlesnake
    •  Added new car Grace GT
    •  Updated car model Wellington S20
    •  Added a new D-Master body to the Spark ZR vehicle
    •  Added a new D-Master body to the Wanderer L30 vehicle
    •  Added a new D-Master body to the Atlas GT car
    •  Added new ProFlow body to Asura M1
    •  Added new ProFlow body for Wanderer L30
    •  Added new StreetX body to the Spector RS
    •  Added new StreetX body to Wanderer L30
    •  Added new spoiler and rear bumper Missile to Burner JDM car
    •  Added new StreetX spoiler to Burner JDM vehicle
    •  Added new Custom rear bumper to Burner JDM car
    •  Added new headlights and CBW visor to Wellington S20
    •  Lots of tweaks to lighting, vinyl application and fleet models
    •  Fixed position of wheels in arches on some cars
    •  Changed the default values ​​of the Racing modification
    •  Advanced tire smoke simulation. Added the ability to adjust the intensity of smoke
    •  Fixed incorrect motion blur
    •  Added new soundtracks
    •  Added sequential transmission volume setting
    •  Added the ability to select a track for a test drive in the dyno
    •  Fixed wrong hand position for CUSTOM lever
    •  Rotating decal now works with symmetry in vinyl editor
    • ` Added support for the DualSense :tm: controller
    •  Added highlighting of the installed part in the “Body kits” section
    •  Lots of UI fixes
    • Added the ability to create rooms with a specific of tire grip
    •  Added new room filter for tire grip
    •  Changed nickname behavior in multiplayer
    •  Adjusted car spawn zones in multiplayer
    •  Fixed erroneous counting of overtaking by the pursuer in the XDS Doubles competition
    •  Changed approach to loading game resources
    •  Optimized image compression technology for better performance
    •  Added new parameter “Texture Compression” in graphics settings
    • App size reduction
    • Vehicle handling physics improvement
    • Graphics optimization for low-end devices
    • Added stability fixes.
    • Other minor bug fix.

    CarX Drift Racing Online is currently available for purchase on PC, the Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One.
