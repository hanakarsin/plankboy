    Tekken 7 PC Requirements Set

    26 Jan 2024, 00:00

    Tekken 7 is due out at the beginning of June for PlayStation 4, Xbox One and PC.   For those wondering what the PC requirements are going to be for the fighting game, they’ve been revealed via Steam.  Here’s what you’ll need to play Tekken 7 on PC:

    MINIMUM:
    OS: Windows 7/8/10 (64-bit OS required)
    Processor: Intel Core Intel Core i3-4160 @ 3.60GHz or equivalent
    Memory: 6 GB RAM
    Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 660 2GB, GTX 750Ti 2GB, or equivalent
    DirectX: Version 11
    Network: Broadband Internet connection
    Storage: 60 GB available space
    Sound Card: DirectX compatible soundcard or onboard chipset

    RECOMMENDED:
    OS: Windows 7/8/10 (64-bit OS required)
    Processor: Intel Core i5-4690 3.5 GHz or equivalent
    Memory: 8 GB RAM
    Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 1060 equivalent or higher
    DirectX: Version 11
    Network: Broadband Internet connection
    Storage: 60 GB available space
    Sound Card: DirectX compatible soundcard or onboard chipset

    Nvidia also added a little bit of color as to what to expect from the PC version of the game.  Tekken 7 will support 4K resolution and 60FPS, but Nvidia says that you’ll need a GTX 1080 Ti to hit this benchmark.  Even if you don’t have the newest graphics card, you’ll be able to play the game, though as you can see above Bandai is recommending the 1060.

    The GTX 1060 is said to provide 60fps at max settings for 1920×1080 and 2560×1440 resolutions.
