    Throughout your adventures in Bravely Second you will come across many blue treasure chests with great rewards waiting for you inside. However, to the horror of many, these treasure chests are locked and a means for opening them seems to be nowhere in sight. Worry not, dear gamer, as there is most definitely a way to open them and it just requires a little patience.

    Even though you come across these locked blue treasure chests very early on in the game, you won’t actually be able to unlock them until chapter 5. When you find yourself inside of the sanctuary in Gathelatio you will see a shining dot in the hallway that leads to the exit. This is the key that will unlock all of the locked blue treasure chests, gaining you some very helpful items in the process.

    There are a total of 12 locked blue treasure chests throughout the game, and below you will find a list of each one’s location and what they contain:

    • The Middle portion of Harena Sea Cave has a locked blue treasure chest that contains the Air Knife.
    • The Middle portion of Origin Cave has a locked blue treasure chest that contains the Doujigiri-Yasutsna.
    • The 1st Floor portion of Grand Mill Works has a locked blue treasure chest that contains the Fortuna.
    • The KG 2 portion of Mithril Mine has a locked blue treasure chest that contains the Blessed Shield.
    • The Descent portion of Sealed Ba’al Dungeon has a locked blue treasure chest that contains the Hadean Claws.
    • The Middle portion of Miasma Woods has a locked blue treasure chest that contains the Heiji’s Jitte.
    • The KG 28 portion of Grandship Engine Room has a locked blue treasure chest that contains the Kokusenpu.
    • The 2nd Floor portion of Stark Fort has a locked blue treasure chest that contains the Kurro – shippu.
    • The 47th Floor portion of Eternian Central Command has a locked blue treasure chest that contains the Longinus.
    • The Home Affairs portion of Witherwood has a locked blue treasure chest that contains the Musashi’s Oar.
    • The 2nd Floor portion of Everlast Tower has a locked blue treasure chest that contains the Ripper.
    • The KG 2 portion of Harena Ruins has a locked blue treasure chest that contains the Yoichi’s Bow.

    That wraps up the location and contents of every locked blue treasure chest, but be sure to check out our other Bravely Second guides for even more help with the game.
