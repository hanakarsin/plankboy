    How to Enable Cheats on The Sims 4

    Before starting cheats up in The Sims 4, you will first need to enable cheats in the game. You can do so with the following commons on your respective system:

    • PC: Ctrl + Shift + C
    • Mac: Command + Shift + C
    • Console: All four triggers

    Once you have the cheat box open, type in testingcheats true and hit enter to enable cheats for The Sims 4. For a more detailed explanation, visit our guide on enabling cheats in The Sims 4.

    Turn into a Werewolf Cheats

    You can go through the process of turning a Sim into a werewolf, or you can use the cheats below to instantly transform any Sim you wish. If you regret your decision, there’s a cheat to turn them back, too.

    • Make Sim a werewolf: traits.equip_trait trait_occultwerewolf
    • Turn werewolf back to a Sim: traits.remove_trait trait_occultwerewolf

    Again, make sure you are opening the cheat box and typing in these cheats exactly as they are shown.

    Cheating Werewolf Aspirations

    You can complete five werewolf aspirations in the Werewolves expansion pack… or you can just skip them! Once you have enabled cheats, type in aspirations.complete_current_milestone to get through each werewolf aspiration and get your satisfaction points, plus any bonus traits you gain.

    The Sims 4 Werewolves Cheats for Experience Points

    Werewolf XP is earned by doing tasks related to, well, being a werewolf. Skip puppy training and assign yourself as much XP as you like with these cheat codes:

    • stats.set_stat rankedStatistic_Werewolf_Progression #

    The hash will need to be replaced with however many points you want to set your XP at. Here are the following number of points for each stage of werewolf:

    • Runt: 200
    • Prime: 700
    • Veteran: 1600
    • Apex: 3000
