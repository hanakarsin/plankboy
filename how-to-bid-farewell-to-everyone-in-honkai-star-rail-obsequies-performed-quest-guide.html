    How to Bid Farewell to Everyone in Honkai Star Rail | Obsequies Performed Quest Guide

    Honkai Star Rail Version 1.3s new Trailblaze Missions serve as a brief epilogue to the Xianzhou Luofu arc, and before the Astral Express sets off for new horizons, its time to say goodbye to your allies.

    Honkai Star Rail Version 1.3’s new Trailblaze Missions serve as a brief epilogue to the Xianzhou Luofu arc, and before the Astral Express sets off for new horizons, it’s time to say goodbye to your allies.

    Recommended Videos

    One of the final quests in Honkai Star Rail Version 1.3 — Obsequies Performed, A Long Road Ahead — features an optional objective that asks you to bid farewell to the people you got to know in Xianzhou. There are plenty of allies in the Xianzhou Luofu, and this guide will show you where to find everyone from Bailu to Fu Xuan.

    All Bid Farewell Locations in Honkai Star Rail

    You can bid farewell to ten people before leaving the Xianzhou Luofu in Honkai Star Rail. Each of their locations is listed below.

    Most of these characters are just a short walk from a teleport waypoint, so you shouldn’t have difficulty finding them. Make sure to talk to everyone. The hardest of the group to track down are the Mean Kids since they’re tucked away in a corner of the Exalting Sanctum that most people don’t visit, but they’re easy to find once you know where to look.

    Related: Honkai Star Rail Reserved Trailblaze Power Explained

    You don’t get any bonus rewards for speaking to your allies before leaving the Luofu, but you do get some additional dialogue. It’s a bittersweet moment since we’ve spent so much time on the Xianzhou Luofu since Honkai Star Rail launched. New planets are looming on the horizon, though, and there are plenty of new characters to meet.

    Once you’ve wrapped up Version 1.3’s short suite of Trailblaze Missions, you can work farming Stellar Jades to pull for Fu Xuan. If you’re short on Stellar Jades, then the new Swarm Disaster mode of the Simulated Universe has thousands up for grabs. Honkai Star Rail Version 1.3 also has plenty of limited-time events coming up soon, so there’s much to look forward to.
