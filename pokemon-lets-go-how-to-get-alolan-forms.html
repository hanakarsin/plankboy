    Pokemon Lets Go How to Get Alolan Forms

    Alolan Forms are a unique type of Pokmon in Pokmon Lets Go. There are a number of different Alolan Form Pokmon to collect in the game, but the requirements to get them are quite different when you look down the list of them. Some Alolan Form Pokmon are exclusive to each version of the game.

    21 Mar 2024, 00:00

    Alolan Forms are a unique type of Pokémon in Pokémon Let’s Go.  There are a number of different Alolan Form Pokémon to collect in the game, but the requirements to get them are quite different when you look down the list of them.  Some Alolan Form Pokémon are exclusive to each version of the game.  So which Alolan Forms you can get depends on what version of the game you are playing.

    There are a number of Alolan Form Pokémon that can be got in both the Pikachu and Eevee version of the game.  These include Alolan Raichu, Alolan Rattata, Alolan Diglett, Alolan Geodude, Alolan Exeggutor, and Alolan Marowak.  The following Pokémon can only be obtained in Pokémon Let’s Go Pikachu:  Alolan Grimer and Alolan Sandshrew.  The following Pokémon can only be obtained in Pokémon Let’s Go Eevee:  Alolan Meowth and Alolan Vulpix.

    No matter which game you pick you won’t be finding these Pokémon in the wild.  There are certain requirements to find each one.

    How to find Alolan Raichu

    Requirements:  Level 30

    Location: NPC Trader in Saffron City – Can be found in the Pokemon Center at top left.

    Version: Found in Both versions of the game

    Alolan Raichu will be found at the Saffron City NPC you must trade with them at Level 30 or above.

    How to find Alolan Rattata

    Requirements: Level 12

    Location: NPC Trader in Cerulean City – Girl can be found sitting down on the couch at the Pokemon Center

    Version: Found in both versions of the game

    Alolan Rattata will be found at Cerulean City NPC you must trade with them at Level 12 or above.

    How to find Alolan Diglett

    Requirements: Level 25

    Location: NPC Trader in Lavender Town – Character can be found in the Pokemon Center on the left side.

    Version: Found in both versions of the game

    Alolan Diglett will be found at the Lavender Town NPC you must trade with them at Level 25 or above.

    How to find Alolan Geodude

    Requirements: Level 16

    Location: NPC Trader in Vermilion City – can be found in the left side of the Pokemon Center

    Version: Found in both versions of the game

    Alolan Geodude will be found at the Vermilion City NPC and you must trade with them at Level 16 or above.

    How to find Alolan Exeggutor

    Requirements: Level 46

    Location: NPC Trader – Can be found in the Pokemon Center at top left of Indigo Plateau

    Version: Found in both versions of the game

    Alolan Exeggutor will be found at the Indigo Plateau NPC and you must trade with them at Level 46 or above.

    How to find Alolan Marowak

    Requirements: Level 38

    Location: NPC Trader in Fuchsia City can be found on top left of Pokemon Center.

    Version: Found in both versions of the game

    Alolan Marowak will be found at the Fuchsia City NPC and you must trade with them at Level 38 or above.

    How to find Alolan Grimer

    Requirements: Level 44

    Location: NPC Trader on Cinnabar Island at Pokemon Center look on the top right side.

    Version: Pokemon Let’s Go Pikacu

    Alolan Grimer is exclusive to Let’s Go Pikachu and will be found at the Cinnabar Island NPC.  You must trade with them at Level 44 or above.

    How to find Alolan Sandshrew

    Requirements: Level 27

    Location: NPC Trader at Celadon City can be found in the right side of the Pokemon Center.

    Version: Pokemon Let’s Go Pikachu

    Alolan Sandshrew is exclusive to Let’s Go Pikachu and will be found at the Celadon City NPC.  You must trade with them at Level 27 or above.

    How to find Alolan Meowth

    Requirements: Level 44

    Location: NPC Trader at Cinnabar Island top right of the Pokemon Center.

    Version: Pokemon Let’s Go Eevee

    Alolan Meowth is exclusive to Let’s Go Eevee and will be found at Cinnabar Island.  You must trade with the NPC there after Level 44

    How to find Alolan Vulpix

    Requirements: Level 27

    Location: NPC Trader at Celedon City can be found on the right side of the Pokemon Center.

    Version: Pokemon Let’s Go Eevee

    Alolan Vulpix is exclusive to Let’s Go Eevee and will be found at Celadon City.  You must trade with the NPC there after level 27.
