    Call Of Duty: Black Ops 3 Guide Zombies Weapon Unlocks

    The recently released Call of Duty: Black Ops 3 is one of the most content filled Call of Duty games in the series and that means gamers have plenty to experience. One of those is the return of the Zombies mode in the game, which has a lot of customization involved.

    10 May 2024, 00:00

    The recently released Call of Duty: Black Ops 3 is one of the most content filled Call of Duty games in the series and that means gamers have plenty to experience. One of those is the return of the Zombies mode in the game, which has a lot of customization involved.

    One of the best features in Call of Duty: Black Ops 3’s Zombies mode is that it has its own separate XP progression system involved, which has having different levels from the regular multiplayer. This makes the Zombies mode actually feel like its own thing even more than in the past, with progression working to get you to new areas over time.

    As a result, you must keep playing Zombies again and again to better your arsenal, with plenty of weapons to unlock that can show up on the map as you progress further in Zombies.

    These can be found in the Call of Duty: Black Ops 3 menu when looking more in depth, but we thought we’d put them all together for you here to make it a little easier to keep up with as well.

    These are the varying gun types and the levels at which you will unlock the different types of weapons in the Zombies mode for Call of Duty: Black Ops 3. Make sure that you have reached these levels on the Zombie XP system exclusively, as the multiplayer one has nothing to do with that.

    Submachine Guns

    Vesper – Level 4

    VMP – Level 8

    Kuda – Level 16

    Pharo – Level 23

    Weevil – Level 29

    Light Machine Guns

    BRM – Level 12

    Dingo – Level 19

    48 Dredge – Level 27

    Gorgon – Level 31

    Assault Rifles

    ICR-1 – Level 2

    KN-44 – Level 7

    M8A7 – Level 17

    Sheiva – Level 20

    HVK-30 – Level 25

    Man-O-War – Level 31

    Sniper Rifles

    Locus – Level 11

    Drakon – Level 14

    SVG-100 – Level 24

    Shotguns

    KRM-262 – Level 5

    Argus – Level 13

    205 Brecci – Level 18

    Haymaker 12 – Level 33

    Secondaries

    Bowie Knife – Unlocked from start

    RK5 – Level 9

    L-CAR 9 – Level 22

    XM-53 – Level 28
