    What is One Piece’s Manga Release Schedule?

    One Piece is released weekly, like Mashle: Magic and Muscle and most of the Shonen Jump manga, but Oda also often takes breaks after publishing three chapters, making the schedule a bit different. That allows Oda to take a breather and think more about the story while also letting us reflect on everything that happened in the story (and, of course, craft more theories).
