    The Call of Duty patch addressed one of the community’s biggest complaints by temporarily disabling Nova’s Gaia skin (and its BlackCell variant), whose see-through body allowed players to almost perfectly blend with the environment and gain what many considered to be an unfair advantage on confrontations.

    But will the Gaia skin (also known in the community as the Groot skin) ever become available again? And if so, when? Here’s when the Gaia (Groot) Skin will return to MW3 and Warzone.

    Will the Gaia (Groot) Skin be Returning to MW3 and Warzone?

    Unfortunately for all looking to deploy while wearing one of the coolest — but most frustrating — skins in both Call of Duty MW3 and Warzone, Sledgehammer Games has not revealed when the Gaia skin will once again become available.

    According to the patch notes for the November 30 patch — which you can check out by heading to its page on the official Call of Duty site — the skin will only return after the developers are satisfied with the adjustments made to its in-game visibility.

    Related: How to Get the Juggernaut Killstreak in Modern Warfare 3 Zombies

    Although the skin is currently unavailable, it does not mean it was removed from the game’s The Haunting Battle Pass. If you haven’t unlocked the skin already, you can have it as part of your collection once it’s available by purchasing the Battle Pass and then fully unlocking Sector F11. The Haunting Battle Pass grid will be available until December 6, 2023.

    If you are used to using Gaia and are looking for a new Operator, Jet is my absolute favorite, as well as my main. You can check out how to unlock her in our How to Unlock Jet in Modern Warfare 3 guide.
