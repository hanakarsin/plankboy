    Trying to get that Platinum trophy? We’re here to help! Below, you’ll find a complete list of all Helldivers 2 trophies and instructions on how to earn them.

    Recommended Videos

    Helldivers 2 has a total of 39 trophies, made up of one Platinum, three Gold, 11 Silver, and 24 Bronze. From a quick look at the trophy list, and having not got hands-on with the game just yet, this doesn’t feel like the most difficult Platinum trophy that’ll be up for grabs in 2024’s batch of new games. The trickiest, of course, appear to be the three Gold trophies that require you to not only complete missions on the higher difficulty settings, but meet other requirements as well such as extracting within six minutes or not having a single teammate die. Sounds like you’ll want to get a good band of friends together to cross these ones off the list. You can check them all out for yourself below.
