    Are you looking for all Master Override locations in Dead Space Remake so you can find all the sweet loot to make Isaac more powerful? Not so fast, though. Unfortunately, you will have to find the RIGs of different officers from the Ishimura and create the Master Override before accessing each Master Override Location to obtain valuable loot like weapon upgrades and Power Nodes. This may seem simple, but just like finding all weapons or schematics, you must locate them in multiple chapters and backtrack through the Ishimura. Fear not, though, because here are all Master Override locations in Dead Space Remake to make finding them as easy as possible for you.

    Recommended Videos

    All Master Override Locations in Dead Space Remake

    As mentioned above, you will need to find all seven RIGs and create the Master Override before accessing the areas requiring this clearance. If you already have the Master Override, you can proceed with our list below. Otherwise, see our guide on how and where to find all crew RIG Locations so you can complete the You Are Not Authorized side quest and create the Master Override. The earliest you can create the Master Override is after Chapter 9, while the latest you can do it is when Chapter 11 ends, so make sure to get these rewards before completing the game.

    Here are all eight Master Override locations in Dead Space Remake:

    • Water Purification Storage (Bridge): Pulse Rifle Upgrade
    • Transfer Junction (Aegis VII): Nodes x3
    • Deluxe Quarters (Crew Quarters): Flamethrower Upgrade
    • Executive Quarters (V. Holt): Gold Semiconductor
    • Machine Shop (Engineering): Ripper Upgrade
    • Cargo Tram Control (Hangar): Force Gun Upgrade
    • Mining Tram Station (Hydroponics): Line Run Upgrade
    • Tool Storage (Mining): Contact Beam Upgrade

    Once you have located all of these upgrades, you will be closer to getting the Made to Order trophy and beating the game so you can play with your newly upgraded weapons on New Game Plus.

    Dead Space Remake is available on PlayStation 5, Xbox Series X|S, and PC.
