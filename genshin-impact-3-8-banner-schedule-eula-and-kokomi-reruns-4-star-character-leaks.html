    As we approach the debut of the second wave of banners part of Genshin Impact’s version 3.7, more and more information regarding the game’s still unnamed version 3.8 continues to surface. Thus revealing more about what seems to be the last patch before the long-awaited debut of the region of Fontaine, as well as the first update to feature a Eula rerun since version 2.3. Now, here’s everything we know about the Genshin Impact version 3.8 banners, including all its leaked characters, release dates, and more.

    Recommended Videos

    Genshin Impact Version 3.8 Banner Leaks: All Leaked 4 and 5-star Characters

    According to the Save Your Primos team, known for the veracity of their leaks, it is highly likely that Genshin Impact‘s version 3.8 will feature Eula, Sangonomiya Kokomi, and Klee as the stars of three out of its four Character Featured Banners. Klee will also be receiving a previously leaked witch-themed skin.

    Although which 5-star characters are set to star on each banner phase is still a mystery, known leaker リリィrevealed that according to Uncle SB, Mika, Rosaria, and Razor will be the 4-stars featured in the first wave of banners, while Thoma, Yanfei, and Faruzan will be staring in the second one.

    If the leaks prove to be true, Eula will receive her first banner in almost two years and also star on a banner featuring two of the game’s top 5 supports for her and Razor (Mika and Rosaria).

    Related: Genshin Impact Banner History: The Complete List (June 2023)

    Genshin Impact 3.8 Banner Schedule: When Will Each Banner Debut?

    Taking into account that as revealed by many known sources in the community, Genshin Impact’s still unnamed version 3.8 is set to be released on July 5, 2023, it is safe to say that the first wave of banners featuring Mika, Rosaria, and Razor as its 4-stars will debut together with it. The second wave of banners, however, featuring Thoma, Yanfei, and Faruzan should be released around July 25-27, given the duration of the game’s standard banner cycles.

    To recap, here are all the characters set to star on the Genshin Impact version 3.8 banners, as well as the possible release date of each phase:

    • First Banner Phase (July 5, 2023)
      • 5-stars: Two among Eula/Kokomi/Klee/??
      • 4-stars: Mika, Rosaria, and Razor
    • Second Banner Phase (July 25-27, 2023)
      • 5-stars: Two among Eula/Kokomi/Klee/??
      • 4-stars: Thoma, Yanfei, and Faruzan

    According to Genshin Update, a live stream focused on Genshin Impact’s version 3.8 will take place around either June 23 or 24, 2023.
