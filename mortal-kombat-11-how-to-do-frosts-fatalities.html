    Frost was included as the only unlockable character in Mortal Kombat 11, though she does factor into the story and shows up in the character selection menu from the start. Frost was once Sub-Zero’s apprentice, but now has turned on him during the events of the game. As his protege, Frost is full of ice based attacks and her Fatalities reflect that as well. This guide will explain how you can pull off Frost’s two Fatalities.

    How To Do Frost’s Fatalities In Mortal Kombat 11

    Frost is a little different than we remember her in Mortal Kombat 11, having now received cybernetic enhancements due to the Lin Kuei’s Cyber Initiative that also turned Cyrax and Sektor in the past. For the purposes of this guide, we will be discussing controls based on the Xbox One version of the game.

    Frost’s first Fatality is known as Ice Sculpture and is performed from Close range by pressing Forward, Back, Down, X. She starts by making a ice spiral on her right hand and stabs right into the head before turning her left hand into an ice spiral as well and stabbing the lower torso. Ripping some guts out along the way, Frost then grabs an ice grenade from her chest and sticks it into the body before it explodes, freezing the upper half as it explodes.

    Her second Fatality is known as The Cyber Initiative and is done by pressing Back, Forward, Down, Forward, B from Mid distance. Frost starts with a giant ice beam from her chest being shot right at the enemy, freezing their whole body. She then punches the chest, leaving nothing of the upper body besides the spine and brain, the latter of which is grabbed and taken to be put into a new cybernetic body.
