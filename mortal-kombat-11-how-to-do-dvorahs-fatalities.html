    D’vorah was one of the newcomers in Mortal Kombat X that quickly made her presence known with the killing of Mileena in the story mode. The character’s love for bugs plays into her moveset and that is also the case with her Fatalities. In this guide, we will explain how to execute D’vorah’s deadly Fatalities.

    How To Do D’vorah’s Fatalities in Mortal Kombat 11

    There are definitely some wild Fatalities in Mortal Kombat 11 and D’vorah’s are certainly no different. This guide will be based on the Xbox One controls, so adjust accordingly based on your platform.

    The first Fatality for D’vorah is known as New Species and can be performed by pressing Back, Forward, Back, A from Close distance. D’vorah starts by stabbing the opponent in the shoulder before spewing acid right into their mouth. This causes the body of the opponent to convulse before it actually forms a giant bug from their torso.

    D’vorah’s second Fatality is known as Can’t Die and is done from Mid distance by pressing Back, Down, Down, B. This one starts with D’vorah flying up and shooting some sort of sticky stuff at the enemy, knocking them back onto the ground. She then hovers over them and stabs the opponent and rips the front half of their head and torso off.
