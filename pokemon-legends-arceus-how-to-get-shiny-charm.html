    Pokémon Legends Arceus gives players the chance to catch shiny variants of many of the Pokémon available in the game, but finding the variant versions is, in true Pokémon fashion, an extremely rare occurrence. With that said, the game offers, for those looking to get the shiny versions of all their favorite Pokémon, an item capable of increasing the rate at which the encounters happen, but getting it is not an easy feat. Now, to help you get both the item and your favorite Shiny Pokémon as fast as possible here’s how to get the Shiny Charm in Pokémon Legends Arceus.

    How to Get the Shiny Charm in Pokémon Legends Arceus

    You can get the Shiny Charm in Pokémon Legends Arceus by visiting Professor Laventon in the Galaxy Hall, but only after completing the Hisui Pokédex and reaching research level 10 on all of its 242 entries. Once you do that, he will give you the Shiny Charm during a celebratory cutscene. With that said, you can raise your research level fast by completing Research Tasks, such as battling with the said Pokémon on the field, catching them without being noticed, leveling them up, evolving them, defeating them, as well as using specific moves against them. It’s also worth pointing out that completing the tasks marked with two white arrows will reward you with more points, so you might want to prioritize them during your quest to reach research level 10 with all the Pokémon available in the game.

    Now that you know how to get the Shiny Charm in Pokémon LA, don’t forget to check out how to catch Cresselia, Regigigas, Giratina, Arceus, Chimchar, Piplup, and Turtwig in Pokémon Legends Arceus.
