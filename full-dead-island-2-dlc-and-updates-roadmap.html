    Dead Island 2 Full DLC and Update Roadmap

    Apart from the currently available Premium Character Packs —each featuring a new weapon and look for a set character— and the ‘Til Dawn’ Collection, Dambuster Studios revealed that Dead Island 2 is set to receive a series of ‘From Dusk’ Collection skins and two story expansions, Haus and SOLA Festival, throughout both 2023 and 2024.

    The skins part of the ‘From Dusk’ Collection are set to arrive throughout 2023, while the Haus and SOLA Festival story expansions are set to be released on Q4 2023 and Q2 2024 respectivelly.

    What Will be Included in the Future DLCs?

    As we wait for an official release date for the story expansions, the roadmap’s official announcement post revealed the first details about each of them.

    On it, it was revealed that Haus will be centered around a millionaire’s ”techno-death cult”. The SOLA Festival, on the other hand, promises to bring players to the center of a Woodstock-type festival filled with well… we can say for sure that it will have blood.

    According to the description of the game’s Expansion Pass on the Xbox Game Store, the expansions will bring new foes, weapons, abilities, and areas of Hell-A for players to explore. So if you are looking to expand your inventory and add abilities to your build, purchase the expansion pack so you are ready for when it officially releases.

    As an extra, it has been confirmed that Saints Row provides a crossover cosmetic pack with Dead Island 2 — although it will only feature in the Saints Row game. It was released on April 21, 2023, so if you want some more Dead Island 2, go ahead and boot up Saints Row and get those cosmetics downloaded immediately!
