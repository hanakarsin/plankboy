    While Resident Evil 2 is all about locked doors and keeping you funneled into specific areas of the map, sometimes you look around and wonder how you’ll ever get to specific spots in RPD. Two of these are right next to each other, and present different challenges for either Claire or Leon. Here’s how to get to the Interrogation and Observation Rooms in Resident Evil 2 Remake.

    First off, to get to either room as both Claire and Leon, you need to progress decently far into the story. Once you arrive back at RPD from the Parking Garage, you will be able to get into these rooms, if you have the right key. For Leon you need the Club Key, and for Claire you need the Heart Key. Click on those links to find out how to get them, if you haven’t already.

    Once you are past the garage and have the key you can get into one of these two rooms. With Leon, you can enter the Observation Room with the Club Key, and with Claire you can go into the Interrogation Room with the Heart Key. Once inside, grab everything you can.

    As Claire, you can only explore the Interrogation Room at first, as you try to exit a Licker will crash through the window, so be ready. As tough as he is, he gives you access to the Observation Room, so once you are safe just head to the glass to hop over.Leon already comes in after the glass is shattered, so just hop over when you have every item from the first room.

    And that’s how to get to the Interrogation and Observation Rooms in Resident Evil 2 Remake. Once inside, the most important thing you’ll find is the bejeweled box. See what to do with it right here.
