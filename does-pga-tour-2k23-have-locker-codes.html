    PGA Tour 2K23 is the go-to golf game for anyone looking to tee off from the comfort of their own home. Standing among other grand 2K sports titles, players have been wondering if PGA Tour 2K23 includes the locker codes feature that’s become a 2K staple in recent years. Keep reading to learn everything you need to know about locker codes in PGA Tour 2K23.

    Do Locker Codes Exist in PGA Tour 2K23?

    Locker codes are in PGA Tour 2K23. However, working codes are very rarely released, and there are often no active codes available to redeem. Unlike NBA2K, which regularly receives new codes, PGA Tour 2K23’s stream of codes dried up shortly after launch.

    How to Find PGA Tour 2K23 Locker Codes

    If any future codes are released, you can usually find them through the official PGA Tour 2K X (formerly Twitter) account. Codes have also been known to pop up on the official PGA Tour 2K Discord and given away during 2K livestreams on Twitch.

    Related: How To Import Legacy Courses Into PGA Tour 2K23

    If you’re hopeful more locker codes will be released, we recommend turning notifications on for PGA Tour 2K’s X account. If you can handle that many notifications about golf, that is. You should also subscribe to the PGA Tour 2K subreddit, where other players can post locker codes they’ve found in the wind.

    Locker codes in PGA Tour 2K23 have been known to reward either gear or VC (Virtual Currency). VC can be spent on customizing your PGA Tour MyPLAYER with branded equipment and outfits, club fittings, golf balls, and golf ball skins.
