    Demon Slayer -Kimetsu no Yaiba- The Hinokami Chronicles offers players the chance to battle as their favorite set of characters, and like with all fighting games, they can wear different costumes, or battle attires, when in battle. With that said, we will now tell you how to unlock all costumes currently available in the game.

    How to Unlock all Costumes on Demon Slayer -Kimetsu no Yaiba- The Hinokami Chronicles

    In Demon Slayer -Kimetsu no Yaiba- The Hinokami Chronicles players can unlock a total of 32 costumes/battle attires. Here are all the costumes current available in the game and how to unlock them:

    Tanjiro Kamado 

    • Demon Slayer Uniform: Default.
    • No Haori: Rank A on the Third Battle of ”Encroaching Assassins”.
    • Final Selection: Complete the Reward Mission: Final Selection Challengers.
    • Charcoal Seller: Obtain Rank A on the First Battle of ”Enmu”.
    • Training: Complete the Prologue.
    • Urokodaki’s House: Complete the Reward Mission: Final Selection Survivors.
    • Butterfly Mansion Patient Wear: Digital Deluxe Edition Exclusive

    Nezuko Kamado 

    • Kimono: Default

    Zenitsu Agatsuma

    • Demon Slayer Uniform: Default
    • Final Selection: Achieve Rank A on the Special Mission ”The Settlement Intruder” (Normal).
    • Butterfly Mansion Patient Wear: Digital Deluxe Edition Exclusive

    Inosuke Hashibira

    • Demon Slayer Uniform: Default
    • No Boar Head: Achieve Rank A on the Special Mission ”The Settlement Intruder” (Hard).
    • Butterfly Mansion Patient Wear: Digital Deluxe Edition Exclusive

    Sakonji Urokodaki

    • Present: Default
    • Past: Achieve Rank A on ”What Lurks in the Mountains”.

    Sabito

    • Warding Mask: Default
    • No Mask: Complete the Prologue

    Makomo

    • Warding Mask: Default

    Giyu Tomioka

    • Demon Slayer Uniform: Default
    • No Haori: Achieve Rank A or higher on Chapter 6.

    Shinobu Kocho

    • Demon Slayer Uniform: Default
    • No Haori: Obtain Rank A on the Gourd Breaker Mini-game (Normal).

    Kyojuro Rengoku

    • Demon Slayer Uniform: Default
    • No Haori: Obtain Rank C or higher on Chapter 8.

    Murata

    • Demon Slayer Uniform: Default

    Tanjiro Kamado (Kimetsu Academy)

    • Kimetsu Academy Uniform: Default

    Nezuko Kamado (Kimetsu Academy)

    • Kimetsu Academy Uniform: Default

    Zenitsu Agatsuma (Kimetsu Academy)

    • Kimetsu Academy Uniform: Default

    Inosuke Hashibira (Kimetsu Academy)

    • Kimetsu Academy Uniform: Default

    Shinobu Kocho (Kimetsu Academy)

    • Kimetsu Academy Uniform: Default

    Giyu Tomioka (Kimetsu Academy)

    • Kimetsu Academy Faculty: Default
