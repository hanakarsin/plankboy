    22 Apr 2024, 00:00

    The Battle Pass for Apex Legends Season 13: Saviors has been revealed with a new trailer showing off all the Legendary skins and rewards available to players to earn throughout the new season. The latest Apex Legends season comes with a ton of changes and improvements for the game, including a brand-new Legend named Newcastle who has a huge emphasis on team support. The Storm Point map also receives huge changes this season such as a new Downed Beast POI and the introduction of IMC Armories swarming with AI enemies. Here’s what to expect from this season’s Battle Pass.

    Apex Legends Season 13 Battle Pass Overview

    The new Season 13 Battle Pass includes Legendary skins for characters like Fuse and Bloodhound along with one for the CAR submachine gun. Pathfinder, Mad Maggie, and Newcastle receive new Epic skins with this Battle Pass, and the Spitfire gets a new reactive skin as well. Of course, this Battle Pass also includes new skydiving emotes too.

    Those are all from the paid premium version of the Battle Pass through. If you’re a free-to-play player, then you can earn these free rewards:

    • Crypto Character Skin
    • 7 Apex Packs
    • 12 Weapon Skins
    • 3 Loading Screens
    • Trackers for All Legends
    • 2 Music Packs
    • 300 Apex Coins
    • Season Badge

    The free version of the Apex Legends Battle Pass is available to all players, but the paid version will cost 950 Apex Coins, which will set you back $10. You can purchase the Premium Battle Pass Bundle for 2800 Apex Coins which will allow you to instantly skip 25 levels and earn the first quarter of this season’s rewards.

    You can see a full rundown of every item in the Season 13 Battle Pass on the official Apex Legends website.
