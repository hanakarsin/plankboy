    How to Get Lifmunk Effigies to Enhance Player Stats in Palworld

    Are you looking for a quick boost in stats for your character in Palworld? Well, you can achieve this through Lifmunk Effigies, a rare item that will increase your characters catch rate, increasing your success rate for catching rarer Pals.

    16 Feb 2024, 00:00

    Are you looking for a quick boost in stats for your character in Palworld? Well, you can achieve this through Lifmunk Effigies, a rare item that will increase your character’s catch rate, increasing your success rate for catching rarer Pals.

    Recommended Videos

    In this guide, we have everything you need to know about Lifmunk Effigies in Palworld, including how to find them, what they look like, and how to use them!

    Where to Find Lifmunk Effigies to Increase Player Stats in Palworld

    Palworld Lifmunk Effigies are small structures resembling a pedestal with a green glow on top of them. Players can get Lifmunk Effigies from various locations on the Palworld map. From personal experience, Lifmunk Effigies reside near castles, forests, bodies of water (ponds/rivers) and stone structures.

    In other words, getting Lifmunk Effigies in Palworld boils down to exploring everywhere and looking out for the green glow they have on top of them. The good news is that the green glow on top of Lifmunk Effigies is very bright and easy to spot from a distance.

    All Lifmunk Effigies Locations in Palworld

    Below you will find all Lifmunk Effigies in Palworld, each section separated by region. Using this, you’ll increase your catch stat quickly so you are able to collect even the toughest Pals found in the game world.

    All Lifmunk Effigies in the Desert Region

    The desert region has its fair share of Lifmunk Effigies, but less compared to the forest areas of the game.

    All Lifmunk Effigies in the Volcano Region

    In the volcano region of Palworld, you will find the majority of Lifmunk Effigies near each other in the southern area.

    All Lifmunk Effigies in Marsh Island Central Region

    The central section of the map has the majority of Lifmunk Effigies of the game. It makes sense, considering it is also the largest and most open area available.

    All Lifmunk Effigies in Verdant Brook Region

    Finding Lifmunk Effigies in Verdant Brook can be challenging due to how hilly it is, but hopefully this map makes it more manageable.

    All Lifmunk Effigies in Windswept Hills Region

    Windswept Hills are where the Plateau of Beginnings is located and where the game begins, so you will likely find these early on if you search thoroughly enough.

    All Lifmunk Effigies in the Snow Tundra Region

    The Snow region of Palworld has the smallest amount of Lifmunk Effigies, with zero to the north.

    Once you find a Lifmunk Effigie, go up to it and interact with the green glow to officially claim it. You have now officially collected a Lifmunk Effigie in Palworld.

    How to Use Lifmunk Effigies in Palworld

    Using a Lifmunk Effigie in Palworld is straightforward; all you need to do is build a Statue of Power from the Level 6 Technology tree. Building a Statue of Power will cost you 20x Stone and 10x Paldium Fragments.

    Once you build a Statue of Power, interact with it to use your Lifmunk Effigie. This will instantly boost your catch stat, increasing your chances of catching tougher Pals and even allowing you to catch Pals without doing a ton of damage to them first.
