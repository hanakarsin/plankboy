    Overwatch 2 has a range of heroes for you to choose from and your crosshair choice can be just as important for heroes such as Reaper. Of course, making the decision of a crosshair can take some time if you don’t know exactly what you are looking for in terms of how the crosshair could affect gameplay style. This article will take you through what the best Reaper crosshair in Overwatch 2 is.

    Recommended Videos

    Overwatch 2: What is the Best Crosshair for Reaper?

    The best crosshair for Reaper is the circle, as he wields shotguns that have a poor spread over long distances but are more than sufficient for lining up shots at close range. His crosshair preferences are identical to Roadhog, although even more specifically focused on close range due to a lack of options for when the enemy is far away aside from just warping in behind them.

    Related: Who is Ramattra in Overwatch 2? – Answered

    His Ultimate attack naturally forgoes any need for a reticle, but when you’ve got only 8 devastating shots to work with before a reload is needed, you want them to be as accurate as possible, and any contact will do a good chunk of damage with Reaper.

    How Do You Change Reaper’s Crosshairs in Overwatch 2?

    Much like for other characters needing this tweak, you can change reticles through the menu even mid-session, whether on console or PC. You can select the ‘Options’ menu from there, ‘Controls,’ then select from the Reticles drop-down menu. You may also choose to change the color of your reticle which can be a great advantage to your overall gameplay too.

    For additional tweaks such as to scale with the game’s resolution, you can select the advanced options. Now that you know what the best Reaper Crosshair is, you can take your pick, and get ready to slip behind enemies and raise some hell — in style.
